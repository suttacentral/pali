3.5.2 Pabhāsutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Ābhāvagga
Pabhāsutta
142.
884“Catasso imā, bhikkhave, pabhā. Katamā catasso? Candappabhā, sūriyappabhā, aggippabhā, paññāpabhā—  imā kho, bhikkhave, catasso pabhā. Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, imāsaṃ catunnaṃ pabhānaṃ yadidaṃ paññāpabhā”ti.
885Dutiyaṃ.
