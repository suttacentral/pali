149.
541Abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paccayā abyākato dhammo uppajjati naāhārapaccayā…  naindriyapaccayā…  najhānapaccayā…pe…  cakkhāyatanaṃ paccayā cakkhuviññāṇaṃ…pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ paccayā kāyaviññāṇaṃ. (Na jhāne idaṃ nānākaraṇaṃ.) Namaggapaccayā…  cakkhāyatanaṃ paccayā cakkhuviññāṇaṃ…pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ paccayā kāyaviññāṇaṃ. Vatthuṃ paccayā ahetukavipākābyākatā kiriyābyākatā khandhā. (Namagge idaṃ nānākaraṇaṃ.)
542(Avasesaṃ yathā paṭiccavāre paccanīyaṃ, evaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)
