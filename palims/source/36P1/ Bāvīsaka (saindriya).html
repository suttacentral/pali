Paccayapaccanīyānuloma
Nahetuduka
Bāvīsaka (saindriya)
1707Nahetupaccayā naārammaṇapaccayā (mūlakaṃ saṃkhittaṃ) naindriyapaccayā najhānapaccayā namaggapaccayā nasampayuttapaccayā navippayuttapaccayā nonatthipaccayā novigatapaccayā āhāre ekaṃ, atthiyā ekaṃ, avigate ekaṃ.
1708Nahetumūlakaṃ.
