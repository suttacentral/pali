10.7.7.3 Paccayānulomapaccanīya
Mahantaraduka
Cittasahabhūduka
Pañhāvāra
Paccayānulomapaccanīya
275.
2998Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi, naadhipatiyā tīṇi, naanantare tīṇi, nasamanantare tīṇi, naaññamaññe tīṇi, naupanissaye tīṇi (sabbattha tīṇi), nasampayutte tīṇi, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā tīṇi, novigate tīṇi.
