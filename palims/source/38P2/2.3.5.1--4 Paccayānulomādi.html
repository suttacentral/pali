610Anidassanaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho anidassano dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  anidassanaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… .
611Anidassanaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho anidassano dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā. (Evaṃ sabbaṃ sappaccayagaṇanāhi saddhiṃ kātabbaṃ.)
