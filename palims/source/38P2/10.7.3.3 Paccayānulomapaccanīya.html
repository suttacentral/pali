10.7.3.3 Paccayānulomapaccanīya
Mahantaraduka
Cittasahabhūduka
Paccayavāra
Paccayānulomapaccanīya
253.
2941Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe nava (sabbattha nava), nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, nasampayutte nava, navippayutte pañca, nonatthiyā nava, novigate nava.
