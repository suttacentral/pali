12.1--7 Paṭiccādivāra
Lokiyaduka
Paṭiccādivāra
26.
66Nalokiyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nalokiyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nalokiyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nalokuttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nalokiyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nalokiyo ca nalokuttaro ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)
67Nalokuttaraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nalokuttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā.
68Nalokiyañca nalokuttarañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nalokuttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (2) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
69Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve…pe…  avigate pañca. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)
