Cittasamuṭṭhānaduka
58.
184Nacittasamuṭṭhānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nacittasamuṭṭhāno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
185Hetuyā nava…pe…  adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  purejāte āsevane pañca…pe…  avigate nava.
