51 Saṃkiliṭṭhaduka
Saṃkiliṭṭhaduka
70.
218Nasaṃkiliṭṭhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaṃkiliṭṭho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)
219Naasaṃkiliṭṭhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naasaṃkiliṭṭho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naasaṃkiliṭṭhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaṃkiliṭṭho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naasaṃkiliṭṭhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaṃkiliṭṭho ca naasaṃkiliṭṭho ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)
220Nasaṃkiliṭṭhañca naasaṃkiliṭṭhañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaṃkiliṭṭho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
221Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate pañca. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)
