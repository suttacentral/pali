1.1.3 Kaṅkhārevatattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Kaṅkhārevatattheragāthā
3.
9
“Paññaṃ imaṃ passa tathāgatānaṃ,
Aggi yathā pajjalito nisīthe;
Ālokadā cakkhudadā bhavanti,
Ye āgatānaṃ vinayanti kaṅkhan”ti.
10Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kaṅkhārevato thero gāthaṃ abhāsitthāti.
