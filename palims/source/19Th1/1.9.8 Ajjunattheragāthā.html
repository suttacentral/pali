1.9.8 Ajjunattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Navamavagga
Ajjunattheragāthā
88.
197
“Asakkhiṃ vata attānaṃ,
uddhātuṃ udakā thalaṃ;
Vuyhamāno mahogheva,
saccāni paṭivijjhahan”ti.
198…  Ajjuno thero… .
