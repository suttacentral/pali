2.1.3 Valliyattheragāthā
Dukanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Valliyattheragāthā
125.
280
“Makkaṭo pañcadvārāyaṃ,
kuṭikāyaṃ pasakkiya;
Dvārena anupariyeti,
ghaṭṭayanto muhuṃ muhuṃ.
126.
281
Tiṭṭha makkaṭa mā dhāvi,
na hi te taṃ yathā pure;
Niggahītosi paññāya,
neva dūraṃ gamissatī”ti.
282…  Valliyo thero… .
