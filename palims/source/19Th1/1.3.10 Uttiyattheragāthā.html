1.3.10 Uttiyattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Tatiyavagga
Uttiyattheragāthā
30.
68
“Ābādhe me samuppanne,
sati me udapajjatha;
Ābādho me samuppanno,
kālo me nappamajjitun”ti.
69…  Uttiyo thero… .
Vaggo tatiyo.
70Tassuddānaṃ
71
Nigrodho cittako thero,
gosālathero sugandho;
Nandiyo abhayo thero,
thero lomasakaṅgiyo;
Jambugāmikaputto ca,
hārito uttiyo isīti.
