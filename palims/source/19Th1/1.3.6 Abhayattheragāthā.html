1.3.6 Abhayattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Tatiyavagga
Abhayattheragāthā
26.
60
“Sutvā subhāsitaṃ vācaṃ,
buddhassādiccabandhuno;
Paccabyadhiṃ hi nipuṇaṃ,
vālaggaṃ usunā yathā”ti.
61…  Abhayo thero… .
