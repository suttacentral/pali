1.10.2 Vijayattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Dasamavagga
Vijayattheragāthā
92.
207
“Yassāsavā parikkhīṇā,
āhāre ca anissito;
Suññato animitto ca,
vimokkho yassa gocaro;
Ākāseva sakuntānaṃ,
padaṃ tassa durannayan”ti.
