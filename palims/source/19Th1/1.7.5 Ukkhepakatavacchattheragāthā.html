1.7.5 Ukkhepakatavacchattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Sattamavagga
Ukkhepakatavacchattheragāthā
65.
147
“Ukkhepakatavacchassa,
Saṅkalitaṃ bahūhi vassehi;
Taṃ bhāsati gahaṭṭhānaṃ,
Sunisinno uḷārapāmojjo”ti.
