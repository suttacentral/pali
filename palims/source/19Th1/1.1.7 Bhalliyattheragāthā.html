1.1.7 Bhalliyattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Bhalliyattheragāthā
7.
17
“Yopānudī maccurājassa senaṃ,
Naḷasetuṃva sudubbalaṃ mahogho;
Vijitāvī apetabheravo hi,
Danto so parinibbuto ṭhitatto”ti.
18Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā bhalliyo thero gāthaṃ abhāsitthāti.
