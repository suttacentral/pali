1.7.7 Ekadhammasavanīyattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Sattamavagga
Ekadhammasavanīyattheragāthā
67.
151
“Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Vikkhīṇo jātisaṃsāro,
natthi dāni punabbhavo”ti.
152…  Ekadhammasavanīyo thero… .
