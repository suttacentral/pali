2.1.9 Gotamattheragāthā
Dukanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Gotamattheragāthā
137.
298
“Sukhaṃ supanti munayo,
ye itthīsu na bajjhare;
Sadā ve rakkhitabbāsu,
yāsu saccaṃ sudullabhaṃ.
138.
299
Vadhaṃ carimha te kāma,
anaṇā dāni te mayaṃ;
Gacchāma dāni nibbānaṃ,
yattha gantvā na socatī”ti.
300…  Gotamo thero… .
