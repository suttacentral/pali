1.6.6 Kuṭivihārittheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Chaṭṭhavagga
Kuṭivihārittheragāthā
56.
127
“Ko kuṭikāyaṃ bhikkhu kuṭikāyaṃ,
Vītarāgo susamāhitacitto;
Evaṃ jānāhi āvuso,
Amoghā te kuṭikā katā”ti.
