1.12.9 Vajjiputtattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Dvādasamavagga
Vajjiputtattheragāthā
119.
265
“Rukkhamūlagahanaṃ pasakkiya,
Nibbānaṃ hadayasmiṃ opiya;
Jhāya gotama mā ca pamādo,
Kiṃ te biḷibiḷikā karissatī”ti.
