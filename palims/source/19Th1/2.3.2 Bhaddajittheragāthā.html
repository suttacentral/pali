2.3.2 Bhaddajittheragāthā
Dukanipāta
Tatiyavagga
Bhaddajittheragāthā
163.
341
“Panādo nāma so rājā,
yassa yūpo suvaṇṇayo;
Tiriyaṃ soḷasubbedho,
ubbhamāhu sahassadhā.
164.
342
Sahassakaṇḍo satageṇḍu,
dhajālu haritāmayo;
Anaccuṃ tattha gandhabbā,
chasahassāni sattadhā”ti.
343…  Bhaddajitthero… .
