1.3.3 Gosālattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Tatiyavagga
Gosālattheragāthā
23.
54
“Ahaṃ kho veḷugumbasmiṃ,
bhutvāna madhupāyasaṃ;
Padakkhiṇaṃ sammasanto,
khandhānaṃ udayabbayaṃ;
Sānuṃ paṭigamissāmi,
vivekamanubrūhayan”ti.
55…  Gosālo thero… .
