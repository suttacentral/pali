1.11.5 Malitavambhattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Ekādasamavagga
Malitavambhattheragāthā
105.
235
“Ukkaṇṭhitopi na vase,
ramamānopi pakkame;
Na tvevānatthasaṃhitaṃ,
vase vāsaṃ vicakkhaṇo”ti.
