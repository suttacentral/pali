1.1.5 Dabbattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Dabbattheragāthā
5.
13
“Yo duddamiyo damena danto,
Dabbo santusito vitiṇṇakaṅkho;
Vijitāvī apetabheravo hi,
Dabbo so parinibbuto ṭhitatto”ti.
14Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā dabbo thero gāthaṃ abhāsitthāti.
