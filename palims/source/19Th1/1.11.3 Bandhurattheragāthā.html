1.11.3 Bandhurattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Ekādasamavagga
Bandhurattheragāthā
103.
231
“Nāhaṃ etena atthiko,
Sukhito dhammarasena tappito;
Pitvā rasaggamuttamaṃ,
Na ca kāhāmi visena santhavan”ti.
