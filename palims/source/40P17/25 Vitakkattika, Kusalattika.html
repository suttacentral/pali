25 Vitakkattika, Kusalattika
Vitakkattika, Kusalattika
42.
116Savitakkasavicāraṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasavitakkasavicāro nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
117Avitakkavicāramattaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naavitakkavicāramatto nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pannarasa, adhipatiyā pannarasa…pe…  avigate pannarasa.
118Savitakkasavicāraṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasavitakkasavicāro naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava, adhipatiyā nava…pe…  avigate nava.
