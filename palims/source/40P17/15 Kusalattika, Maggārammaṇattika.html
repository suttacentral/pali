27.
72Kusalaṃ maggārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo namaggārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  avigate cha.
