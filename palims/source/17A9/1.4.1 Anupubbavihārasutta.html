1.4.1 Anupubbavihārasutta
Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka
Mahāvagga
Anupubbavihārasutta
32.
246“Navayime, bhikkhave, anupubbavihārā. Katame nava? Paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ, dutiyaṃ jhānaṃ, tatiyaṃ jhānaṃ, catutthaṃ jhānaṃ, ākāsānañcāyatanaṃ, viññāṇañcāyatanaṃ, ākiñcaññāyatanaṃ, nevasaññānāsaññāyatanaṃ, saññāvedayitanirodho—  ime kho, bhikkhave, nava anupubbavihārā”ti.
247Paṭhamaṃ.
