2.2.7 Macchariyasutta
Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka
Satipaṭṭhānavagga
Macchariyasutta
69.
454“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, macchariyāni. Katamāni pañca? Āvāsamacchariyaṃ, kulamacchariyaṃ, lābhamacchariyaṃ, vaṇṇamacchariyaṃ, dhammamacchariyaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañca macchariyāni.
455Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṃ macchariyānaṃ pahānāya…pe…  ime cattāro satipaṭṭhānā bhāvetabbā”ti.
456Sattamaṃ.
