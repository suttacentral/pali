65 Sanidassanattika, Sārammaṇaduka
Sanidassanattika, Sārammaṇaduka
111.
271Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ sārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho nasārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ sārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho nasārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…pe…  anidassanaappaṭighaṃ sārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho nasārammaṇo ca naanidassanasappaṭigho nasārammaṇo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  aññamaññe tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
272Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ anārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho naanārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
