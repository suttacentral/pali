38 Hīnattika, Hetuduka
Hīnattika, Hetuduka
80.
209Hīnaṃ hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahīno nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā terasa, ārammaṇe nava…pe…  vipāke aṭṭha…pe…  avigate terasa.
210Hīnaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca namajjhimo nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava…pe…  vipāke cha…pe…  avigate nava.
