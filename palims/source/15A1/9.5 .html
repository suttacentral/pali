Pamādādivagga
86.
181“Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yo evaṃ mahato anatthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, mahicchatā. Mahicchatā, bhikkhave, mahato anatthāya saṃvattatī”ti.
