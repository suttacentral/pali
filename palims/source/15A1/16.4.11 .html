16.4.11
Ekadhammapāḷi
Catutthavagga
332.
423…  Evamevaṃ kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye saṃviggā yoniso padahanti; atha kho eteva sattā bahutarā ye saṃviggā yoniso na padahanti.
