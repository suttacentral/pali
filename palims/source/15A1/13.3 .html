13.3
Ekapuggalavagga
172.
265“Ekapuggalo, bhikkhave, loke uppajjamāno uppajjati acchariyamanusso. Katamo ekapuggalo? Tathāgato arahaṃ sammāsambuddho. Ayaṃ kho, bhikkhave, ekapuggalo loke uppajjamāno uppajjati acchariyamanusso”ti.
