9.14
Pamādādivagga
95.
199“Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yo evaṃ mahato atthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, kalyāṇamittatā. Kalyāṇamittatā, bhikkhave, mahato atthāya saṃvattatī”ti.
200Cuddasamaṃ.
