4.4
Adantavagga
34.
72“Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yaṃ evaṃ guttaṃ mahato atthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, cittaṃ. Cittaṃ, bhikkhave, guttaṃ mahato atthāya saṃvattatī”ti.
73Catutthaṃ.
