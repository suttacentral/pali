Pamādādivagga
85.
179“Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yo evaṃ mahato atthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, vīriyārambho. Vīriyārambho, bhikkhave, mahato atthāya saṃvattatī”ti.
