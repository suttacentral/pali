Adantavagga
35.
74“Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yaṃ evaṃ arakkhitaṃ mahato anatthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, cittaṃ. Cittaṃ, bhikkhave, arakkhitaṃ mahato anatthāya saṃvattatī”ti.
75Pañcamaṃ.
