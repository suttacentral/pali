10.1
Dutiyapamādādivagga
98.
206“Ajjhattikaṃ, bhikkhave, aṅganti karitvā nāññaṃ ekaṅgampi samanupassāmi yaṃ evaṃ mahato anatthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, pamādo. Pamādo, bhikkhave, mahato anatthāya saṃvattatī”ti.
207Paṭhamaṃ.
