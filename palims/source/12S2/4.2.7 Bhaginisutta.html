4.2.7 Bhaginisutta
Anamataggasaṃyutta
Dutiyavagga
Bhaginisutta
140.
879Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati…pe…  “na so, bhikkhave, satto sulabharūpo yo nabhaginibhūtapubbo…pe…  alaṃ vimuccitun”ti.
880Sattamaṃ.
