4.2.5 Pitusutta
Anamataggasaṃyutta
Dutiyavagga
Pitusutta
138.
875Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. “Anamataggoyaṃ, bhikkhave, saṃsāro…pe…  na so, bhikkhave, satto sulabharūpo yo napitābhūtapubbo…pe…  alaṃ vimuccitun”ti.
876Pañcamaṃ.
