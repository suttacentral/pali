6.4.3 Kusaladhammasutta
Lābhasakkārasaṃyutta
Catutthavagga
Kusaladhammasutta
182.
1088Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. “Dāruṇo, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko. Lābhasakkārasilokena abhibhūtassa pariyādiṇṇacittassa, bhikkhave, devadattassa kusalo dhammo samucchedamagamā. Evaṃ dāruṇo kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko…pe…  sikkhitabban”ti.
1089Tatiyaṃ.
