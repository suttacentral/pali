28Sukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
29Nahetuyā ekavīsa, naārammaṇe ekavīsa…pe…  nakamme nava…pe…  navippayutte cuddasa…pe…  novigate ekavīsa.
