9.1.4 Vīmaṃsiddhipāda
Iddhipādavibhaṅga
Suttantabhājanīya
Vīmaṃsiddhipāda
441.
1482Kathañca bhikkhu vīmaṃsāsamādhipadhānasaṅkhārasamannāgataṃ iddhipādaṃ bhāveti? Vīmaṃsañce bhikkhu adhipatiṃ karitvā labhati samādhiṃ labhati cittassekaggataṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “vīmaṃsāsamādhi”. So anuppannānaṃ pāpakānaṃ akusalānaṃ dhammānaṃ anuppādāya chandaṃ janeti vāyamati vīriyaṃ ārabhati cittaṃ paggaṇhāti padahati, uppannānaṃ pāpakānaṃ akusalānaṃ dhammānaṃ pahānāya…pe…  anuppannānaṃ kusalānaṃ dhammānaṃ uppādāya…pe…  uppannānaṃ kusalānaṃ dhammānaṃ ṭhitiyā asammosāya bhiyyobhāvāya vepullāya bhāvanāya pāripūriyā chandaṃ janeti vāyamati vīriyaṃ ārabhati cittaṃ paggaṇhāti padahati. Ime vuccanti “padhānasaṅkhārā”. Iti ayañca vīmaṃsāsamādhi, ime ca padhānasaṅkhārā; tadekajjhaṃ abhisaññūhitvā abhisaṅkhipitvā vīmaṃsāsamādhipadhānasaṅkhārotveva saṅkhyaṃ gacchati.
442.
1483Tattha katamā vīmaṃsā? Yā paññā pajānanā…pe…  amoho dhammavicayo sammādiṭṭhi—  ayaṃ vuccati “vīmaṃsā”.
1484Tattha katamo samādhi? Yā cittassa ṭhiti saṇṭhiti…pe…  sammāsamādhi—  ayaṃ vuccati “samādhi”.
1485Tattha katamo padhānasaṅkhāro? Yo cetasiko vīriyārambho…pe…  sammāvāyāmo—  ayaṃ vuccati “padhānasaṅkhāro”. Iti imāya ca vīmaṃsāya, iminā ca samādhinā, iminā ca padhānasaṅkhārena upeto hoti…pe…  samannāgato. Tena vuccati “vīmaṃsāsamādhipadhānasaṅkhārasamannāgato”ti.
443.
1486“Iddhī”ti. Yā tesaṃ dhammānaṃ iddhi samiddhi ijjhanā samijjhanā lābho paṭilābho patti sampatti phusanā sacchikiriyā upasampadā.
1487“Iddhipādo”ti. Tathābhūtassa vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho, viññāṇakkhandho.
1488“Iddhipādaṃ bhāvetī”ti. Te dhamme āsevati bhāveti bahulīkaroti. Tena vuccati “iddhipādaṃ bhāvetī”ti.
1489Suttantabhājanīyaṃ.
