10.3 Pañhāpucchaka
Bojjhaṅgavibhaṅga
Pañhāpucchaka
482.
1594Satta bojjhaṅgā—  satisambojjhaṅgo, dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgo, vīriyasambojjhaṅgo, pītisambojjhaṅgo, passaddhisambojjhaṅgo, samādhisambojjhaṅgo, upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo.
Bojjhaṅgavibhaṅga
Pañhāpucchaka
483.
1595Sattannaṃ bojjhaṅgānaṃ kati kusalā, kati akusalā, kati abyākatā…pe…  kati saraṇā, kati araṇā?
