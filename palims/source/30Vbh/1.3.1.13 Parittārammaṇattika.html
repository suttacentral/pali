1.3.1.13 Parittārammaṇattika
Khandhavibhaṅga
Pañhāpucchaka
Tika
Parittārammaṇattika
512Rūpakkhandho anārammaṇo. Cattāro khandhā siyā parittārammaṇā, siyā mahaggatārammaṇā, siyā appamāṇārammaṇā, siyā na vattabbā—  “parittārammaṇā”tipi, “mahaggatārammaṇā”tipi, “appamāṇārammaṇā”tipi.
