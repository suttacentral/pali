1.3.1.22 Sanidassanattika
Khandhavibhaṅga
Pañhāpucchaka
Tika
Sanidassanattika
521Cattāro khandhā anidassanaappaṭighā. Rūpakkhandho siyā sanidassanasappaṭigho, siyā anidassanasappaṭigho, siyā anidassanaappaṭigho.
