4.1 Suttantabhājanīya
Saccavibhaṅga
Suttantabhājanīya
189.
714Cattāri ariyasaccāni—  dukkhaṃ ariyasaccaṃ, dukkhasamudayaṃ ariyasaccaṃ, dukkhanirodhaṃ ariyasaccaṃ, dukkhanirodhagāminī paṭipadā ariyasaccaṃ.
