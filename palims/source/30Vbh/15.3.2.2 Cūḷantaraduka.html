15.3.2.2 Cūḷantaraduka
Paṭisambhidāvibhaṅga
Pañhāpucchaka
Duka
Cūḷantaraduka
2196Sappaccayā, saṅkhatā, anidassanā, appaṭighā, arūpā, tisso paṭisambhidā lokiyā, atthapaṭisambhidā siyā lokiyā, siyā lokuttarā, kenaci viññeyyā, kenaci na viññeyyā.
