3.4.5 Dhammasutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Sādhuvagga
Dhammasutta
138.
1114“Dhammañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi adhammañca. Taṃ suṇātha…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, adhammo? Micchādiṭṭhi…pe…  micchāvimutti—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, adhammo. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, dhammo? Sammādiṭṭhi…pe…  sammāvimutti—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, dhammo”ti.
1115Pañcamaṃ.
