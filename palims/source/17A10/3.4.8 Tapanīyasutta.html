3.4.8 Tapanīyasutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Sādhuvagga
Tapanīyasutta
141.
1120“Tapanīyañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṃ desessāmi atapanīyañca. Taṃ suṇātha…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, tapanīyo dhammo? Micchādiṭṭhi…pe…  micchāvimutti—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, tapanīyo dhammo. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, atapanīyo dhammo? Sammādiṭṭhi…pe…  sammāvimutti—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, atapanīyo dhammo”ti.
1121Aṭṭhamaṃ.
