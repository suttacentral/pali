1.20 Hetuduka, Sārammaṇaduka
Hetuduka, Duka
Hetuduka, Sārammaṇaduka
44.
127Hetuṃ sārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu sārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
128Nahetuṃ sārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu sārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
129Hetuṃ sārammaṇañca nahetuṃ sārammaṇañca dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu sārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
130Hetuyā nava…pe…  avigate nava. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)
131Nahetuṃ anārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu anārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Sabbattha ekaṃ.)
