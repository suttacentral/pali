3.2.1 Puññakiriyavatthusutta
Tikanipāta
Dutiyavagga
Puññakiriyavatthusutta
60.
387Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ— 
388“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, puññakiriyavatthūni. Katamāni tīṇi? Dānamayaṃ puññakiriyavatthu, sīlamayaṃ puññakiriyavatthu, bhāvanāmayaṃ puññakiriyavatthu—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi puññakiriyavatthūnī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati— 
389
“Puññameva so sikkheyya,
āyataggaṃ sukhudrayaṃ;
Dānañca samacariyañca,
mettacittañca bhāvaye.
390
Ete dhamme bhāvayitvā,
tayo sukhasamuddaye;
Abyābajjhaṃ sukhaṃ lokaṃ,
paṇḍito upapajjatī”ti.
391Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
392Paṭhamaṃ.
