1.15.9 Ekapaṇṇajātaka
Ekakanipāta
Kakaṇṭakavagga
Ekapaṇṇajātaka
149.
329
“Ekapaṇṇo ayaṃ rukkho,
na bhūmyā caturaṅgulo;
Phalena visakappena,
mahāyaṃ kiṃ bhavissatī”ti.
330Ekapaṇṇajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
