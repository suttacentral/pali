1.6.6 Kañcanakkhandhajātaka
Ekakanipāta
Āsīsavagga
Kañcanakkhandhajātaka
56.
123
“Yo pahaṭṭhena cittena,
pahaṭṭhamanaso naro;
Bhāveti kusalaṃ dhammaṃ,
yogakkhemassa pattiyā;
Pāpuṇe anupubbena,
sabbasaṃyojanakkhayan”ti.
124Kañcanakkhandhajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
