1.5.8 Vedabbajātaka
Ekakanipāta
Atthakāmavagga
Vedabbajātaka
48.
104
“Anupāyena yo atthaṃ,
Icchati so vihaññati;
Cetā haniṃsu vedabbaṃ,
Sabbe te byasanamajjhagūti.
105Vedabbajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
