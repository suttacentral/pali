1.11.5 Dubbalakaṭṭhajātaka
Ekakanipāta
Parosatavagga
Dubbalakaṭṭhajātaka
105.
233
“Bahumpetaṃ vane kaṭṭhaṃ,
vāto bhañjati dubbalaṃ;
Tassa ce bhāyasī nāga,
kiso nūna bhavissasī”ti.
234Dubbalakaṭṭhajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
