2.6.4 Vīrakajātaka
Dukanipāta
Nataṃdaḷhavagga
Vīrakajātaka
107.
515
“Api vīraka passesi,
sakuṇaṃ mañjubhāṇakaṃ;
Mayūragīvasaṅkāsaṃ,
patiṃ mayhaṃ saviṭṭhakaṃ”.
108.
516
“Udakathalacarassa pakkhino,
Niccaṃ āmakamacchabhojino;
Tassānukaraṃ saviṭṭhako,
Sevāle paliguṇṭhito mato”ti.
517Vīrakajātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
