1.8.4 Rukkhadhammajātaka
Ekakanipāta
Varuṇavagga
Rukkhadhammajātaka
74.
163
“Sādhū sambahulā ñātī,
api rukkhā araññajā;
Vāto vahati ekaṭṭhaṃ,
brahantampi vanappatin”ti.
164Rukkhadhammajātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
