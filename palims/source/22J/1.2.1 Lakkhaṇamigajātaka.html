1.2.1 Lakkhaṇamigajātaka
Ekakanipāta
Sīlavagga
Lakkhaṇamigajātaka
11.
24
“Hoti sīlavataṃ attho,
paṭisanthāravuttinaṃ;
Lakkhaṇaṃ passa āyantaṃ,
ñātisaṅghapurakkhataṃ;
Atha passasimaṃ kāḷaṃ,
suvihīnaṃva ñātibhī”ti.
25Lakkhaṇamigajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
