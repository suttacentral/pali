2.6 Chakkapuggalapaññatti
Niddesa
Chakkapuggalapaññatti
202.
512Tatra yvāyaṃ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṃ saccāni abhisambujjhati, tattha ca sabbaññutaṃ pāpuṇāti balesu ca vasībhāvaṃ, sammāsambuddho tena daṭṭhabbo. (1)
513Tatra yvāyaṃ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṃ saccāni abhisambujjhati, na ca tattha sabbaññutaṃ pāpuṇāti na ca balesu vasībhāvaṃ, paccekasambuddho tena daṭṭhabbo. (2)
514Tatra yvāyaṃ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṃ saccāni anabhisambujjhati, diṭṭheva dhamme dukkhassantakaro hoti, sāvakapāramiñca pāpuṇāti, sāriputtamoggallānā tena daṭṭhabbā. (3)
515Tatra yvāyaṃ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṃ saccāni anabhisambujjhati, diṭṭheva dhamme dukkhassantakaro hoti, na ca sāvakapāramiṃ pāpuṇāti, avasesā arahantā tena daṭṭhabbā. (4)
516Tatra yvāyaṃ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṃ saccāni anabhisambujjhati, na ca diṭṭheva dhamme dukkhassantakaro hoti, anāgāmī hoti anāgantā itthattaṃ, anāgāmī tena daṭṭhabbo. (5)
517Tatra yvāyaṃ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṃ saccāni anabhisambujjhati, na ca diṭṭheva dhamme dukkhassantakaro hoti, āgantā itthattaṃ, sotāpannasakadāgāmino tena daṭṭhabbā. (6)
518Chakkaniddeso.
