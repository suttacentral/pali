4.3.5 Suddhakasutta
Indriyasaṃyutta
Chaḷindriyavagga
Suddhakasutta
495.
1069“Chayimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni cha? Cakkhundriyaṃ, sotindriyaṃ, ghānindriyaṃ, jivhindriyaṃ, kāyindriyaṃ, manindriyaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, cha indriyānī”ti.
1070Pañcamaṃ.
