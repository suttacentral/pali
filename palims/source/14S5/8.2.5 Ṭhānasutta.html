8.2.5 Ṭhānasutta
Anuruddhasaṃyutta
Dutiyavagga
Ṭhānasutta
913.
1601“Imesañca panāhaṃ, āvuso, catunnaṃ satipaṭṭhānānaṃ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā ṭhānañca ṭhānato aṭṭhānañca aṭṭhānato yathābhūtaṃ pajānāmī”ti.
1602Pañcamaṃ.
