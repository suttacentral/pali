2.18.1--10 Punaoghādisutta
Bojjhaṅgasaṃyutta
Punaoghavagga
Punaoghādisutta
357--366.
744…pe… 
745Bojjhaṅgasaṃyuttassa punaoghavaggo aṭṭhārasamo.
746Uddānaṃ
747
Ogho yogo upādānaṃ,
Ganthā anusayena ca;
Kāmaguṇā nīvaraṇā,
Khandhā oruddhambhāgiyānīti.
748(Rāgavinayapariyosāna-dosavinayapariyosāna-mohavinayapariyosānavaggo vitthāretabbo.) (Yadapi maggasaṃyuttaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ, tadapi bojjhaṅgasaṃyuttaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)
749Bojjhaṅgasaṃyuttaṃ dutiyaṃ.
