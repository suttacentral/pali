44 Lokiyaduka, Sanidassanattika
Lokiyaduka, Sanidassanattika
92.
235Nalokiyaṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca lokiyo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ.
236Nalokuttaraṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca lokiyo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ.
237(Gaṇitakena gaṇetabbā tīṇi pañhā. Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
238Nalokiyaṃ naanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca lokiyo anidassanasappaṭigho… . (Tīṇiyeva pañhā kātabbā. Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  aññamaññe ekaṃ…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
239Nalokuttaraṃ naanidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca lokiyo anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.
