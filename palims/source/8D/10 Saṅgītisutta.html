10 Saṅgītisutta
Saṅgītisutta
296.
773Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā mallesu cārikaṃ caramāno mahatā bhikkhusaṃghena saddhiṃ pañcamattehi bhikkhusatehi yena pāvā nāma mallānaṃ nagaraṃ tadavasari. Tatra sudaṃ bhagavā pāvāyaṃ viharati cundassa kammāraputtassa ambavane.
