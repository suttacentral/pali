5.1.7 Padhānadesanā
Sampasādanīyasutta
Sāriputtasīhanāda
Padhānadesanā
151.
311Aparaṃ pana, bhante, etadānuttariyaṃ, yathā bhagavā dhammaṃ deseti padhānesu. Sattime, bhante, sambojjhaṅgā satisambojjhaṅgo dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgo vīriyasambojjhaṅgo pītisambojjhaṅgo passaddhisambojjhaṅgo samādhisambojjhaṅgo upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo. Etadānuttariyaṃ, bhante, padhānesu.
