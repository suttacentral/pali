11 Dasuttarasutta
Dasuttarasutta
350.
1132Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā campāyaṃ viharati gaggarāya pokkharaṇiyā tīre mahatā bhikkhusaṃghena saddhiṃ pañcamattehi bhikkhusatehi. Tatra kho āyasmā sāriputto bhikkhū āmantesi—  “āvuso bhikkhave”ti. “Āvuso”ti kho te bhikkhū āyasmato sāriputtassa paccassosuṃ. Āyasmā sāriputto etadavoca— 
Dasuttarasutta
1133
“Dasuttaraṃ pavakkhāmi,
dhammaṃ nibbānapattiyā;
Dukkhassantakiriyāya,
sabbaganthappamocanaṃ.
