Parittattika
Paṭiccādivāra
25.
53Naparittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  namahaggataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  naappamāṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
54Hetuyā bāttiṃsa, ārammaṇe sattavīsa, adhipatiyā sattavīsa…pe…  aññamaññe tiṃsa…pe…  purejāte āsevane catuvīsa…pe…  avigate bāttiṃsa.
