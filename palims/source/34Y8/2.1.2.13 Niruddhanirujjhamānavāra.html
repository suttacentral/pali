2.1.2.13 Niruddhanirujjhamānavāra
Niddesa
Suddhacittasāmañña
Dhammavāra
Niruddhanirujjhamānavāra
103.
490»  Niruddhaṃ nirujjhamānanti? No.
491«  Nirujjhamānaṃ niruddhanti? No.
492»  Na niruddhaṃ na nirujjhamānanti?
493Bhaṅgakkhaṇe na niruddhaṃ, no ca na nirujjhamānaṃ. Uppādakkhaṇe anāgatañca cittaṃ na ceva niruddhaṃ na ca nirujjhamānaṃ.
494«  Na nirujjhamānaṃ na niruddhanti?
495Atītaṃ cittaṃ na nirujjhamānaṃ, no ca na niruddhaṃ. Uppādakkhaṇe anāgatañca cittaṃ na ceva nirujjhamānaṃ na ca niruddhaṃ.
