38.6 Gandhamuṭṭhiyattheraapadāna
Bodhivandanavagga
Gandhamuṭṭhiyattheraapadāna
33.
4138
“Citake karīyamāne,
nānāgandhe samāhate;
Pasannacitto sumano,
gandhamuṭṭhimapūjayiṃ.
34.
4139
Satasahassito kappe,
citakaṃ yaṃ apūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
citapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
35.
4140
Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
…pe… 
viharāmi anāsavo.
36.
4141
Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.
37.
4142
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
4143Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā gandhamuṭṭhiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
4144Gandhamuṭṭhiyattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
