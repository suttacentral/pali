16.5 Mandāravapupphapūjakattheraapadāna
Bandhujīvakavagga
Mandāravapupphapūjakattheraapadāna
25.
2385
“Devaputto ahaṃ santo,
pūjayiṃ sikhināyakaṃ;
Mandāravena pupphena,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.
26.
2386
Sattāhaṃ chadanaṃ āsi,
dibbaṃ mālaṃ tathāgate;
Sabbe janā samāgantvā,
namassiṃsu tathāgataṃ.
27.
2387
Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
28.
2388
Ito ca dasame kappe,
rājāhosiṃ jutindharo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
29.
2389
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
2390Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā mandāravapupphapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2391Mandāravapupphapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
