40.1.8.7 Nakhacchedanānisaṃsa
Pilindavacchavagga
Pilindavacchattheraapadāna
Dānānisaṃsakathā
Nakhacchedanānisaṃsa
112.
4409
Nakhacchedane sugate,
datvā saṃghe gaṇuttame;
Pañcānisaṃse anubhomi,
kammānucchavike mama.
113.
4410
Dāsidāse gavasse ca,
bhatake nāṭake bahū;
Nhāpite bhattake sūde,
sabbattheva labhāmahaṃ.
