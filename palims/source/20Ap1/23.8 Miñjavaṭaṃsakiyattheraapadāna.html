23.8 Miñjavaṭaṃsakiyattheraapadāna
Ālambaṇadāyakavagga
Miñjavaṭaṃsakiyattheraapadāna
39.
2951
“Nibbute lokanāthamhi,
sikhimhi vadataṃ vare;
Vaṭaṃsakehi ākiṇṇaṃ,
bodhipūjaṃ akāsahaṃ.
40.
2952
Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pūjamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bodhipūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
41.
2953
Ito chabbīsatikappe,
ahuṃ meghabbhanāmako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
42.
2954
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
2955Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā miñjavaṭaṃsakiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2956Miñjavaṭaṃsakiyattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
