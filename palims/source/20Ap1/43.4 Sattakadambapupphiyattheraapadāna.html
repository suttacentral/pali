43.4 Sattakadambapupphiyattheraapadāna
Sakiṃsammajjakavagga
Sattakadambapupphiyattheraapadāna
61.
5536
“Himavantassāvidūre,
kukkuṭo nāma pabbato;
Tamhi pabbatapādamhi,
satta buddhā vasiṃsu te.
62.
5537
Kadambaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
paggahetvāna añjaliṃ;
Satta mālā gahetvāna,
puññacittena okiriṃ.
63.
5538
Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.
64.
5539
Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
65.
5540
Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
…pe… 
viharāmi anāsavo.
66.
5541
Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.
67.
5542
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
5543Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sattakadambapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
5544Sattakadambapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
