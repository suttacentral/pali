28.5 Ekañjalikattheraapadāna
Suvaṇṇabibbohanavagga
Ekañjalikattheraapadāna
19.
3249
“Udumbare vasantassa,
niyate paṇṇasanthare;
Vutthokāso mayā dinno,
samaṇassa mahesino.
20.
3250
Tissassa dvipadindassa,
lokanāthassa tādino;
Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
santhariṃ pupphasantharaṃ.
21.
3251
Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ kariṃ pupphasantharaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
santharassa idaṃ phalaṃ.
22.
3252
Ito cuddasakappamhi,
ahosiṃ manujādhipo;
Ekaañjaliko nāma,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
23.
3253
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
3254Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekañjaliko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
3255Ekañjalikattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
