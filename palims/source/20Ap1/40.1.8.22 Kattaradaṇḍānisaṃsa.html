40.1.8.22 Kattaradaṇḍānisaṃsa
Pilindavacchavagga
Pilindavacchattheraapadāna
Dānānisaṃsakathā
Kattaradaṇḍānisaṃsa
149.
4446
Kattaradaṇḍe sugate,
datvā saṃghe gaṇuttame;
Chānisaṃse anubhomi,
kammānucchavike mama.
150.
4447
Puttā mayhaṃ bahū honti,
tāso mayhaṃ na vijjati;
Appasayho sadā homi,
sabbārakkhehi rakkhito;
Khalitampi na jānāmi,
abhantaṃ mānasaṃ mama.
