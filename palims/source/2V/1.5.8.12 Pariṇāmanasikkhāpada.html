1.5.8.12 Pariṇāmanasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Pācittiyakaṇḍa
Sahadhammikavagga
Pariṇāmanasikkhāpada
489.
1435Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena sāvatthiyaṃ aññatarassa pūgassa saṃghassa sacīvarabhattaṃ paṭiyattaṃ hoti—  “bhojetvā cīvarena acchādessāmā”ti. Atha kho chabbaggiyā bhikkhū yena so pūgo tenupasaṅkamiṃsu; upasaṅkamitvā taṃ pūgaṃ etadavocuṃ—  “dethāvuso, imāni cīvarāni imesaṃ bhikkhūnan”ti. “Na mayaṃ, bhante, dassāma. Amhākaṃ saṃghassa anuvassaṃ sacīvarabhikkhā paññattā”ti. “Bahū, āvuso, saṃghassa dāyakā, bahū saṃghassa bhattā. Ime tumhe nissāya tumhe sampassantā idha viharanti. Tumhe ce imesaṃ na dassatha, atha ko carahi imesaṃ dassati? Dethāvuso, imāni cīvarāni imesaṃ bhikkhūnan”ti. Atha kho so pūgo chabbaggiyehi bhikkhūhi nippīḷiyamāno yathāpaṭiyattaṃ cīvaraṃ chabbaggiyānaṃ bhikkhūnaṃ datvā saṃghaṃ bhattena parivisi. Ye te bhikkhū jānanti saṃghassa sacīvarabhattaṃ paṭiyattaṃ “na ca jānanti chabbaggiyānaṃ bhikkhūnaṃ dinnan”ti te evamāhaṃsu—  “oṇojethāvuso, saṃghassa cīvaran”ti. “Natthi, bhante. Yathāpaṭiyattaṃ cīvaraṃ ayyā chabbaggiyā ayyānaṃ chabbaggiyānaṃ pariṇāmesun”ti.
1436Ye te bhikkhū appicchā…pe…  te ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma chabbaggiyā bhikkhū jānaṃ saṃghikaṃ lābhaṃ pariṇataṃ puggalassa pariṇāmessantī”ti…pe…  “saccaṃ kira tumhe, bhikkhave, jānaṃ saṃghikaṃ lābhaṃ pariṇataṃ puggalassa pariṇāmethā”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā…pe…  kathañhi nāma tumhe, moghapurisā, jānaṃ saṃghikaṃ lābhaṃ pariṇataṃ puggalassa pariṇāmessatha. Netaṃ, moghapurisā, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya…pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddiseyyātha— 
490.
1437“Yo pana bhikkhu jānaṃ saṃghikaṃ lābhaṃ pariṇataṃ puggalassa pariṇāmeyya, pācittiyan”ti. (82:131)
491.
1438Yo panāti yo yādiso…pe…  bhikkhūti…pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippeto bhikkhūti.
1439Jānāti nāma sāmaṃ vā jānāti, aññe vā tassa ārocenti, so vā āroceti.
1440Saṃghikaṃ nāma saṃghassa dinnaṃ hoti pariccattaṃ.
1441Lābho nāma cīvarapiṇḍapātasenāsanagilānappaccayabhesajjaparikkhārā, antamaso cuṇṇapiṇḍopi, dantakaṭṭhampi, dasikasuttampi.
1442Pariṇataṃ nāma “dassāma karissāmā”ti vācā bhinnā hoti, taṃ puggalassa pariṇāmeti, āpatti pācittiyassa.
492.
1443Pariṇate pariṇatasaññī puggalassa pariṇāmeti, āpatti pācittiyassa. Pariṇate vematiko puggalassa pariṇāmeti, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Pariṇate apariṇatasaññī puggalassa pariṇāmeti, anāpatti. Saṃghassa pariṇataṃ aññasaṃghassa vā cetiyassa vā pariṇāmeti, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Cetiyassa pariṇataṃ aññacetiyassa vā saṃghassa vā puggalassa vā pariṇāmeti, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Puggalassa pariṇataṃ aññapuggalassa vā saṃghassa vā cetiyassa vā pariṇāmeti, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Apariṇate pariṇatasaññī, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Apariṇate vematiko, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Apariṇate apariṇatasaññī, anāpatti.
493.
1444Anāpatti—  “kattha demā”ti pucchīyamāno—  “yattha tumhākaṃ deyyadhammo paribhogaṃ vā labheyya paṭisaṅkhāraṃ vā labheyya ciraṭṭhitiko vā assa yattha vā pana tumhākaṃ cittaṃ pasīdati tattha dethā”ti bhaṇati, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.
1445Pariṇāmanasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ dvādasamaṃ.
Sahadhammikavaggo aṭṭhamo.
1446Tassuddānaṃ
1447
Sahadhamma vivaṇṇañca,
mohāpanaṃ pahārakaṃ;
Talasatti amūlañca,
sañcicca ca upassuti;
Paṭibāhana chandañca,
dabbañca pariṇāmananti.
