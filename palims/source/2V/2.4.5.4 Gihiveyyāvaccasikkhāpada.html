2.4.5.4 Gihiveyyāvaccasikkhāpada
Bhikkhunivibhaṅga
Pācittiyakaṇḍa
Cittāgāravagga
Gihiveyyāvaccasikkhāpada
990.
3019Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena bhikkhuniyo gihiveyyāvaccaṃ karonti. Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā…pe…  tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo gihiveyyāvaccaṃ karissantī”ti…pe…  “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo gihiveyyāvaccaṃ karontī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā…pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo gihiveyyāvaccaṃ karissanti. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya…pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu— 
991.
3020“Yā pana bhikkhunī gihiveyyāvaccaṃ kareyya, pācittiyan”ti. (44:99)
992.
3021Yā panāti yā yādisā…pe…  bhikkhunīti…pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.
3022Gihiveyyāvaccaṃ nāma agārikassa yāguṃ vā bhattaṃ vā khādanīyaṃ vā pacati, sāṭakaṃ vā veṭhanaṃ vā dhovati, āpatti pācittiyassa.
993.
3023Anāpatti—  yāgupāne, saṃghabhatte, cetiyapūjāya, attano veyyāvaccakarassa yāguṃ vā bhattaṃ vā khādanīyaṃ vā pacati, sāṭakaṃ vā veṭhanaṃ vā dhovati, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.
3024Catutthasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
