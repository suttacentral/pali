1.7.1.5 Susaṃvutasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Parimaṇḍalavagga
Susaṃvutasikkhāpada
580.
1705Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū hatthampi pādampi kīḷāpentā antaraghare gacchanti…pe… .
1706“Susaṃvuto antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (5:150)
1707Susaṃvutena antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca hatthaṃ vā pādaṃ vā kīḷāpento antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1708Anāpatti—  asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.
1709Pañcamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
