1.7.1.6 Dutiyasusaṃvutasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Parimaṇḍalavagga
Dutiyasusaṃvutasikkhāpada
581.
1710Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū hatthampi pādampi kīḷāpentā antaraghare nisīdanti…pe… .
1711“Susaṃvuto antaraghare nisīdissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (6:151)
1712Susaṃvutena antaraghare nisīditabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca hatthaṃ vā pādaṃ vā kīḷāpento antaraghare nisīdati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1713Anāpatti—  asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.
1714Chaṭṭhasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
