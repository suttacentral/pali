1.7.6.3 Pattanillehakasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Surusuruvagga
Pattanillehakasikkhāpada
629.
1950Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū pattanillehakaṃ bhuñjanti…pe… .
1951“Na pattanillehakaṃ bhuñjissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (53:198)
1952Na pattanillehakaṃ bhuñjitabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca pattanillehakaṃ bhuñjati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1953Anāpatti— asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, parittake sese ekato saṅkaḍḍhitvā nillehitvā bhuñjati, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.
1954Tatiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
