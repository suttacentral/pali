1.7.4.10 Ālopasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Sakkaccavagga
Ālopasikkhāpada
616.
1884Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū dīghaṃ ālopaṃ karonti…pe… .
1885“Parimaṇḍalaṃ ālopaṃ karissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (40:185)
1886Parimaṇḍalo ālopo kātabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca dīghaṃ ālopaṃ karoti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1887Anāpatti— asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, khajjake, phalāphale, uttaribhaṅge, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.
1888Dasamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
Sakkaccavaggo catuttho.
