Sanidassanattika, Pītittika
54.
171Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ napītisahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho pītisahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
172Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nasukhasahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho sukhasahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
173Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ naupekkhāsahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho upekkhāsahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
