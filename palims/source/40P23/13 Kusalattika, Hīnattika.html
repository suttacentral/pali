13 Kusalattika, Hīnattika
Kusalattika, Hīnattika
17.
60Nakusalaṃ nahīnaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā akusalo hīno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
61Nakusalaṃ namajjhimaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato majjhimo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
62Nakusalaṃ napaṇītaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo paṇīto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha…pe…  āsevane tīṇi…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
