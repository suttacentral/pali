2.2.5.6 Paccanīkapuggalokāsa
Pavattivāra
Nirodhavāra
Paccuppannānāgatavāra
Paccanīkapuggalokāsa
151.
646»  Yassa yattha kusalā dhammā na nirujjhanti tassa tattha akusalā dhammā na nirujjhissantīti?
647Sabbesaṃ cittassa uppādakkhaṇe kusalavippayuttacittassa bhaṅgakkhaṇe tesaṃ tattha kusalā dhammā na nirujjhanti, no ca tesaṃ tattha akusalā dhammā na nirujjhissanti. Aggamaggassa uppādakkhaṇe arahantānaṃ yassa cittassa anantarā aggamaggaṃ paṭilabhissanti tassa cittassa uppādakkhaṇe asaññasattānaṃ tesaṃ tattha kusalā ca dhammā na nirujjhanti akusalā ca dhammā na nirujjhissanti.
648«  Yassa vā pana yattha akusalā dhammā na nirujjhissanti tassa tattha kusalā dhammā na nirujjhantīti?
649Aggamaggassa bhaṅgakkhaṇe yassa cittassa anantarā aggamaggaṃ paṭilabhissanti tassa cittassa bhaṅgakkhaṇe tesaṃ tattha akusalā dhammā na nirujjhissanti, no ca tesaṃ tattha kusalā dhammā na nirujjhanti. Aggamaggassa uppādakkhaṇe arahantānaṃ yassa cittassa anantarā aggamaggaṃ paṭilabhissanti tassa cittassa uppādakkhaṇe asaññasattānaṃ tesaṃ tattha akusalā ca dhammā na nirujjhissanti kusalā ca dhammā na nirujjhanti.
650»  Yassa yattha kusalā dhammā na nirujjhanti tassa tattha abyākatā dhammā na nirujjhissantīti?
651Sabbesaṃ cittassa uppādakkhaṇe kusalavippayuttacittassa bhaṅgakkhaṇe asaññasattānaṃ tesaṃ tattha kusalā dhammā na nirujjhanti, no ca tesaṃ tattha abyākatā dhammā na nirujjhissanti. Pacchimacittassa bhaṅgakkhaṇe tesaṃ tattha kusalā ca dhammā na nirujjhanti abyākatā ca dhammā na nirujjhissanti.
652«  Yassa vā pana yattha abyākatā dhammā na nirujjhissanti tassa tattha kusalā dhammā na nirujjhantīti? Āmantā.
