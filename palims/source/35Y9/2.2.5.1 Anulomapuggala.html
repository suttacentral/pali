2.2.5.1 Anulomapuggala
Pavattivāra
Nirodhavāra
Paccuppannānāgatavāra
Anulomapuggala
143.
614»  Yassa kusalā dhammā nirujjhanti tassa akusalā dhammā nirujjhissantīti?
615Aggamaggassa bhaṅgakkhaṇe yassa cittassa anantarā aggamaggaṃ paṭilabhissanti tassa cittassa bhaṅgakkhaṇe tesaṃ kusalā dhammā nirujjhanti, no ca tesaṃ akusalā dhammā nirujjhissanti. Itaresaṃ kusalānaṃ bhaṅgakkhaṇe tesaṃ kusalā ca dhammā nirujjhanti akusalā ca dhammā nirujjhissanti.
616«  Yassa vā pana akusalā dhammā nirujjhissanti tassa kusalā dhammā nirujjhantīti?
617Sabbesaṃ cittassa uppādakkhaṇe kusalavippayuttacittassa bhaṅgakkhaṇe nirodhasamāpannānaṃ asaññasattānaṃ tesaṃ akusalā dhammā nirujjhissanti, no ca tesaṃ kusalā dhammā nirujjhanti. Kusalānaṃ bhaṅgakkhaṇe tesaṃ akusalā ca dhammā nirujjhissanti kusalā ca dhammā nirujjhanti.
618»  Yassa kusalā dhammā nirujjhanti tassa abyākatā dhammā nirujjhissantīti? Āmantā.
619«  Yassa vā pana abyākatā dhammā nirujjhissanti tassa kusalā dhammā nirujjhantīti?
620Sabbesaṃ cittassa uppādakkhaṇe kusalavippayuttacittassa bhaṅgakkhaṇe nirodhasamāpannānaṃ asaññasattānaṃ tesaṃ abyākatā dhammā nirujjhissanti, no ca tesaṃ kusalā dhammā nirujjhanti. Kusalānaṃ bhaṅgakkhaṇe tesaṃ abyākatā ca dhammā nirujjhissanti kusalā ca dhammā nirujjhanti.
