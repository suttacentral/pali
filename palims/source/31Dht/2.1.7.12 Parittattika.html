2.1.7.12 Parittattika
Niddesa
Paṭhamanaya saṅgahāsaṅgahapadaniddesa
Tika
Parittattika
95.
141Parittā dhammā pañcahi khandhehi dvādasahāyatanehi aṭṭhārasahi dhātūhi saṅgahitā. Katihi asaṅgahitā? Na kehici khandhehi na kehici āyatanehi na kāhici dhātūhi asaṅgahitā.
96.
142Appamāṇā dhammā…  paṇītā dhammā asaṅkhataṃ khandhato ṭhapetvā catūhi khandhehi dvīhāyatanehi dvīhi dhātūhi saṅgahitā. Katihi asaṅgahitā? Ekena khandhena dasahāyatanehi soḷasahi dhātūhi asaṅgahitā.
