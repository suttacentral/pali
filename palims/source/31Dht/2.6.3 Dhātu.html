2.6.3 Dhātu
Niddesa
Chaṭṭhanaya Sampayogavippayogapadaniddesa
Dhātu
233.
310Cakkhudhātu…pe…  phoṭṭhabbadhātu…pe…  sampayuttāti? Natthi. Katihi vippayuttā? Catūhi khandhehi ekenāyatanena sattahi dhātūhi vippayuttā; ekenāyatanena ekāya dhātuyā kehici vippayuttā.
234.
311Cakkhuviññāṇadhātu…pe…  manodhātu…  manoviññāṇadhātu tīhi khandhehi sampayuttā; ekenāyatanena ekāya dhātuyā kehici sampayuttā. Katihi vippayuttā? Ekena khandhena dasahāyatanehi soḷasahi dhātūhi vippayuttā; ekenāyatanena ekāya dhātuyā kehici vippayuttā.
