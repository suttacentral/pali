2.4.11
Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka
Dhammavagga
98.
180“Dveme, bhikkhave, dhammā. Katame dve? Āpattikusalatā ca āpattivuṭṭhānakusalatā ca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve dhammā”ti.
181Dhammavaggo catuttho.
