3.3.5
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Dānavagga
146.
237“Dveme, bhikkhave, bhogā. Katame dve? Āmisabhogo ca dhammabhogo ca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve bhogā. Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, imesaṃ dvinnaṃ bhogānaṃ yadidaṃ dhammabhogo”ti.
