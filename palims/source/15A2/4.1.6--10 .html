Peyyāla
Kodhapeyyāla
182.
277“Dveme, bhikkhave, dhammā. Katame dve? Akkodho ca anupanāho ca…  amakkho ca apaḷāso ca…  anissā ca amacchariyañca…  amāyā ca asāṭheyyañca…  hirī ca ottappañca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve dhammā”.
