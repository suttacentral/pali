32 Āsavaduka, Kusalattika
Āsavaduka, Kusalattika
41.
119Noāsavaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca noāsavo nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.
120Āsavaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca noāsavo naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
121Noāsavaṃ abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paccayā nanoāsavo naabyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
