1.4.4 Catutthasuttantaniddesa
Mahāvagga
Indriyakathā
Catutthasuttantaniddesa
198.
1073Purimanidānaṃ. “Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni pañca? Saddhindriyaṃ, vīriyindriyaṃ, satindriyaṃ, samādhindriyaṃ, paññindriyaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyāni. Imāni pañcindriyāni katihākārehi kenaṭṭhena daṭṭhabbāni? Imāni pañcindriyāni chahākārehi tenaṭṭhena daṭṭhabbāni—  ādhipateyyaṭṭhena ādivisodhanaṭṭhena adhimattaṭṭhena, adhiṭṭhānaṭṭhena, pariyādānaṭṭhena, patiṭṭhāpakaṭṭhena”.
