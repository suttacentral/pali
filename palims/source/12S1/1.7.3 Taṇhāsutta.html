1.7.3 Taṇhāsutta
Devatāsaṃyutta
Addhavagga
Taṇhāsutta
63.
315
“Kenassu nīyati loko,
kenassu parikassati;
Kissassu ekadhammassa,
sabbeva vasamanvagū”ti.
316
“Taṇhāya nīyati loko,
taṇhāya parikassati;
Taṇhāya ekadhammassa,
sabbeva vasamanvagū”ti.
