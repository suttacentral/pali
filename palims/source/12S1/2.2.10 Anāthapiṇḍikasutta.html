2.2.10 Anāthapiṇḍikasutta
Devaputtasaṃyutta
Anāthapiṇḍikavagga
Anāthapiṇḍikasutta
101.
462Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho anāthapiṇḍiko devaputto bhagavato santike imā gāthāyo abhāsi— 
463
“Idañhi taṃ jetavanaṃ,
isisaṃghanisevitaṃ;
Āvutthaṃ dhammarājena,
pītisañjananaṃ mama.
464
Kammaṃ vijjā ca dhammo ca,
sīlaṃ jīvitamuttamaṃ;
Etena maccā sujjhanti,
na gottena dhanena vā.
465
Tasmā hi paṇḍito poso,
sampassaṃ atthamattano;
Yoniso vicine dhammaṃ,
evaṃ tattha visujjhati.
466
Sāriputtova paññāya,
sīlena upasamena ca;
Yopi pāraṅgato bhikkhu,
etāvaparamo siyā”ti.
467Idamavoca anāthapiṇḍiko devaputto. Idaṃ vatvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā padakkhiṇaṃ katvā tatthevantaradhāyi.
468Atha kho bhagavā tassā rattiyā accayena bhikkhū āmantesi—  “imaṃ, bhikkhave, rattiṃ aññataro devaputto abhikkantāya rattiyā abhikkantavaṇṇo kevalakappaṃ jetavanaṃ obhāsetvā yenāhaṃ tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā maṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ aṭṭhāsi. Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho, bhikkhave, so devaputto mama santike imā gāthāyo abhāsi— 
469
‘Idañhi taṃ jetavanaṃ,
isisaṃghanisevitaṃ;
Āvutthaṃ dhammarājena,
pītisañjananaṃ mama.
470
Kammaṃ vijjā ca dhammo ca,
sīlaṃ jīvitamuttamaṃ;
Etena maccā sujjhanti,
na gottena dhanena vā.
471
Tasmā hi paṇḍito poso,
sampassaṃ atthamattano;
Yoniso vicine dhammaṃ,
evaṃ tattha visujjhati.
472
Sāriputtova paññāya,
sīlena upasamena ca;
Yopi pāraṅgato bhikkhu,
etāvaparamo siyā’ti.
473Idamavoca, bhikkhave, so devaputto. Idaṃ vatvā maṃ abhivādetvā padakkhiṇaṃ katvā tatthevantaradhāyī”ti.
474Evaṃ vutte, āyasmā ānando bhagavantaṃ etadavoca—  “so hi nūna, bhante, anāthapiṇḍiko devaputto bhavissati. Anāthapiṇḍiko gahapati āyasmante sāriputte abhippasanno ahosī”ti. “Sādhu sādhu, ānanda, yāvatakaṃ kho, ānanda, takkāya pattabbaṃ anuppattaṃ taṃ tayā. Anāthapiṇḍiko hi so, ānanda, devaputto”ti.
475Anāthapiṇḍikavaggo dutiyo.
476Tassuddānaṃ
477
Candimaso ca veṇḍu ca,
Dīghalaṭṭhi ca nandano;
Candano vāsudatto ca,
Subrahmā kakudhena ca;
Uttaro navamo vutto,
Dasamo anāthapiṇḍikoti.
