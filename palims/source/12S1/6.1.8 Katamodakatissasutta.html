6.1.8 Katamodakatissasutta
Brahmasaṃyutta
Paṭhamavagga
Katamodakatissasutta
179.
1072Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Tena kho pana samayena bhagavā divāvihāragato hoti paṭisallīno. Atha kho subrahmā ca paccekabrahmā suddhāvāso ca paccekabrahmā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkamiṃsu; upasaṅkamitvā paccekaṃ dvārabāhaṃ nissāya aṭṭhaṃsu. Atha kho suddhāvāso paccekabrahmā katamodakatissakaṃ bhikkhuṃ ārabbha bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi— 
1073
“Appameyyaṃ paminanto,
Kodha vidvā vikappaye;
Appameyyaṃ pamāyinaṃ,
Nivutaṃ taṃ maññe akissavan”ti.
