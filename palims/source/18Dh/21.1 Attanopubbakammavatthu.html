21.1 Attanopubbakammavatthu
Pakiṇṇakavagga
Attanopubbakammavatthu
290.
313
Mattāsukhapariccāgā,
passe ce vipulaṃ sukhaṃ;
Caje mattāsukhaṃ dhīro,
sampassaṃ vipulaṃ sukhaṃ.
