12.10 Attadatthattheravatthu
Attavagga
Attadatthattheravatthu
166.
178
Attadatthaṃ paratthena,
bahunāpi na hāpaye;
Attadatthamabhiññāya,
sadatthapasuto siyā.
179Attavaggo dvādasamo.
