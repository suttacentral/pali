15.6 Pasenadikosalavatthu
Sukhavagga
Pasenadikosalavatthu
204.
220
Ārogyaparamā lābhā,
Santuṭṭhiparamaṃ dhanaṃ;
Vissāsaparamā ñāti,
Nibbānaṃ paramaṃ sukhaṃ.
