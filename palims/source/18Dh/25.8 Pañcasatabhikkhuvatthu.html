25.8 Pañcasatabhikkhuvatthu
Bhikkhuvagga
Pañcasatabhikkhuvatthu
377.
404
Vassikā viya pupphāni,
maddavāni pamuñcati;
Evaṃ rāgañca dosañca,
vippamuñcetha bhikkhavo.
