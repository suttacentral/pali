18.7 Pañcaupāsakavatthu
Malavagga
Pañcaupāsakavatthu
246.
266
Yo pāṇamatipāteti,
musāvādañca bhāsati;
Loke adinnamādiyati,
paradārañca gacchati.
247.
267
Surāmerayapānañca,
yo naro anuyuñjati;
Idheva meso lokasmiṃ,
mūlaṃ khaṇati attano.
248.
268
Evaṃ bho purisa jānāhi,
pāpadhammā asaññatā;
Mā taṃ lobho adhammo ca,
ciraṃ dukkhāya randhayuṃ.
