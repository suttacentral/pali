16.5 Anitthigandhakumāravatthu
Piyavagga
Anitthigandhakumāravatthu
215.
233
Kāmato jāyatī soko,
kāmato jāyatī bhayaṃ;
Kāmato vippamuttassa,
natthi soko kuto bhayaṃ.
