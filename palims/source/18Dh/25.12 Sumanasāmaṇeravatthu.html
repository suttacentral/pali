25.12 Sumanasāmaṇeravatthu
Bhikkhuvagga
Sumanasāmaṇeravatthu
382.
409
Yo have daharo bhikkhu,
yuñjati buddhasāsane;
Somaṃ lokaṃ pabhāseti,
abbhā muttova candimā.
410Bhikkhuvaggo pañcavīsatimo.
