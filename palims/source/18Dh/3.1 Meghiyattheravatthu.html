3.1 Meghiyattheravatthu
Cittavagga
Meghiyattheravatthu
33.
36
Phandanaṃ capalaṃ cittaṃ,
dūrakkhaṃ dunnivārayaṃ;
Ujuṃ karoti medhāvī,
usukārova tejanaṃ.
34.
37
Vārijova thale khitto,
okamokataubbhato;
Pariphandatidaṃ cittaṃ,
māradheyyaṃ pahātave.
