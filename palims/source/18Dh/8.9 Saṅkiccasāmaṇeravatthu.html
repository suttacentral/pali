8.9 Saṅkiccasāmaṇeravatthu
Sahassavagga
Saṅkiccasāmaṇeravatthu
110.
118
Yo ca vassasataṃ jīve,
dussīlo asamāhito;
Ekāhaṃ jīvitaṃ seyyo,
sīlavantassa jhāyino.
