23.3 Parijiṇṇabrāhmaṇaputtavatthu
Nāgavagga
Parijiṇṇabrāhmaṇaputtavatthu
324.
349
Dhanapālo nāma kuñjaro,
Kaṭukabhedano dunnivārayo;
Baddho kabaḷaṃ na bhuñjati,
Sumarati nāgavanassa kuñjaro.
