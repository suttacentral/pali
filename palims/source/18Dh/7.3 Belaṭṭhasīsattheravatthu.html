7.3 Belaṭṭhasīsattheravatthu
Arahantavagga
Belaṭṭhasīsattheravatthu
92.
99
Yesaṃ sannicayo natthi,
ye pariññātabhojanā;
Suññato animitto ca,
vimokkho yesaṃ gocaro;
Ākāseva sakuntānaṃ,
gati tesaṃ durannayā.
