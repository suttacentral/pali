4.5 Macchariyakosiyaseṭṭhivatthu
Pupphavagga
Macchariyakosiyaseṭṭhivatthu
49.
53
Yathāpi bhamaro pupphaṃ,
vaṇṇagandhamaheṭhayaṃ;
Paleti rasamādāya,
evaṃ gāme munī care.
