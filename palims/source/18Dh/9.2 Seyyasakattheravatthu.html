9.2 Seyyasakattheravatthu
Pāpavagga
Seyyasakattheravatthu
117.
126
Pāpañce puriso kayirā,
Na naṃ kayirā punappunaṃ;
Na tamhi chandaṃ kayirātha,
Dukkho pāpassa uccayo.
