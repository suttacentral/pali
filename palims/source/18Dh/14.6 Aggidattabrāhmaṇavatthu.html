14.6 Aggidattabrāhmaṇavatthu
Buddhavagga
Aggidattabrāhmaṇavatthu
188.
202
Bahuṃ ve saraṇaṃ yanti,
pabbatāni vanāni ca;
Ārāmarukkhacetyāni,
manussā bhayatajjitā.
189.
203
Netaṃ kho saraṇaṃ khemaṃ,
netaṃ saraṇamuttamaṃ;
Netaṃ saraṇamāgamma,
sabbadukkhā pamuccati.
190.
204
Yo ca buddhañca dhammañca,
saṃghañca saraṇaṃ gato;
Cattāri ariyasaccāni,
sammappaññāya passati.
191.
205
Dukkhaṃ dukkhasamuppādaṃ,
dukkhassa ca atikkamaṃ;
Ariyaṃ caṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ,
dukkhūpasamagāminaṃ.
192.
206
Etaṃ kho saraṇaṃ khemaṃ,
etaṃ saraṇamuttamaṃ;
Etaṃ saraṇamāgamma,
sabbadukkhā pamuccati.
