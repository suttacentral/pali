13.3 Pañcasatavipassakabhikkhuvatthu
Lokavagga
Pañcasatavipassakabhikkhuvatthu
170.
183
Yathā pubbuḷakaṃ passe,
yathā passe marīcikaṃ;
Evaṃ lokaṃ avekkhantaṃ,
maccurājā na passati.
