26.4 Aññatarabrāhmaṇavatthu
Brāhmaṇavagga
Aññatarabrāhmaṇavatthu
386.
414
Jhāyiṃ virajamāsīnaṃ,
katakiccamanāsavaṃ;
Uttamatthamanuppattaṃ,
tamahaṃ brūmi brāhmaṇaṃ.
