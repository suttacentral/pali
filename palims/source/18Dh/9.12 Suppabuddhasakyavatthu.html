9.12 Suppabuddhasakyavatthu
Pāpavagga
Suppabuddhasakyavatthu
128.
137
Na antalikkhe na samuddamajjhe,
Na pabbatānaṃ vivaraṃ pavissa;
Na vijjatī so jagatippadeso,
Yatthaṭṭhitaṃ nappasaheyya maccu.
138Pāpavaggo navamo.
