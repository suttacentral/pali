25.3 Kokālikavatthu
Bhikkhuvagga
Kokālikavatthu
363.
390
Yo mukhasaṃyato bhikkhu,
mantabhāṇī anuddhato;
Atthaṃ dhammañca dīpeti,
madhuraṃ tassa bhāsitaṃ.
