14.3 Erakapattanāgarājavatthu
Buddhavagga
Erakapattanāgarājavatthu
182.
196
Kiccho manussapaṭilābho,
kicchaṃ maccāna jīvitaṃ;
Kicchaṃ saddhammassavanaṃ,
kiccho buddhānamuppādo.
