25.10 Naṅgalakulattheravatthu
Bhikkhuvagga
Naṅgalakulattheravatthu
379.
406
Attanā codayattānaṃ,
paṭimaṃsetha attanā;
So attagutto satimā,
sukhaṃ bhikkhu vihāhisi.
380.
407
Attā hi attano nātho,
ko hi nātho paro siyā;
Attā hi attano gati,
tasmā saṃyamamattānaṃ;
Assaṃ bhadraṃva vāṇijo.
