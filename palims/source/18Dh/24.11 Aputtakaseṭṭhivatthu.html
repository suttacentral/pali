24.11 Aputtakaseṭṭhivatthu
Taṇhāvagga
Aputtakaseṭṭhivatthu
355.
381
Hananti bhogā dummedhaṃ,
no ca pāragavesino;
Bhogataṇhāya dummedho,
hanti aññeva attanaṃ.
