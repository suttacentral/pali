16.7 Pañcasatadārakavatthu
Piyavagga
Pañcasatadārakavatthu
217.
235
Sīladassanasampannaṃ,
dhammaṭṭhaṃ saccavedinaṃ;
Attano kamma kubbānaṃ,
taṃ jano kurute piyaṃ.
