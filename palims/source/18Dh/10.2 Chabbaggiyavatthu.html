10.2 Chabbaggiyavatthu
Daṇḍavagga
Chabbaggiyavatthu
130.
140
Sabbe tasanti daṇḍassa,
sabbesaṃ jīvitaṃ piyaṃ;
Attānaṃ upamaṃ katvā,
na haneyya na ghātaye.
