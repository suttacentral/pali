3.3 Aññataraukkaṇṭhitabhikkhuvatthu
Cittavagga
Aññataraukkaṇṭhitabhikkhuvatthu
36.
39
Sududdasaṃ sunipuṇaṃ,
yatthakāmanipātinaṃ;
Cittaṃ rakkhetha medhāvī,
cittaṃ guttaṃ sukhāvahaṃ.
