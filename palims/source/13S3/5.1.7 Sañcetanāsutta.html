5.1.7 Sañcetanāsutta
Uppādasaṃyutta
Uppādavagga
Sañcetanāsutta
318.
1081Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Yo kho, bhikkhave, rūpasañcetanāya uppādo ṭhiti…pe…  jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo…pe…  yo dhammasañcetanāya uppādo ṭhiti abhinibbatti pātubhāvo, dukkhasseso uppādo, rogānaṃ ṭhiti, jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo. Yo ca kho, bhikkhave, rūpasañcetanāya nirodho…pe…  jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo…pe…  yo dhammasañcetanāya nirodho vūpasamo atthaṅgamo, dukkhasseso nirodho, rogānaṃ vūpasamo, jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo”ti.
1082Sattamaṃ.
