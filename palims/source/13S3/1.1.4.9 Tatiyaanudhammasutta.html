1.1.4.9 Tatiyaanudhammasutta
Khandhasaṃyutta
Mūlapaṇṇāsaka
Natumhākavagga
Tatiyaanudhammasutta
41.
185Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Dhammānudhammappaṭipannassa, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno ayamanudhammo hoti yaṃ rūpe dukkhānupassī vihareyya…pe…  parimuccati dukkhasmāti vadāmī”ti.
186Navamaṃ.
