1.1.4.8 Dutiyaanudhammasutta
Khandhasaṃyutta
Mūlapaṇṇāsaka
Natumhākavagga
Dutiyaanudhammasutta
40.
183Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Dhammānudhammappaṭipannassa, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno ayamanudhammo hoti yaṃ rūpe aniccānupassī vihareyya…pe…  parimuccati dukkhasmāti vadāmī”ti.
184Aṭṭhamaṃ.
