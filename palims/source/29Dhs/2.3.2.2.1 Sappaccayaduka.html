2.3.2.2.1 Sappaccayaduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Dukanikkhepa
Cūḷantaraduka
Sappaccayaduka
1089.
1758Katame dhammā sappaccayā? Pañcakkhandhā—  rūpakkhandho, vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho, viññāṇakkhandho—  ime dhammā sappaccayā.
1090.
1759Katame dhammā appaccayā? Asaṅkhatā dhātu—  ime dhammā appaccayā.
