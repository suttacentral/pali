2.3.2.10.2 Cittaduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Dukanikkhepa
Mahantaraduka
Cittaduka
1193.
1889Katame dhammā cittā? Cakkhuviññāṇaṃ, sotaviññāṇaṃ, ghānaviññāṇaṃ, jivhāviññāṇaṃ, kāyaviññāṇaṃ, manodhātu, manoviññāṇadhātu—  ime dhammā cittā.
1194.
1890Katame dhammā no cittā? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho, sabbañca rūpaṃ, asaṅkhatā ca dhātu—  ime dhammā no cittā.
