2.2.2.10 Dasaka
Niddesa
Rūpakaṇḍa
Mātikā
Dasaka
592.
1228Dasavidhena rūpasaṅgaho— 
1229Cakkhundriyaṃ, sotindriyaṃ, ghānindriyaṃ, jivhindriyaṃ kāyindriyaṃ, itthindriyaṃ, purisindriyaṃ, jīvitindriyaṃ, na indriyaṃ rūpaṃ atthi sappaṭighaṃ, atthi appaṭighaṃ.
1230Evaṃ dasavidhena rūpasaṅgaho.
Dasakaṃ.
