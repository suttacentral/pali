2.4.2.5.3 Ganthasampayuttaduka
Niddesa
Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa
Dukaatthuddhāra
Ganthagocchaka
Ganthasampayuttaduka
1493.
2204Katame dhammā ganthasampayuttā? Cattāro diṭṭhigatasampayuttacittuppādā, cattāro diṭṭhigatavippayuttalobhasahagatacittuppādā, etthuppannaṃ lobhaṃ ṭhapetvā, dve domanassasahagatacittuppādā, etthuppannaṃ paṭighaṃ ṭhapetvā—  ime dhammā ganthasampayuttā.
1494.
2205Katame dhammā ganthavippayuttā? Catūsu diṭṭhigatavippayuttalobhasahagatesu cittuppādesu uppanno lobho, dvīsu domanassasahagatesu cittuppādesu uppannaṃ paṭighaṃ, vicikicchāsahagato cittuppādo, uddhaccasahagato cittuppādo, catūsu bhūmīsu kusalaṃ, catūsu bhūmīsu vipāko, tīsu bhūmīsu kiriyābyākataṃ, rūpañca, nibbānañca—  ime dhammā ganthavippayuttā.
