2.2.3.6 Chakka
Niddesa
Rūpakaṇḍa
Rūpavibhatti
Chakka
972.
1630Rūpāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, saddāyatanaṃ sotaviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, gandhāyatanaṃ ghānaviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, rasāyatanaṃ jivhāviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ kāyaviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, sabbaṃ rūpaṃ manoviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ.
1631Evaṃ chabbidhena rūpasaṅgaho.
Chakkaṃ.
