2.1.2.14 Dve ārammaṇāni
Niddesa
Cittuppādakaṇḍa
Rūpāvacarakusala

667Dve ārammaṇāni

232.
668Katame dhammā kusalā? Yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti ajjhattaṃ arūpasaññī bahiddhā rūpāni passati appamāṇāni, tāni abhibhuyya jānāmi passāmīti vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati parittaṃ appamāṇārammaṇaṃ, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti…pe…  avikkhepo hoti…pe…  ime dhammā kusalā.
233.
