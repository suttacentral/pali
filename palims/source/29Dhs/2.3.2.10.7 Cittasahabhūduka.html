2.3.2.10.7 Cittasahabhūduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Dukanikkhepa
Mahantaraduka
Cittasahabhūduka
1203.
1899Katame dhammā cittasahabhuno? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho, kāyaviññatti, vacīviññatti—  ime dhammā cittasahabhuno.
1204.
1900Katame dhammā no cittasahabhuno? Cittañca, avasesañca rūpaṃ, asaṅkhatā ca dhātu—  ime dhammā no cittasahabhuno.
