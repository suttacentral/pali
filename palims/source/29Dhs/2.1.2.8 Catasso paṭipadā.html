Niddesa
Cittuppādakaṇḍa
Rūpāvacarakusala

630Catasso paṭipadā

206.
631Katame dhammā kusalā? Yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti ajjhattaṃ arūpasaññī bahiddhā rūpāni passati parittāni, tāni abhibhuyya jānāmi passāmīti vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati dukkhapaṭipadaṃ dandhābhiññaṃ, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti…pe…  avikkhepo hoti…pe…  ime dhammā kusalā.
