2.2.2.9 Navaka
Niddesa
Rūpakaṇḍa
Mātikā
Navaka
591.
1225Navavidhena rūpasaṅgaho— 
1226Cakkhundriyaṃ, sotindriyaṃ, ghānindriyaṃ, jivhindriyaṃ, kāyindriyaṃ, itthindriyaṃ, purisindriyaṃ, jīvitindriyaṃ, yañca rūpaṃ na indriyaṃ.
1227Evaṃ navavidhena rūpasaṅgaho.
Navakaṃ.
