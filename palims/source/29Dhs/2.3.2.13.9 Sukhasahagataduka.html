2.3.2.13.9 Sukhasahagataduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Dukanikkhepa
Piṭṭhiduka
Sukhasahagataduka
1283.
1984Katame dhammā sukhasahagatā? Sukhabhūmiyaṃ kāmāvacare rūpāvacare apariyāpanne, sukhaṃ ṭhapetvā, taṃsampayutto saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho, viññāṇakkhandho—  ime dhammā sukhasahagatā.
1284.
1985Katame dhammā na sukhasahagatā? Na sukhabhūmiyaṃ kāmāvacare rūpāvacare arūpāvacare apariyāpanne; vedanākkhandho…pe…  viññāṇakkhandho; sukhañca, sabbañca rūpaṃ, asaṅkhatā ca dhātu—  ime dhammā na sukhasahagatā.
