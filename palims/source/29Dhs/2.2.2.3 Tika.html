Yaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ ajjhattikaṃ, taṃ na saddāyatanaṃ…pe…  na gandhāyatanaṃ…pe…  na rasāyatanaṃ…pe…  na phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ. Yaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ bāhiraṃ, taṃ atthi phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ, atthi na phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ.
