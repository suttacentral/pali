47 Sāsavaduka, Sanidassanattika
Sāsavaduka, Sanidassanattika
95.
249Nasāsavaṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sāsavo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ.
250Naanāsavaṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sāsavo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
251(Nasāsavaṃ naanidassanasappaṭighamūlassa purimanayena tīṇi pañhā.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  aññamaññe ekaṃ…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
252Naanāsavaṃ naanidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sāsavo anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.
