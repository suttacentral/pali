2.1.11 Abhabbasutta
Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka
Khemavagga
Abhabbasutta
62.
430“Nava, bhikkhave, dhamme appahāya abhabbo arahattaṃ sacchikātuṃ. Katame nava? Rāgaṃ, dosaṃ, mohaṃ, kodhaṃ, upanāhaṃ, makkhaṃ, paḷāsaṃ, issaṃ, macchariyaṃ—  ime kho, bhikkhave, nava dhamme appahāya abhabbo arahattaṃ sacchikātuṃ.
431Nava, bhikkhave, dhamme pahāya bhabbo arahattaṃ sacchikātuṃ. Katame nava? Rāgaṃ, dosaṃ, mohaṃ, kodhaṃ, upanāhaṃ, makkhaṃ, paḷāsaṃ, issaṃ, macchariyaṃ—  ime kho, bhikkhave, nava dhamme pahāya bhabbo arahattaṃ sacchikātun”ti.
432Ekādasamaṃ.
433Khemavaggo paṭhamo.
434Tassuddānaṃ
435
Khemo ca amatañceva,
Abhayaṃ passaddhiyena ca;
Nirodho anupubbo ca,
Dhammaṃ pahāya bhabbena cāti.
