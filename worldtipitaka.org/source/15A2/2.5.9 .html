Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka
Bālavagga
107.
190“Dveme, bhikkhave, bālā. Katame dve? Yo ca avinaye vinayasaññī, yo ca vinaye avinayasaññī. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve bālā”ti.
