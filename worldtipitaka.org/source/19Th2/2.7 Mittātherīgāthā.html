2.7 Mittātherīgāthā
Dukanipāta
Mittātherīgāthā
31.
57
“Cātuddasiṃ pañcadasiṃ,
yā ca pakkhassa aṭṭhamī;
Pāṭihāriyapakkhañca,
aṭṭhaṅgasusamāgataṃ.
32.
58
Uposathaṃ upāgacchiṃ,
devakāyābhinandinī;
Sājja ekena bhattena,
muṇḍā saṅghāṭipārutā;
Devakāyaṃ na patthehaṃ,
vineyya hadaye daran”ti.
59…  Mittā therī… .
