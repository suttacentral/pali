3.5.8 Catutthakhatasutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Maṅgalavagga
Catutthakhatasutta
154.
1084“Tīhi, bhikkhave…pe…  asucinā kāyakammena, asucinā vacīkammena, asucinā manokammena…pe… .
1085Tīhi, bhikkhave…pe…  sucinā kāyakammena, sucinā vacīkammena, sucinā manokammena—  imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhi dhammehi samannāgato paṇḍito viyatto sappuriso akkhataṃ anupahataṃ attānaṃ pariharati, anavajjo ca hoti ananuvajjo ca viññūnaṃ, bahuñca puññaṃ pasavatī”ti.
1086Aṭṭhamaṃ.
