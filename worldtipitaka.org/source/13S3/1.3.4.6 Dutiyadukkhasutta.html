1.3.4.6 Dutiyadukkhasutta
Khandhasaṃyutta
Uparipaṇṇāsaka
Kukkuḷavagga
Dutiyadukkhasutta
141.
771Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Yaṃ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṃ; tatra vo rāgo pahātabbo…pe…  yaṃ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṃ; tatra vo rāgo pahātabbo”ti.
772Chaṭṭhaṃ.
