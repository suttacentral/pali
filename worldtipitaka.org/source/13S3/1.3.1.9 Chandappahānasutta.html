1.3.1.9 Chandappahānasutta
Khandhasaṃyutta
Uparipaṇṇāsaka
Antavagga
Chandappahānasutta
111.
674Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Rūpe, bhikkhave, yo chando yo rāgo yā nandī yā taṇhā, taṃ pajahatha. Evaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ pahīnaṃ bhavissati ucchinnamūlaṃ tālāvatthukataṃ anabhāvaṃkataṃ āyatiṃ anuppādadhammaṃ. Vedanāya…pe…  saññāya…  saṅkhāresu…  viññāṇe yo chando yo rāgo yā nandī yā taṇhā, taṃ pajahatha. Evaṃ taṃ viññāṇaṃ pahīnaṃ bhavissati ucchinnamūlaṃ tālāvatthukataṃ anabhāvaṃkataṃ āyatiṃ anuppādadhamman”ti.
675Navamaṃ.
