7.1.6 Viññāṇañcāyatanasutta
Sāriputtasaṃyutta
Sāriputtavagga
Viññāṇañcāyatanasutta
337.
1132Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Addasā kho āyasmā ānando…pe…  “idhāhaṃ, āvuso, sabbaso ākāsānañcāyatanaṃ samatikkamma anantaṃ viññāṇanti viññāṇañcāyatanaṃ upasampajja viharāmi…pe…  vuṭṭhitoti vā”ti.
1133Chaṭṭhaṃ.
