1.3.4.13 Dukkhānupassīsutta
Khandhasaṃyutta
Uparipaṇṇāsaka
Kukkuḷavagga
Dukkhānupassīsutta
148.
785Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Saddhāpabbajitassa, bhikkhave, kulaputtassa ayamanudhammo hoti—  yaṃ rūpe dukkhānupassī vihareyya. Vedanāya…  saññāya…  saṅkhāresu…  viññāṇe dukkhānupassī vihareyya…pe…  ‘parimuccati dukkhasmā’ti vadāmī”ti.
786Terasamaṃ.
