1.1.2.2 Dukkhasutta
Khandhasaṃyutta
Mūlapaṇṇāsaka
Aniccavagga
Dukkhasutta
13.
100Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Rūpaṃ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṃ, vedanā dukkhā, saññā dukkhā, saṅkhārā dukkhā, viññāṇaṃ dukkhaṃ. Evaṃ passaṃ…pe…  nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.
101Dutiyaṃ.
