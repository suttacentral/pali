3.1.15 Hotitathāgatosutta
Diṭṭhisaṃyutta
Sotāpattivagga
Hotitathāgatosutta
220.
965Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Kismiṃ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṃ upādāya, kiṃ abhinivissa evaṃ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘hoti tathāgato paraṃ maraṇā’”ti? “Bhagavaṃmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā…pe…  niyato sambodhiparāyano”ti.
966Pannarasamaṃ.
