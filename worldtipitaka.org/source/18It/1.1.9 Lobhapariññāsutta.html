1.1.9 Lobhapariññāsutta
Ekakanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Lobhapariññāsutta
9.
43Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ— 
44“Lobhaṃ, bhikkhave, anabhijānaṃ aparijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ avirājayaṃ appajahaṃ abhabbo dukkhakkhayāya. Lobhañca kho, bhikkhave, abhijānaṃ parijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ virājayaṃ pajahaṃ bhabbo dukkhakkhayāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati— 
45
“Yena lobhena luddhāse,
sattā gacchanti duggatiṃ;
Taṃ lobhaṃ sammadaññāya,
pajahanti vipassino;
Pahāya na punāyanti,
imaṃ lokaṃ kudācanan”ti.
46Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
47Navamaṃ.
