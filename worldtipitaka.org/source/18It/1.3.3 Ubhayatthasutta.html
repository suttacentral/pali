1.3.3 Ubhayatthasutta
Ekakanipāta
Tatiyavagga
Ubhayatthasutta
23.
128Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ— 
129“Ekadhammo, bhikkhave, bhāvito bahulīkato ubho atthe samadhigayha tiṭṭhati—  diṭṭhadhammikañceva atthaṃ samparāyikañca. Katamo ekadhammo? Appamādo kusalesu dhammesu. Ayaṃ kho, bhikkhave, ekadhammo bhāvito bahulīkato ubho atthe samadhigayha tiṭṭhati—  diṭṭhadhammikañceva atthaṃ samparāyikañcā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati— 
130
“Appamādaṃ pasaṃsanti,
puññakiriyāsu paṇḍitā;
Appamatto ubho atthe,
adhigaṇhāti paṇḍito.
131
Diṭṭhe dhamme ca yo attho,
yo cattho samparāyiko;
Atthābhisamayā dhīro,
paṇḍitoti pavuccatī”ti.
132Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
133Tatiyaṃ.
