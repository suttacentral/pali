1.1.2 Dosasutta
Ekakanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Dosasutta
2.
7Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ— 
8“Ekadhammaṃ, bhikkhave, pajahatha; ahaṃ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāya. Katamaṃ ekadhammaṃ? Dosaṃ, bhikkhave, ekadhammaṃ pajahatha; ahaṃ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati— 
9
“Yena dosena duṭṭhāse,
sattā gacchanti duggatiṃ;
Taṃ dosaṃ sammadaññāya,
pajahanti vipassino;
Pahāya na punāyanti,
imaṃ lokaṃ kudācanan”ti.
10Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
11Dutiyaṃ.
