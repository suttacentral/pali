1.1.3 Mohasutta
Ekakanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Mohasutta
3.
12Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ— 
13“Ekadhammaṃ, bhikkhave, pajahatha; ahaṃ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāya. Katamaṃ ekadhammaṃ? Mohaṃ, bhikkhave, ekadhammaṃ pajahatha; ahaṃ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati— 
14
“Yena mohena mūḷhāse,
sattā gacchanti duggatiṃ;
Taṃ mohaṃ sammadaññāya,
pajahanti vipassino;
Pahāya na punāyanti,
imaṃ lokaṃ kudācanan”ti.
15Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
16Tatiyaṃ.
