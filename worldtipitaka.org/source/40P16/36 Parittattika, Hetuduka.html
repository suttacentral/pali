36 Parittattika, Hetuduka
Parittattika, Hetuduka
78.
205Parittaṃ hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca namahaggato nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā terasa, ārammaṇe nava…pe…  aññamaññe ekādasa…pe…  avigate terasa.
206Parittaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naparitto nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cuddasa, ārammaṇe cuddasa, adhipatiyā nava…pe…  avigate cuddasa.
