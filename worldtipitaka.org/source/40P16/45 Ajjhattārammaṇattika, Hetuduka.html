Ajjhattārammaṇattika, Hetuduka
87.
221Ajjhattārammaṇaṃ hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naajjhattārammaṇo nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha…pe…  purejāte āsevane dve…pe…  avigate cha.
222Ajjhattārammaṇaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nabahiddhārammaṇo nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve…pe…  avigate dve.
