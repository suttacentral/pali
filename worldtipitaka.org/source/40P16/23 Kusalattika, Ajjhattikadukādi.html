23 Kusalattika, Ajjhattikadukādi
Kusalattika, Ajjhattikadukādi
43.
121Kusalaṃ ajjhattikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ, cittadukasadisaṃ.)
122Kusalaṃ bāhiraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naakusalo nabāhiro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
123Hetuyā nava…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate nava.
44.
124Abyākataṃ upādā dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo naupādā dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
125Kusalaṃ noupādā dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo nanoupādā dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pannarasa, adhipatiyā pannarasa…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate pannarasa.
45.
126Abyākataṃ upādinnaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo naupādinno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Abyākataṃ upādinnaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naakusalo naupādinno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Abyākataṃ upādinnaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo naupādinno ca naakusalo naupādinno ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
127Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
