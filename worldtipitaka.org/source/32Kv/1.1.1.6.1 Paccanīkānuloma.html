1.1.1.6.1 Paccanīkānuloma
Mahāpaṇṇāsaka
Paṭhamavagga
Puggalakathā
Kālasaccikaṭṭha
Paccanīkānuloma
15.
56× Puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenāti? Āmantā. Sabbadā puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenāti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe.
57Ājānāhi niggahaṃ. Hañci puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthena, tena vata re vattabbe—  “sabbadā puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā”ti. Yaṃ tattha vadesi—  “vattabbe kho—  ‘puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthena’, no ca vattabbe—  ‘sabbadā puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā’”ti micchā.
58No ce pana vattabbe—  “sabbadā puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā”ti, no ca vata re vattabbe—  “puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā”ti. Yaṃ tattha vadesi—  “vattabbe kho—  ‘puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthena’, no ca vattabbe—  ‘sabbadā puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā’”ti micchā…pe… .
59Sattamo niggaho.
