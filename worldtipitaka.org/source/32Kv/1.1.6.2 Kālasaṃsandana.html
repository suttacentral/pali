624๐ Atītaṃ atthīti? Āmantā. Nanu atītaṃ niruddhaṃ vigataṃ vipariṇataṃ atthaṅgataṃ abbhatthaṅgatanti? Āmantā. Hañci atītaṃ niruddhaṃ vigataṃ vipariṇataṃ atthaṅgataṃ abbhatthaṅgataṃ, no ca vata re vattabbe—  “atītaṃ atthī”ti.
