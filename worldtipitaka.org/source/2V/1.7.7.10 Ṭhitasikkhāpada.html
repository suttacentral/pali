1.7.7.10 Ṭhitasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Pādukavagga
Ṭhitasikkhāpada
648.
2057Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū ṭhitā nisinnassa dhammaṃ desenti…pe… .
2058“Na ṭhito nisinnassa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (70:215)
2059Na ṭhitena nisinnassa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca ṭhito nisinnassa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
2060Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.
2061Dasamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
