1.7.7.4 Sayanasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Pādukavagga
Sayanasikkhāpada
641.
2018Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū sayanagatassa dhammaṃ desenti…pe… .
2019“Na sayanagatassa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (64:209)
2020Na sayanagatassa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca antamaso chamāyampi nipannassa sayanagatassa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
2021Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.
2022Catutthasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
