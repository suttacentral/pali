1.7.3.8 Pattasaññīpaṭiggahaṇasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Khambhakatavagga
Pattasaññīpaṭiggahaṇasikkhāpada
603.
1820Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū tahaṃ tahaṃ olokentā piṇḍapātaṃ paṭiggaṇhanti, ākirantepi atikkantepi na jānanti…pe… .
1821“Pattasaññī piṇḍapātaṃ paṭiggahessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (28:173)
1822Pattasaññinā piṇḍapāto paṭiggahetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca tahaṃ tahaṃ olokento piṇḍapātaṃ paṭiggaṇhāti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1823Anāpatti— asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.
1824Aṭṭhamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
