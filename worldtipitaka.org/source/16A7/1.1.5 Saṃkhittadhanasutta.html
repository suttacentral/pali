1.1.5 Saṃkhittadhanasutta
Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka
Dhanavagga
Saṃkhittadhanasutta
5.
25“Sattimāni, bhikkhave, dhanāni. Katamāni satta? Saddhādhanaṃ, sīladhanaṃ, hirīdhanaṃ, ottappadhanaṃ, sutadhanaṃ, cāgadhanaṃ, paññādhanaṃ. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, satta dhanānīti.
26
Saddhādhanaṃ sīladhanaṃ,
hirī ottappiyaṃ dhanaṃ;
Sutadhanañca cāgo ca,
paññā ve sattamaṃ dhanaṃ.
27
Yassa ete dhanā atthi,
itthiyā purisassa vā;
Adaliddoti taṃ āhu,
amoghaṃ tassa jīvitaṃ.
28
Tasmā saddhañca sīlañca,
Pasādaṃ dhammadassanaṃ;
Anuyuñjetha medhāvī,
Saraṃ buddhāna sāsanan”ti.
29Pañcamaṃ.
