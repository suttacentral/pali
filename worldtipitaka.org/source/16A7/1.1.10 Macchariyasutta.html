1.1.10 Macchariyasutta
Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka
Dhanavagga
Macchariyasutta
10.
53“Sattimāni, bhikkhave, saṃyojanāni. Katamāni satta? Anunayasaṃyojanaṃ, paṭighasaṃyojanaṃ, diṭṭhisaṃyojanaṃ, vicikicchāsaṃyojanaṃ, mānasaṃyojanaṃ, issāsaṃyojanaṃ, macchariyasaṃyojanaṃ. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, satta saṃyojanānī”ti.
54Dasamaṃ.
55Dhanavaggo paṭhamo.
56Tassuddānaṃ
57
Dve piyāni balaṃ dhanaṃ,
Saṃkhittañceva vitthataṃ;
Uggaṃ saṃyojanañceva,
Pahānaṃ macchariyena cāti.
