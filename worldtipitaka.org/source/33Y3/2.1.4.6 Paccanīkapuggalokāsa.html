730»  Yassa yattha cakkhāyatanaṃ nuppajjati tassa tattha sotāyatanaṃ nuppajjitthāti?
731Pañcavokārā cavantānaṃ acakkhukānaṃ kāmāvacaraṃ upapajjantānaṃ tesaṃ tattha cakkhāyatanaṃ nuppajjati, no ca tesaṃ tattha sotāyatanaṃ nuppajjittha. Suddhāvāse parinibbantānaṃ asaññasattānaṃ arūpānaṃ tesaṃ tattha cakkhāyatanañca nuppajjati sotāyatanañca nuppajjittha.
