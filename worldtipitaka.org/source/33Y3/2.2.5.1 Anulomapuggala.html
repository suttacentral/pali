166.
1347»  Yassa cakkhāyatanaṃ nirujjhati tassa sotāyatanaṃ nirujjhissatīti?
1348Pañcavokāre parinibbantānaṃ ye ca arūpaṃ upapajjitvā parinibbāyissanti tesaṃ cavantānaṃ tesaṃ cakkhāyatanaṃ nirujjhati, no ca tesaṃ sotāyatanaṃ nirujjhissati. Itaresaṃ sacakkhukānaṃ cavantānaṃ tesaṃ cakkhāyatanañca nirujjhati sotāyatanañca nirujjhissati.
1349«  Yassa vā pana sotāyatanaṃ nirujjhissati tassa cakkhāyatanaṃ nirujjhatīti?
1350Sabbesaṃ upapajjantānaṃ acakkhukānaṃ cavantānaṃ tesaṃ sotāyatanaṃ nirujjhissati, no ca tesaṃ cakkhāyatanaṃ nirujjhati. Sacakkhukānaṃ cavantānaṃ tesaṃ sotāyatanañca nirujjhissati cakkhāyatanañca nirujjhati.
1351»  Yassa cakkhāyatanaṃ nirujjhati tassa ghānāyatanaṃ nirujjhissatīti?
1352Pañcavokāre parinibbantānaṃ ye ca rūpāvacaraṃ arūpāvacaraṃ upapajjitvā parinibbāyissanti tesaṃ cavantānaṃ tesaṃ cakkhāyatanaṃ nirujjhati, no ca tesaṃ ghānāyatanaṃ nirujjhissati. Itaresaṃ sacakkhukānaṃ cavantānaṃ tesaṃ cakkhāyatanañca nirujjhati ghānāyatanañca nirujjhissati.
