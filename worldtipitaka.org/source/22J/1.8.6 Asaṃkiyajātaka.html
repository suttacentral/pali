1.8.6 Asaṃkiyajātaka
Ekakanipāta
Varuṇavagga
Asaṃkiyajātaka
76.
167
“Asaṅkiyomhi gāmamhi,
araññe natthi me bhayaṃ;
Ujuṃ maggaṃ samārūḷho,
mettāya karuṇāya cā”ti.
168Asaṃkiyajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
