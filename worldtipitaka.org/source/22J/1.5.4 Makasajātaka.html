1.5.4 Makasajātaka
Ekakanipāta
Atthakāmavagga
Makasajātaka
44.
96
“Seyyo amitto matiyā upeto,
Na tveva mitto mativippahīno;
Makasaṃ vadhissanti hi eḷamūgo,
Putto pitu abbhidā uttamaṅgan”ti.
97Makasajātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
