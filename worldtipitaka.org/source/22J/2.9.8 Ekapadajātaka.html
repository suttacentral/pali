2.9.8 Ekapadajātaka
Dukanipāta
Upāhanavagga
Ekapadajātaka
175.
623
“Iṅgha ekapadaṃ tāta,
anekatthapadassitaṃ;
Kiñci saṅgāhikaṃ brūsi,
yenatthe sādhayemase”.
176.
624
“Dakkheyyekapadaṃ tāta,
anekatthapadassitaṃ;
Tañca sīlena saññuttaṃ,
khantiyā upapāditaṃ;
Alaṃ mitte sukhāpetuṃ,
amittānaṃ dukhāya cā”ti.
625Ekapadajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
