1.10.6 Telapattajātaka
Ekakanipāta
Littavagga
Telapattajātaka
96.
212
“Samatittikaṃ anavasekaṃ,
Telapattaṃ yathā parihareyya;
Evaṃ sacittamanurakkhe,
Patthayāno disaṃ agatapubban”ti.
213Telapattajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
