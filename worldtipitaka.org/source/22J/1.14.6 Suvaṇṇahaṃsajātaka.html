1.14.6 Suvaṇṇahaṃsajātaka
Ekakanipāta
Asampadānavagga
Suvaṇṇahaṃsajātaka
136.
301
“Yaṃ laddhaṃ tena tuṭṭhabbaṃ,
Atilobho hi pāpako;
Haṃsarājaṃ gahetvāna,
Suvaṇṇā parihāyathā”ti.
302Suvaṇṇahaṃsajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
