1.9.8 Sārambhajātaka
Ekakanipāta
Apāyimhavagga
Sārambhajātaka
88.
194
“Kalyāṇimeva muñceyya,
na hi muñceyya pāpikaṃ;
Mokkho kalyāṇiyā sādhu,
mutvā tappati pāpikan”ti.
195Sārambhajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
