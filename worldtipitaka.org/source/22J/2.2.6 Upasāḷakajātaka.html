2.2.6 Upasāḷakajātaka
Dukanipāta
Santhavavagga
Upasāḷakajātaka
31.
389
“Upasāḷakanāmāni,
sahassāni catuddasa;
Asmiṃ padese daḍḍhāni,
natthi loke anāmataṃ.
32.
390
Yamhi saccañca dhammo ca,
ahiṃsā saṃyamo damo;
Etaṃ ariyā sevanti,
etaṃ loke anāmatan”ti.
391Upasāḷakajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
