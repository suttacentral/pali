1.1.6 Devadhammajātaka
Ekakanipāta
Apaṇṇakavagga
Devadhammajātaka
6.
12
“Hiriottappasampannā,
sukkadhammasamāhitā;
Santo sappurisā loke,
devadhammāti vuccare”ti.
13Devadhammajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
