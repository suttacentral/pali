1.7.7 Ucchaṅgajātaka
Ekakanipāta
Itthivagga
Ucchaṅgajātaka
67.
147
“Ucchaṅge deva me putto,
Pathe dhāvantiyā pati;
Tañca desaṃ na passāmi,
Yato sodariyamānayeti.
148Ucchaṅgajātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
