1.14.9 Ubhatobhaṭṭhajātaka
Ekakanipāta
Asampadānavagga
Ubhatobhaṭṭhajātaka
139.
307
“Akkhī bhinnā paṭo naṭṭho,
sakhigehe ca bhaṇḍanaṃ;
Ubhato paduṭṭhā kammantā,
udakamhi thalamhi cā”ti.
308Ubhatobhaṭṭhajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
