1.8.3 Saccaṃkirajātaka
Ekakanipāta
Varuṇavagga
Saccaṃkirajātaka
73.
161
“Saccaṃ kirevamāhaṃsu,
narā ekacciyā idha;
Kaṭṭhaṃ niplavitaṃ seyyo,
na tvevekacciyo naro”ti.
162Saccaṃkirajātakaṃ tatiyaṃ.
