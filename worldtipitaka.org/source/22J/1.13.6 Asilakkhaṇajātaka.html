1.13.6 Asilakkhaṇajātaka
Ekakanipāta
Kusanāḷivagga
Asilakkhaṇajātaka
126.
279
“Tadevekassa kalyāṇaṃ,
tadevekassa pāpakaṃ;
Tasmā sabbaṃ na kalyāṇaṃ,
sabbaṃ vāpi na pāpakan”ti.
280Asilakkhaṇajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
