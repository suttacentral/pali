1.1.4 Cūḷaseṭṭhijātaka
Ekakanipāta
Apaṇṇakavagga
Cūḷaseṭṭhijātaka
4.
8
“Appakenapi medhāvī,
pābhatena vicakkhaṇo;
Samuṭṭhāpeti attānaṃ,
aṇuṃ aggiṃva sandhaman”ti.
9Cūḷaseṭṭhijātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
