1.14.8 Godhajātaka
Ekakanipāta
Asampadānavagga
Godhajātaka
138.
305
“Kiṃ te jaṭāhi dummedha,
kiṃ te ajinasāṭiyā;
Abbhantaraṃ te gahanaṃ,
bāhiraṃ parimajjasī”ti.
306Godhajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
