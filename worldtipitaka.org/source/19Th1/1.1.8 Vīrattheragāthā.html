1.1.8 Vīrattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Vīrattheragāthā
8.
19
“Yo duddamiyo damena danto,
Vīro santusito vitiṇṇakaṅkho;
Vijitāvī apetalomahaṃso,
Vīro so parinibbuto ṭhitatto”ti.
20Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā vīro thero gāthaṃ abhāsitthāti.
