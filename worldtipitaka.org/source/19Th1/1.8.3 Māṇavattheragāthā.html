1.8.3 Māṇavattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Aṭṭhamavagga
Māṇavattheragāthā
73.
165
“Jiṇṇañca disvā dukhitañca byādhitaṃ,
Matañca disvā gatamāyusaṅkhayaṃ;
Tato ahaṃ nikkhamitūna pabbajiṃ,
Pahāya kāmāni manoramānī”ti.
