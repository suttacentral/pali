1.5.5 Ramaṇīyavihārittheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Pañcamavagga
Ramaṇīyavihārittheragāthā
45.
103
“Yathāpi bhaddo ājañño,
khalitvā patitiṭṭhati;

sammāsambuddhasāvakan”ti.
