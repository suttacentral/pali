4.1.9 Candanattheragāthā
Catukkanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Candanattheragāthā
299.
543
“Jātarūpena sañchannā,
dāsīgaṇapurakkhatā;
Aṅkena puttamādāya,
bhariyā maṃ upāgami.
300.
544
Tañca disvāna āyantiṃ,
sakaputtassa mātaraṃ;
Alaṅkataṃ suvasanaṃ,
maccupāsaṃva oḍḍitaṃ.
301.
545
Tato me manasīkāro,
yoniso udapajjatha;
Ādīnavo pāturahu,
nibbidā samatiṭṭhatha.
302.
546
Tato cittaṃ vimucci me,
passa dhammasudhammataṃ;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanan”ti.
547…  Candano thero… .
