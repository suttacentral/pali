2.1.1 Uttarattheragāthā
Dukanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Uttarattheragāthā
121.
274
“Natthi koci bhavo nicco,
saṅkhārā vāpi sassatā;
Uppajjanti ca te khandhā,
cavanti aparāparaṃ.
122.
275
Etamādīnavaṃ ñatvā,
bhavenamhi anatthiko;
Nissaṭo sabbakāmehi,
patto me āsavakkhayo”ti.
276Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā uttaro thero gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
