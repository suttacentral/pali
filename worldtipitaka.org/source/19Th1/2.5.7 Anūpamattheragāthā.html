2.5.7 Anūpamattheragāthā
Dukanipāta
Pañcamavagga
Anūpamattheragāthā
213.
420
“Nandamānāgataṃ cittaṃ,
sūlamāropamānakaṃ;
Tena teneva vajasi,
yena sūlaṃ kaliṅgaraṃ.
214.
421
Tāhaṃ cittakaliṃ brūmi,
taṃ brūmi cittadubbhakaṃ;
Satthā te dullabho laddho,
mānatthe maṃ niyojayī”ti.
422…  Anūpamo thero… .
