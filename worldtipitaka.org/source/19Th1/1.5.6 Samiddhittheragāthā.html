1.5.6 Samiddhittheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Pañcamavagga
Samiddhittheragāthā
46.
105
“Saddhāyāhaṃ pabbajito,
Agārasmānagāriyaṃ;
Sati paññā ca me vuḍḍhā,
Cittañca susamāhitaṃ;
Kāmaṃ karassu rūpāni,
Neva maṃ byādhayissasī”ti.
