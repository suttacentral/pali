1.1.2 Mahākoṭṭhikattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Mahākoṭṭhikattheragāthā
2.
7
“Upasanto uparato,
mantabhāṇī anuddhato;
Dhunāti pāpake dhamme,
dumapattaṃva māluto”ti.
8Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā mahākoṭṭhiko thero gāthaṃ abhāsitthāti.
