1.11.8 Dhammasavapituttheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Ekādasamavagga
Dhammasavapituttheragāthā
108.
241
“Sa vīsavassasatiko,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanan”ti.
