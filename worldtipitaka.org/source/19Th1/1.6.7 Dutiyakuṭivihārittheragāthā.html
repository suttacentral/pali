1.6.7 Dutiyakuṭivihārittheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Chaṭṭhavagga
Dutiyakuṭivihārittheragāthā
57.
129
“Ayamāhu purāṇiyā kuṭi,
Aññaṃ patthayase navaṃ kuṭiṃ;
Āsaṃ kuṭiyā virājaya,
Dukkhā bhikkhu puna navā kuṭī”ti.
