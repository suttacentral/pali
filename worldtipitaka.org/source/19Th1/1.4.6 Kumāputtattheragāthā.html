1.4.6 Kumāputtattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Catutthavagga
Kumāputtattheragāthā
36.
82
“Sādhu sutaṃ sādhu caritakaṃ,
Sādhu sadā aniketavihāro;
Atthapucchanaṃ padakkhiṇakammaṃ,
Etaṃ sāmaññamakiñcanassā”ti.
