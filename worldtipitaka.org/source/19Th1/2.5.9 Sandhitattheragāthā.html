2.5.9 Sandhitattheragāthā
Dukanipāta
Pañcamavagga
Sandhitattheragāthā
217.
426
“Assatthe haritobhāse,
saṃvirūḷhamhi pādape;
Ekaṃ buddhagataṃ saññaṃ,
alabhitthaṃ patissato.
218.
427
Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Tassā saññāya vāhasā,
patto me āsavakkhayo”ti.
428…  Sandhito thero… .
Vaggo pañcamo.
429Tassuddānaṃ
430
Kumārakassapo thero,
dhammapālo ca brahmāli;
Mogharājā visākho ca,
cūḷako ca anūpamo;
Vajjito sandhito thero,
kilesarajavāhanoti.
431Dukanipāto niṭṭhito.
432Tatruddānaṃ
433
Gāthādukanipātamhi,
navuti ceva aṭṭha ca;
Therā ekūnapaññāsaṃ,
bhāsitā nayakovidāti.
