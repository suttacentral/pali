1.8.5 Susāradattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Aṭṭhamavagga
Susāradattheragāthā
75.
169
“Sādhu suvihitāna dassanaṃ,
Kaṅkhā chijjati buddhi vaḍḍhati;
Bālampi karonti paṇḍitaṃ,
Tasmā sādhu sataṃ samāgamo”ti.
