22.6 Issāpakataitthivatthu
Nirayavagga
Issāpakataitthivatthu
314.
338
Akataṃ dukkaṭaṃ seyyo,
pacchā tappati dukkaṭaṃ;
Katañca sukataṃ seyyo,
yaṃ katvā nānutappati.
