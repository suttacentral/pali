3.4 Saṃgharakkhitabhāgineyyattheravatthu
Cittavagga
Saṃgharakkhitabhāgineyyattheravatthu
37.
40
Dūraṅgamaṃ ekacaraṃ,
asarīraṃ guhāsayaṃ;
Ye cittaṃ saṃyamissanti,
mokkhanti mārabandhanā.
