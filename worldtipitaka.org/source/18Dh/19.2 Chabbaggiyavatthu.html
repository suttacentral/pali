19.2 Chabbaggiyavatthu
Dhammaṭṭhavagga
Chabbaggiyavatthu
258.
279
Na tena paṇḍito hoti,
yāvatā bahu bhāsati;
Khemī averī abhayo,
“paṇḍito”ti pavuccati.
