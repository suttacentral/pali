5.7 Suppabuddhakuṭṭhivatthu
Bālavagga
Suppabuddhakuṭṭhivatthu
66.
71
Caranti bālā dummedhā,
amitteneva attanā;
Karontā pāpakaṃ kammaṃ,
yaṃ hoti kaṭukapphalaṃ.
