26.5 Ānandattheravatthu
Brāhmaṇavagga
Ānandattheravatthu
387.
415
Divā tapati ādicco,
rattimābhāti candimā;
Sannaddho khattiyo tapati,
jhāyī tapati brāhmaṇo;
Atha sabbamahorattiṃ,
buddho tapati tejasā.
