19.9 Bālisikavatthu
Dhammaṭṭhavagga
Bālisikavatthu
270.
291
Na tena ariyo hoti,
yena pāṇāni hiṃsati;
Ahiṃsā sabbapāṇānaṃ,
“ariyo”ti pavuccati.
