18.10 Meṇḍakaseṭṭhivatthu
Malavagga
Meṇḍakaseṭṭhivatthu
252.
272
Sudassaṃ vajjamaññesaṃ,
attano pana duddasaṃ;
Paresaṃ hi so vajjāni,
opunāti yathā bhusaṃ;
Attano pana chādeti,
kaliṃva kitavā saṭho.
