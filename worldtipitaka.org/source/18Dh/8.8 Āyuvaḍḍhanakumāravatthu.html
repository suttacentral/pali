8.8 Āyuvaḍḍhanakumāravatthu
Sahassavagga
Āyuvaḍḍhanakumāravatthu
109.
117
Abhivādanasīlissa,
niccaṃ vuḍḍhāpacāyino;
Cattāro dhammā vaḍḍhanti,
āyu vaṇṇo sukhaṃ balaṃ.
