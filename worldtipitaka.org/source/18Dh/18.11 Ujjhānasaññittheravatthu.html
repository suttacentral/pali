18.11 Ujjhānasaññittheravatthu
Malavagga
Ujjhānasaññittheravatthu
253.
273
Paravajjānupassissa,
niccaṃ ujjhānasaññino;
Āsavā tassa vaḍḍhanti,
ārā so āsavakkhayā.
