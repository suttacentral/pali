8.6 Sāriputtattherassa bhāgineyyavatthu
Sahassavagga
Sāriputtattherassa bhāgineyyavatthu
107.
115
Yo ca vassasataṃ jantu,
aggiṃ paricare vane;
Ekañca bhāvitattānaṃ,
muhuttamapi pūjaye;
Sāyeva pūjanā seyyo,
yañce vassasataṃ hutaṃ.
