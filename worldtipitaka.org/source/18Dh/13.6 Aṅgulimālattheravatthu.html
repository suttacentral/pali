13.6 Aṅgulimālattheravatthu
Lokavagga
Aṅgulimālattheravatthu
173.
186
Yassa pāpaṃ kataṃ kammaṃ,
kusalena pidhīyati;
Somaṃ lokaṃ pabhāseti,
abbhā muttova candimā.
