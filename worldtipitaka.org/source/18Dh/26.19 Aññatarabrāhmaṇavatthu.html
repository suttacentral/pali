26.19 Aññatarabrāhmaṇavatthu
Brāhmaṇavagga
Aññatarabrāhmaṇavatthu
402.
430
Yo dukkhassa pajānāti,
idheva khayamattano;
Pannabhāraṃ visaṃyuttaṃ,
tamahaṃ brūmi brāhmaṇaṃ.
