19.1 Vinicchayamahāmattavatthu
Dhammaṭṭhavagga
Vinicchayamahāmattavatthu
256.
277
Na tena hoti dhammaṭṭho,
yenatthaṃ sāhasā naye;
Yo ca atthaṃ anatthañca,
ubho niccheyya paṇḍito.
257.
278
Asāhasena dhammena,
samena nayatī pare;
Dhammassa gutto medhāvī,
“dhammaṭṭho”ti pavuccati.
