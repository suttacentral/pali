13.1 Daharabhikkhuvatthu
Lokavagga
Daharabhikkhuvatthu
167.
180
Hīnaṃ dhammaṃ na seveyya,
pamādena na saṃvase;
Micchādiṭṭhiṃ na seveyya,
na siyā lokavaḍḍhano.
