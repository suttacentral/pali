8.2 Bāhiyadārucīriyattheravatthu
Sahassavagga
Bāhiyadārucīriyattheravatthu
101.
109
Sahassamapi ce gāthā,
anatthapadasaṃhitā;
Ekaṃ gāthāpadaṃ seyyo,
yaṃ sutvā upasammati.
