5.11 Jambukattheravatthu
Bālavagga
Jambukattheravatthu
70.
75
Māse māse kusaggena,
bālo bhuñjeyya bhojanaṃ;
Na so saṅkhātadhammānaṃ,
kalaṃ agghati soḷasiṃ.
