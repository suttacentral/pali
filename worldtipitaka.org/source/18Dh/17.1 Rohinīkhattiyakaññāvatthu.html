17.1 Rohinīkhattiyakaññāvatthu
Kodhavagga
Rohinīkhattiyakaññāvatthu
221.
240
Kodhaṃ jahe vippajaheyya mānaṃ,
Saṃyojanaṃ sabbamatikkameyya;
Taṃ nāmarūpasmimasajjamānaṃ,
Akiñcanaṃ nānupatanti dukkhā.
