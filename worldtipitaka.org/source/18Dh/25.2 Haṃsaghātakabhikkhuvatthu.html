25.2 Haṃsaghātakabhikkhuvatthu
Bhikkhuvagga
Haṃsaghātakabhikkhuvatthu
362.
389
Hatthasaṃyato pādasaṃyato,
Vācāsaṃyato saṃyatuttamo;
Ajjhattarato samāhito,
Eko santusito tamāhu bhikkhuṃ.
