2.9 Nigamavāsitissattheravatthu
Appamādavagga
Nigamavāsitissattheravatthu
32.
34
Appamādarato bhikkhu,
pamāde bhayadassi vā;
Abhabbo parihānāya,
nibbānasseva santike.
35Appamādavaggo dutiyo.
