17.5 Buddhapitubrāhmaṇavatthu
Kodhavagga
Buddhapitubrāhmaṇavatthu
225.
244
Ahiṃsakā ye munayo,
niccaṃ kāyena saṃvutā;
Te yanti accutaṃ ṭhānaṃ,
yattha gantvā na socare.
