11.9 Mahādhanaseṭṭhiputtavatthu
Jarāvagga
Mahādhanaseṭṭhiputtavatthu
155.
166
Acaritvā brahmacariyaṃ,
aladdhā yobbane dhanaṃ;
Jiṇṇakoñcāva jhāyanti,
khīṇamaccheva pallale.
156.
167
Acaritvā brahmacariyaṃ,
aladdhā yobbane dhanaṃ;
Senti cāpātikhīṇāva,
purāṇāni anutthunaṃ.
168Jarāvaggo ekādasamo.
