8.5 Sāriputtattherassa mātulabrāhmaṇavatthu
Sahassavagga
Sāriputtattherassa mātulabrāhmaṇavatthu
106.
114
Māse māse sahassena,
yo yajetha sataṃ samaṃ;
Ekañca bhāvitattānaṃ,
muhuttamapi pūjaye;
Sāyeva pūjanā seyyo,
yañce vassasataṃ hutaṃ.
