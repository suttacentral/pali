11.5 Janapadakalyāṇīrūpanandātherīvatthu
Jarāvagga
Janapadakalyāṇīrūpanandātherīvatthu
150.
161
Aṭṭhīnaṃ nagaraṃ kataṃ,
maṃsalohitalepanaṃ;
Yattha jarā ca maccu ca,
māno makkho ca ohito.
