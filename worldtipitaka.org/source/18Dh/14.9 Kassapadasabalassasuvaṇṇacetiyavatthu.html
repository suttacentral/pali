14.9 Kassapadasabalassasuvaṇṇacetiyavatthu
Buddhavagga
Kassapadasabalassasuvaṇṇacetiyavatthu
195.
209
Pūjārahe pūjayato,
buddhe yadi va sāvake;
Papañcasamatikkante,
tiṇṇasokapariddave.
196.
210
Te tādise pūjayato,
nibbute akutobhaye;
Na sakkā puññaṃ saṅkhātuṃ,
imettamapi kenaci.
211Buddhavaggo cuddasamo.
212Paṭhamabhāṇavāraṃ.
