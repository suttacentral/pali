9.7 Mahādhanavāṇijavatthu
Pāpavagga
Mahādhanavāṇijavatthu
123.
132
Vāṇijova bhayaṃ maggaṃ,
appasattho mahaddhano;
Visaṃ jīvitukāmova,
pāpāni parivajjaye.
