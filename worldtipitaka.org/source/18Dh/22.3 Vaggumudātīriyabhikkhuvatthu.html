22.3 Vaggumudātīriyabhikkhuvatthu
Nirayavagga
Vaggumudātīriyabhikkhuvatthu
308.
332
Seyyo ayoguḷo bhutto,
tatto aggisikhūpamo;
Yañce bhuñjeyya dussīlo,
raṭṭhapiṇḍamasaññato.
