26.31 Sīvalittheravatthu
Brāhmaṇavagga
Sīvalittheravatthu
414.
442
Yomaṃ palipathaṃ duggaṃ,
saṃsāraṃ mohamaccagā;
Tiṇṇo pāraṅgato jhāyī,
anejo akathaṃkathī;
Anupādāya nibbuto,
tamahaṃ brūmi brāhmaṇaṃ.
