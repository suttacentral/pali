11.1 Visākhāyasahāyikānaṃvatthu
Jarāvagga
Visākhāyasahāyikānaṃvatthu
146.
157
Ko nu hāso kimānando,
niccaṃ pajjalite sati;
Andhakārena onaddhā,
padīpaṃ na gavesatha.
