Saraṇaduka
Paṭiccādivāra
Paccanīyuddhāra
60.
165Nasaraṇo dhammo saraṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo, upanissayapaccayena paccayo, purejātapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
166Nahetuyā satta, naārammaṇe satta…pe…  nasahajāte naaññamaññe nanissaye pañca…pe…  napurejāte cha…pe…  nasampayutte pañca, navippayutte cattāri…pe…  noavigate cattāri.
167(Yathā kusalattike pañhāvārassa anulomampi paccanīyampi anulomapaccanīyampi paccanīyānulomampi gaṇitaṃ, evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
