1.2.4.1 Vinītavatthuuddānagāthā
Mahāvibhaṅga
Saṃghādisesakaṇḍa
Attakāmapāricariyasikkhāpada
Vinītavatthuuddānagāthā
1211
Kathaṃ vañjhā labhe puttaṃ,
Piyā ca subhagā siyaṃ;
Kiṃ dajjaṃ kenupaṭṭheyyaṃ,
Kathaṃ gaccheyyaṃ suggatinti.
