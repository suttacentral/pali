Kusalattika, Parittārammaṇattika
24.
63Kusalaṃ parittārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo naparittārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava, adhipatiyā nava…pe…  aññamaññe tīṇi…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate nava.
64Kusalaṃ mahaggatārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo namahaggatārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava, adhipatiyā nava…pe…  avigate nava.
65Kusalaṃ appamāṇārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo naappamāṇārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
