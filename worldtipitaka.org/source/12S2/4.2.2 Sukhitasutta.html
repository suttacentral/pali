4.2.2 Sukhitasutta
Anamataggasaṃyutta
Dutiyavagga
Sukhitasutta
135.
866Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. “Anamataggoyaṃ, bhikkhave, saṃsāro…pe…  yaṃ, bhikkhave, passeyyātha sukhitaṃ susajjitaṃ, niṭṭhamettha gantabbaṃ—  ‘amhehipi evarūpaṃ paccanubhūtaṃ iminā dīghena addhunā’ti. Taṃ kissa hetu? Anamataggoyaṃ, bhikkhave, saṃsāro. Pubbā koṭi na paññāyati…pe…  alaṃ vimuccitun”ti.
867Dutiyaṃ.
