4.2.6 Bhātusutta
Anamataggasaṃyutta
Dutiyavagga
Bhātusutta
139.
877Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati…pe…  “na so, bhikkhave, satto sulabharūpo yo nabhātābhūtapubbo…pe…  alaṃ vimuccitun”ti.
878Chaṭṭhaṃ.
