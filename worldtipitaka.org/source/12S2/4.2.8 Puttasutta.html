4.2.8 Puttasutta
Anamataggasaṃyutta
Dutiyavagga
Puttasutta
141.
881Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati…pe…  “na so, bhikkhave, satto sulabharūpo yo naputtabhūtapubbo…pe…  alaṃ vimuccitun”ti.
882Aṭṭhamaṃ.
