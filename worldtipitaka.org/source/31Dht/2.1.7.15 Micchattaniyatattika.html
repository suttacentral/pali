2.1.7.15 Micchattaniyatattika
Niddesa
Paṭhamanaya saṅgahāsaṅgahapadaniddesa
Tika
Micchattaniyatattika
100.
146Aniyatā dhammā asaṅkhataṃ khandhato ṭhapetvā pañcahi khandhehi dvādasahāyatanehi aṭṭhārasahi dhātūhi saṅgahitā. Katihi asaṅgahitā? Na kehici khandhehi na kehici āyatanehi na kāhici dhātūhi asaṅgahitā.
