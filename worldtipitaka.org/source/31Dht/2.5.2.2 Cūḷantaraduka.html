2.5.2.2 Cūḷantaraduka
Niddesa
Pañcamanaya Asaṅgahitenaasaṅgahitapadaniddesa
Duka
Cūḷantaraduka
214.
288Appaccayehi dhammehi ye dhammā…  asaṅkhatehi dhammehi ye dhammā khandhasaṅgahena asaṅgahitā āyatanasaṅgahena asaṅgahitā dhātusaṅgahena asaṅgahitā, tehi dhammehi ye dhammā…pe…  te dhammā dvīhi khandhehi ekādasahāyatanehi sattarasahi dhātūhi asaṅgahitā.
215.
289Sanidassanehi dhammehi ye dhammā…  sappaṭighehi dhammehi ye dhammā khandhasaṅgahena asaṅgahitā āyatanasaṅgahena asaṅgahitā dhātusaṅgahena asaṅgahitā, tehi dhammehi ye dhammā…pe…  te dhammā catūhi khandhehi dvīhāyatanehi aṭṭhahi dhātūhi asaṅgahitā.
216.
290Rūpīhi dhammehi ye dhammā khandhasaṅgahena asaṅgahitā āyatanasaṅgahena asaṅgahitā dhātusaṅgahena asaṅgahitā, tehi dhammehi ye dhammā…pe…  te dhammā ekena khandhena ekenāyatanena sattahi dhātūhi asaṅgahitā.
217.
291Arūpīhi dhammehi ye dhammā…  lokuttarehi dhammehi ye dhammā khandhasaṅgahena asaṅgahitā āyatanasaṅgahena asaṅgahitā dhātusaṅgahena asaṅgahitā, tehi dhammehi ye dhammā…pe…  te dhammā ekena khandhena dasahāyatanehi dasahi dhātūhi asaṅgahitā.
