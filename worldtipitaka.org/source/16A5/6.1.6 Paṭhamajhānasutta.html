6.1.6 Paṭhamajhānasutta
Chaṭṭhapaṇṇāsaka
Upasampadāvagga
Paṭhamajhānasutta
256.
1361“Pañcime, bhikkhave, dhamme appahāya abhabbo paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharituṃ. Katame pañca? Āvāsamacchariyaṃ, kulamacchariyaṃ, lābhamacchariyaṃ, vaṇṇamacchariyaṃ, dhammamacchariyaṃ. Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca dhamme appahāya abhabbo paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharituṃ.
1362Pañcime, bhikkhave, dhamme pahāya bhabbo paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharituṃ. Katame pañca? Āvāsamacchariyaṃ, kulamacchariyaṃ, lābhamacchariyaṃ, vaṇṇamacchariyaṃ, dhammamacchariyaṃ. Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca dhamme pahāya bhabbo paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharitun”ti.
1363Chaṭṭhaṃ.
