5.1.7 Yāgusutta
Pañcamapaṇṇāsaka
Kimilavagga
Yāgusutta
207.
1208“Pañcime, bhikkhave, ānisaṃsā yāguyā. Katame pañca? Khuddaṃ paṭihanati, pipāsaṃ paṭivineti, vātaṃ anulometi, vatthiṃ sodheti, āmāvasesaṃ pāceti. Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca ānisaṃsā yāguyā”ti.
1209Sattamaṃ.
