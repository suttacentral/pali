2.4.2.10.10 Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhūduka
Niddesa
Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa
Dukaatthuddhāra
Mahantaraduka
Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhūduka
1543.
2254Katame dhammā cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuno? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  ime dhammā cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuno.
1544.
2255Katame dhammā no cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuno? Cittañca, rūpañca, nibbānañca—  ime dhammā no cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuno.
