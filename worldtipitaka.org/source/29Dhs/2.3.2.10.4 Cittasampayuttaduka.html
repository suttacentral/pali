2.3.2.10.4 Cittasampayuttaduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Dukanikkhepa
Mahantaraduka
Cittasampayuttaduka
1197.
1893Katame dhammā cittasampayuttā? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  ime dhammā cittasampayuttā.
1198.
1894Katame dhammā cittavippayuttā? Sabbañca rūpaṃ, asaṅkhatā ca dhātu—  ime dhammā cittavippayuttā. Cittaṃ na vattabbaṃ—  cittena sampayuttantipi, cittena vippayuttantipi.
