2.3.3.32 Samathavipassanāduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Suttantikadukanikkhepa
Samathavipassanāduka
1362.
2067Tattha katamo samatho? Yā cittassa ṭhiti…pe…  sammāsamādhi—  ayaṃ vuccati samatho.
1363.
2068Tattha katamā vipassanā? Yā paññā pajānanā…pe…  amoho dhammavicayo sammādiṭṭhi—  ayaṃ vuccati vipassanā.
