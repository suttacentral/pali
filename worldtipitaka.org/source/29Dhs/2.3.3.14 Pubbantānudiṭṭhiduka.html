2.3.3.14 Pubbantānudiṭṭhiduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Suttantikadukanikkhepa
Pubbantānudiṭṭhiduka
1326.
2031Tattha katamā pubbantānudiṭṭhi? Pubbantaṃ ārabbha yā uppajjati diṭṭhi diṭṭhigataṃ…pe…  vipariyāsaggāho—  ayaṃ vuccati pubbantānudiṭṭhi.
1327.
2032Tattha katamā aparantānudiṭṭhi? Aparantaṃ ārabbha yā uppajjati diṭṭhi diṭṭhigataṃ…pe…  vipariyāsaggāho—  ayaṃ vuccati aparantānudiṭṭhi.
