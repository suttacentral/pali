2.3.3.31 Paṭisaṅkhānabaladuka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Suttantikadukanikkhepa
Paṭisaṅkhānabaladuka
1360.
2065Tattha katamaṃ paṭisaṅkhānabalaṃ? Yā paññā pajānanā…pe…  amoho dhammavicayo sammādiṭṭhi—  idaṃ vuccati paṭisaṅkhānabalaṃ.
1361.
2066Tattha katamaṃ bhāvanābalaṃ? Yā kusalānaṃ dhammānaṃ āsevanā bhāvanā bahulīkammaṃ—  idaṃ vuccati bhāvanābalaṃ. Sattapi bojjhaṅgā bhāvanābalaṃ.
