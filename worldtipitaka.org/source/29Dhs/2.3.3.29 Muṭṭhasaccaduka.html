2.3.3.29 Muṭṭhasaccaduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Suttantikadukanikkhepa
Muṭṭhasaccaduka
1356.
2061Tattha katamaṃ muṭṭhasaccaṃ? Yā asati ananussati appaṭissati asati asaraṇatā adhāraṇatā pilāpanatā sammussanatā—  idaṃ vuccati muṭṭhasaccaṃ.
1357.
2062Tattha katamaṃ asampajaññaṃ? Yaṃ aññāṇaṃ adassanaṃ…pe…  avijjālaṅgī moho akusalamūlaṃ—  idaṃ vuccati asampajaññaṃ.
