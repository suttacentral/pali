2.3.3.6 Adhivacanaduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Suttantikadukanikkhepa
Adhivacanaduka
1313.
2015Katame dhammā adhivacanā? Yā tesaṃ tesaṃ dhammānaṃ saṅkhā samaññā paññatti vohāro nāmaṃ nāmakammaṃ nāmadheyyaṃ nirutti byañjanaṃ abhilāpo—  ime dhammā adhivacanā.
2016Sabbeva dhammā adhivacanapathā.
