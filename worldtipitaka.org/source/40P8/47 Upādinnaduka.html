47 Upādinnaduka
Upādinnaduka
66.
203Naupādinnaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naupādinno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)
204Naanupādinnaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanupādinno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naanupādinnaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naupādinno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naanupādinnaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naupādinno ca naanupādinno ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)
205Naupādinnañca naanupādinnañca dhammaṃ paṭicca naupādinno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
206Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve, adhipatiyā ekaṃ…pe…  āsevane ekaṃ…pe…  avigate pañca. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)
207Mahantaradukaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
