145Ganthavippayuttaṃ naganthaniyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ganthavippayutto naganthaniyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Ganthavippayuttaṃ naganthaniyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ganthavippayutto naganthaniyo ca ganthavippayutto naaganthaniyo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)
146Ganthavippayuttaṃ naaganthaniyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ganthavippayutto naaganthaniyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)
147Ganthavippayuttaṃ naganthaniyañca ganthavippayuttaṃ naaganthaniyañca dhammaṃ paṭicca ganthavippayutto naaganthaniyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
148Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve…pe…  avigate pañca. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)
149Ganthagocchakaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
