28 Ganthasampayuttaduka
Ganthasampayuttaduka
42.
133Naganthasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naganthasampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naganthasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naganthavippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naganthasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naganthasampayutto ca naganthavippayutto ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)
134Naganthavippayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naganthavippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naganthavippayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naganthasampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naganthavippayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naganthasampayutto ca naganthavippayutto ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)
135Naganthasampayuttañca naganthavippayuttañca dhammaṃ paṭicca naganthasampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naganthasampayuttañca naganthavippayuttañca dhammaṃ paṭicca naganthavippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naganthasampayuttañca naganthavippayuttañca dhammaṃ paṭicca naganthasampayutto ca naganthavippayutto ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
136Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe cha, adhipatiyā nava…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate nava. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)
