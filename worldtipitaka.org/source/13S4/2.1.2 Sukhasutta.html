2.1.2 Sukhasutta
Vedanāsaṃyutta
Sagāthāvagga
Sukhasutta
250.
1098“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, vedanā. Katamā tisso? Sukhā vedanā, dukkhā vedanā, adukkhamasukhā vedanā—  imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso vedanāti.
1099
Sukhaṃ vā yadi vā dukkhaṃ,
adukkhamasukhaṃ saha;
Ajjhattañca bahiddhā ca,
yaṃ kiñci atthi veditaṃ.
1100
Etaṃ dukkhanti ñatvāna,
mosadhammaṃ palokinaṃ;
Phussa phussa vayaṃ passaṃ,
evaṃ tattha virajjatī”ti.
1101Dutiyaṃ.
