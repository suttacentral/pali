20.9 Kiṃkaṇikapupphiyattheraapadāna
Tamālapupphiyavagga
Kiṃkaṇikapupphiyattheraapadāna
47.
2728
“Sumaṅgaloti nāmena,
sayambhū aparājito;
Pavanā nikkhamitvāna,
nagaraṃ pāvisī jino.
48.
2729
Piṇḍacāraṃ caritvāna,
nikkhamma nagarā muni;
Katakiccova sambuddho,
so vasī vanamantare.
49.
2730
Kiṃkaṇipupphaṃ paggayha,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
sayambhussa mahesino.
50.
2731
Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
51.
2732
Chaḷāsītimhito kappe,
apilāsisanāmako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
52.
2733
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
2734Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kiṃkaṇikapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2735Kiṃkaṇikapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
