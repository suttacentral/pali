44.5 Ucchukhaṇḍikattheraapadāna
Ekavihārivagga
Ucchukhaṇḍikattheraapadāna
44.
5715
“Nagare bandhumatiyā,
dvārapālo ahosahaṃ;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
sabbadhammāna pāraguṃ.
45.
5716
Ucchukhaṇḍikamādāya,
buddhaseṭṭhassadāsahaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
vipassissa mahesino.
46.
5717
Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ ucchumadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ucchukhaṇḍassidaṃ phalaṃ.
47.
5718
Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
…pe… 
viharāmi anāsavo.
48.
5719
Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.
49.
5720
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
5721Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ucchukhaṇḍiko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
5722Ucchukhaṇḍikattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
