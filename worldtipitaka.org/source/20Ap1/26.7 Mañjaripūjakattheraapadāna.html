26.7 Mañjaripūjakattheraapadāna
Thomakavagga
Mañjaripūjakattheraapadāna
23.
3128
“Mañjarikaṃ karitvāna,
rathiyaṃ paṭipajjahaṃ;
Addasaṃ samaṇānaggaṃ,
bhikkhusaṃghapurakkhataṃ.
24.
3129
Pasannacitto sumano,
paramāya ca pītiyā;
Ubho hatthehi paggayha,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.
25.
3130
Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pupphapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
26.
3131
Ito tesattatikappe,
eko āsiṃ mahīpati;
Jotiyo nāma nāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
27.
3132
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
3133Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā mañjaripūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
3134Mañjaripūjakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
