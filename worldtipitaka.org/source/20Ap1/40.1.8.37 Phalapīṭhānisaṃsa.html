40.1.8.37 Phalapīṭhānisaṃsa
Pilindavacchavagga
Pilindavacchattheraapadāna
Dānānisaṃsakathā
Phalapīṭhānisaṃsa
187.
4484
Phalapīṭhe jine datvā,
saṃghe gaṇavaruttame;
Dvānisaṃse anubhomi,
kammānucchavike mama.
188.
4485
Soṇṇamaye maṇimaye,
dantasāramaye bahū;
Pallaṅkaseṭṭhe vindāmi,
phalapīṭhassidaṃ phalaṃ.
