40.1.8.2 Dussānisaṃsa
Pilindavacchavagga
Pilindavacchattheraapadāna
Dānānisaṃsakathā
Dussānisaṃsa
94.
4391
Dussāni sugate datvā,
saṃghe gaṇavaruttame;
Aṭṭhānisaṃse anubhomi,
kammānucchavike mama.
95.
4392
Suvaṇṇavaṇṇo virajo,
sappabhāso patāpavā;
Siniddhaṃ hoti me gattaṃ,
bhave saṃsarato mama.
96.
4393
Dussasatasahassāni,
setā pītā ca lohitā;
Dhārenti matthake mayhaṃ,
dussadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.
97.
4394
Koseyyakambaliyāni,
khomakappāsikāni ca;
Sabbattha paṭilabhāmi,
tesaṃ nissandato ahaṃ.
