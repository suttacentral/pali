39.10.5
Avaṭaphalavagga
Pubbakammapilotikabuddhaapadāna
80.
4274
Purehaṃ dārako hutvā,
kīḷamāno mahāpathe;
Paccekabuddhaṃ disvāna,
magge sakalikaṃ khipiṃ.
81.
4275
Tena kammavipākena,
idha pacchimake bhave;
Vadhatthaṃ maṃ devadatto,
abhimāre payojayi.
