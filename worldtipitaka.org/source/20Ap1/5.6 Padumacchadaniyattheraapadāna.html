5.6 Padumacchadaniyattheraapadāna
Upālivagga
Padumacchadaniyattheraapadāna
83.
1301
“Nibbute lokanāthamhi,
vipassimhaggapuggale;
Suphullapadumaṃ gayha,
citamāropayiṃ ahaṃ.
84.
1302
Āropite ca citake,
vehāsaṃ nabhamuggami;
Ākāse chadanaṃ katvā,
citakamhi adhārayi.
85.
1303
Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
86.
1304
Sattatālīsito kappe,
padumissaranāmako;
Cāturanto vijitāvī,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
87.
1305
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
1306Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā padumacchadaniyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
1307Padumacchadaniyattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
