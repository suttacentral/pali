15.9 Vaṭaṃsakiyattheraapadāna
Chattavagga
Vaṭaṃsakiyattheraapadāna
43.
2336
“Sumedho nāma nāmena,
sayambhū aparājito;
Vivekamanubrūhanto,
ajjhogāhi mahāvanaṃ.
44.
2337
Saḷalaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
ganthitvāna vaṭaṃsakaṃ;
Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,
sammukhā lokanāyakaṃ.
45.
2338
Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
46.
2339
Ūnavīse kappasate,
soḷasāsuṃ sunimmitā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.
47.
2340
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
2341Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā vaṭaṃsakiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2342Vaṭaṃsakiyattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
