32.1 Ārakkhadāyakattheraapadāna
Ārakkhadāyakavagga
Ārakkhadāyakattheraapadāna
1.
3506
“Dhammadassissa munino,
vati kārāpitā mayā;
Ārakkho ca mayā dinno,
dvipadindassa tādino.
2.
3507
Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Tena kammavisesena,
patto me āsavakkhayo.
3.
3508
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
3509Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ārakkhadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
3510Ārakkhadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
