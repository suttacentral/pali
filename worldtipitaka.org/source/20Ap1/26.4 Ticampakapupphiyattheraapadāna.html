26.4 Ticampakapupphiyattheraapadāna
Thomakavagga
Ticampakapupphiyattheraapadāna
13.
3112
“Himavantassāvidūre,
vikato nāma pabbato;
Tassa vemajjhe vasati,
samaṇo bhāvitindriyo.
14.
3113
Disvāna tassopasamaṃ,
vippasannena cetasā;
Tīṇi campakapupphāni,
gahetvāna samokiriṃ.
15.
3114
Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
16.
3115
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
3116Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ticampakapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
3117Ticampakapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
