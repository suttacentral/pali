39.10.11
Avaṭaphalavagga
Pubbakammapilotikabuddhaapadāna
91.
4285
Tikicchako ahaṃ āsiṃ,
seṭṭhiputtaṃ virecayiṃ;
Tena kammavipākena,
hoti pakkhandikā mama.
