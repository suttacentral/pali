10.9 Veyyāvaccakattheraapadāna
Sudhāvagga
Veyyāvaccakattheraapadāna
51.
1831
“Vipassissa bhagavato,
mahāpūgagaṇo ahu;
Veyyāvaccakaro āsiṃ,
sabbakiccesu vāvaṭo.
52.
1832
Deyyadhammo ca me natthi,
sugatassa mahesino;
Avandiṃ satthuno pāde,
vippasannena cetasā.
53.
1833
Ekanavutito kappe,
veyyāvaccaṃ akāsahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
veyyāvaccassidaṃ phalaṃ.
54.
1834
Ito ca aṭṭhame kappe,
rājā āsiṃ sucintito;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
55.
1835
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
1836Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā veyyāvaccako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
1837Veyyāvaccakattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
