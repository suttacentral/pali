40.1.8.27 Dhūmanettānisaṃsa
Pilindavacchavagga
Pilindavacchattheraapadāna
Dānānisaṃsakathā
Dhūmanettānisaṃsa
158.
4455
Dhūmanette jine datvā,
saṃghe gaṇavaruttame;
Tīṇānisaṃse anubhomi,
kammānucchavike mama.
159.
4456
Sati me ujukā hoti,
susambandhā ca nhāravo;
Labhāmi dibbanayanaṃ,
tassa nissandato ahaṃ.
