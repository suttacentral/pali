21.5 Nigguṇḍipupphiyattheraapadāna
Kaṇikārapupphiyavagga
Nigguṇḍipupphiyattheraapadāna
21.
2778
“Vipassissa bhagavato,
āsimārāmiko ahaṃ;
Nigguṇḍipupphaṃ paggayha,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.
22.
2779
Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
23.
2780
Pañcavīse ito kappe,
eko āsiṃ janādhipo;
Mahāpatāpanāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
24.
2781
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
2782Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā nigguṇḍipupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2783Nigguṇḍipupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
