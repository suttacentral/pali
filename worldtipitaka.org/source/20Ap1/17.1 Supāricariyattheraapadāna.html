17.1 Supāricariyattheraapadāna
Supāricariyavagga
Supāricariyattheraapadāna
1.
2433
“Padumo nāma nāmena,
dvipadindo narāsabho;
Pavanā abhinikkhamma,
dhammaṃ deseti cakkhumā.
2.
2434
Yakkhānaṃ samayo āsi,
avidūre mahesino;
Yena kiccena sampattā,
ajjhāpekkhiṃsu tāvade.
3.
2435
Buddhassa giramaññāya,
amatassa ca desanaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
apphoṭetvā upaṭṭhahiṃ.
4.
2436
Suciṇṇassa phalaṃ passa,
upaṭṭhānassa satthuno;
Tiṃsakappasahassesu,
duggatiṃ nupapajjahaṃ.
5.
2437
Ūnatiṃse kappasate,
samalaṅkatanāmako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
6.
2438
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
2439Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā supāricariyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2440Supāricariyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
