30.4 Saddasaññakattheraapadāna
Citakapūjakavagga
Saddasaññakattheraapadāna
17.
3380
“Anuggatamhi ādicce,
panādo vipulo ahu;
Buddhaseṭṭhassa lokamhi,
pātubhāvo mahesino.
18.
3381
Ghosamassosahaṃ tattha,
na ca passāmi taṃ jinaṃ;
Maraṇañca anuppatto,
buddhasaññamanussariṃ.
19.
3382
Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
20.
3383
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
3384Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā saddasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
3385Saddasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
