10.2 Sucintikattheraapadāna
Sudhāvagga
Sucintikattheraapadāna
8.
1774
“Tissassa lokanāthassa,
suddhapīṭhamadāsahaṃ;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
buddhassādiccabandhuno.
9.
1775
Aṭṭhārase ito kappe,
rājā āsiṃ mahāruci;
Bhogo ca vipulo āsi,
sayanañca anappakaṃ.
10.
1776
Pīṭhaṃ buddhassa datvāna,
vippasannena cetasā;
Anubhomi sakaṃ kammaṃ,
pubbe sukatamattano.
11.
1777
Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ pīṭhamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pīṭhadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.
12.
1778
Aṭṭhatiṃse ito kappe,
tayo te cakkavattino;
Ruci uparuci ceva,
mahāruci tatiyako.
13.
1779
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
1780Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sucintiko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
1781Sucintikattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
