39.1 Avaṭaphaladāyakattheraapadāna
Avaṭaphalavagga
Avaṭaphaladāyakattheraapadāna
1.
4177
“Sataraṃsi nāma bhagavā,
sayambhū aparājito;
Vivekakāmo sambuddho,
gocarāyābhinikkhami.
2.
4178
Phalahattho ahaṃ disvā,
upagacchiṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
avaṭaṃ adadiṃ phalaṃ.
3.
4179
Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ phalamadadiṃ ahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.
4.
4180
Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.
5.
4181
Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
mama buddhassa santike;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.
6.
4182
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
4183Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā avaṭaphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
4184Avaṭaphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
