3.1.5.1--4 Paccayānulomādi
Āsavagocchaka
Āsavaduka
Saṃsaṭṭhavāra
Paccayānulomādi
15.
1005Āsavaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho āsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  kāmāsavaṃ saṃsaṭṭho diṭṭhāsavo avijjāsavo. (Cakkaṃ. Saṃkhittaṃ.)
1006Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe nava (sabbattha nava), vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate nava.
1007Nahetuyā dve, naadhipatiyā nava, napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, najhāne ekaṃ, namagge ekaṃ, navippayutte nava.
