10.9.7.3 Paccayānulomapaccanīya
Mahantaraduka
Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaduka
Pañhāvāra
Paccayānulomapaccanīya
314.
3090Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi, naadhipatiyā nava, naanantare tīṇi, nasamanantare tīṇi, naaññamaññe ekaṃ, naupanissaye tīṇi (sabbattha tīṇi), nasampayutte ekaṃ, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā tīṇi, novigate tīṇi.
