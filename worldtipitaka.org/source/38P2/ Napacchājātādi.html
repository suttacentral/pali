66.
179Sahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sahetuko dhammo uppajjati napacchājātapaccayā…  naāsevanapaccayā…  nakammapaccayā—  sahetuke khandhe paṭicca sahetukā cetanā. (1)
180Ahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ahetuko dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā—  ahetuke khandhe paṭicca ahetukā cetanā…  bāhiraṃ…  āhārasamuṭṭhānaṃ…  utusamuṭṭhānaṃ…pe… . (1)
181Ahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sahetuko dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagataṃ uddhaccasahagataṃ mohaṃ paṭicca sampayuttakā cetanā. (2)
182Sahetukañca ahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca sahetuko dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe ca mohañca paṭicca sampayuttakā cetanā…  navipākapaccayā. (Paṭisandhi natthi.)
