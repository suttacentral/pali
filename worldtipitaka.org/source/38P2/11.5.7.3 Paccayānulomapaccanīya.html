11.5.7.3 Paccayānulomapaccanīya

Pañhāvāra
Paccayānulomapaccanīya
114.
3798Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe nava, naadhipatiyā nava, naanantare nava, nasamanantare nava, naupanissaye nava (sabbattha nava), namagge nava, nasampayutte nava, nonatthiyā nava, novigate nava.
