13.
33Nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu dhammo uppajjati naāhārapaccayā—  bāhiraṃ…  utusamuṭṭhānaṃ…  asaññasattānaṃ…pe…  naindriyapaccayā—  bāhiraṃ…  āhārasamuṭṭhānaṃ…  utusamuṭṭhānaṃ…pe…  asaññasattānaṃ mahābhūte paṭicca rūpajīvitindriyaṃ, najhānapaccayā—  pañcaviññāṇaṃ…pe…  bāhiraṃ…  āhārasamuṭṭhānaṃ…  utusamuṭṭhānaṃ…  asaññasattānaṃ…pe…  namaggapaccayā—  ahetukaṃ nahetuṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca…pe…  bāhiraṃ…  āhārasamuṭṭhānaṃ…  utusamuṭṭhānaṃ…  asaññasattānaṃ…pe…  nasampayuttapaccayā…  navippayuttapaccayā…  (napurejātasadisaṃ, arūpapañhāyeva) nonatthipaccayā…  novigatapaccayā.
