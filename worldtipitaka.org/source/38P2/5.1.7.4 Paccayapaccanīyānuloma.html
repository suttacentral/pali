5.1.7.4 Paccayapaccanīyānuloma
Ganthagocchaka
Ganthaduka
Pañhāvāra
Paccayapaccanīyānuloma
43.
1714Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā nava (anulomapadāni paripuṇṇāni kātabbāni), avigate nava.
1715Ganthadukaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
