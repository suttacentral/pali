1.19 Hetuduka, Saññojanadukādi
Hetuduka, Duka
Hetuduka, Saññojanadukādi
43.
124Hetuṃ saññojanaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  hetuṃ ganthaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  hetuṃ oghaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  hetuṃ yogaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  hetuṃ nīvaraṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  hetuṃ noparāmāsaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu no parāmāso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
125Hetuyā nava…pe…  avigate nava. (Sabbattha gocchakaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)
126Hetudukaparāmāsagocchakaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
