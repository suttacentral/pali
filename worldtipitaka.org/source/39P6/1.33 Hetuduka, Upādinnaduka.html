1.33 Hetuduka, Upādinnaduka
Hetuduka, Duka
Hetuduka, Upādinnaduka
57.
160Hetuṃ upādinnaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu upādinno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
161Hetuyā nava…pe…  avigate nava. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)
162Hetuṃ anupādinnaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu anupādinno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
163Hetuyā nava…pe…  avigate nava. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)
164Hetudukamahantaradukaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
