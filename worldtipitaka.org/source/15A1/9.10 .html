9.10
Pamādādivagga
91.
191“Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yo evaṃ mahato atthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, yoniso—  manasikāro. Yonisomanasikāro, bhikkhave, mahato atthāya saṃvattatī”ti.
192Dasamaṃ.
