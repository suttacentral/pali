21.7.4 Paccayapaccanīyānuloma
Ajjhattattika
Pañhāvāra
Paccayapaccanīyānuloma
56.
2868Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe cattāri, adhipatiyā cattāri (anulomapadāni gaṇetabbāni), avigate cha. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
2869Paccanīyānulomaṃ.
Pañhāvāro.
2870Ajjhattattikaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
