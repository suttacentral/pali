Sāsavaduka, Kusalattika
42.
122Sāsavaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanāsavo nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Anāsavaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanāsavo nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . Hetuyā dve…pe…  avigate dve.
123Sāsavaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanāsavo naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.
124Sāsavaṃ abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paccayā nasāsavo naabyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . Hetuyā dve…pe…  avigate dve.
