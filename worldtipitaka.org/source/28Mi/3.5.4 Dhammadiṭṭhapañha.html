3.5.4 Dhammadiṭṭhapañha
Milindapañha
Buddhavagga
Dhammadiṭṭhapañha
76.
369Rājā āha—  “bhante nāgasena, dhammo tayā diṭṭho”ti. “Buddhanettiyā kho, mahārāja, buddhapaññattiyā yāvajīvaṃ sāvakehi vattitabban”ti.
370“Kallosi, bhante nāgasenā”ti.
371Dhammadiṭṭhapañho catuttho.
