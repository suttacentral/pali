22.1.1--6 Paṭiccādivāra
Saraṇaduka, Piṭṭhiduka
Sauttarapada
Paṭiccādivāra
86.
181Nasaraṇaṃ nadassanena pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca saraṇo dassanena pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.
182Naaraṇaṃ nanadassanena pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo nadassanena pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.
87.
183Nasaraṇaṃ nasauttaraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo sauttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.
184(Sahajātavārampi paccayavārampi nissayavārampi saṃsaṭṭhavārampi sampayuttavārampi paṭiccavārasadisaṃ.)
