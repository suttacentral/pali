18.1--7 Paṭiccādivāra
Āsavavippayuttasāsavaduka
Paṭiccādivāra
21.
72Āsavavippayuttaṃ sāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavavippayutto naanāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)
73Āsavavippayuttaṃ anāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavavippayutto naanāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
74Āsavavippayuttaṃ sāsavañca āsavavippayuttaṃ anāsavañca dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavavippayutto nanoanāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)
75Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve…pe…  avigate pañca. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)
76Āsavagocchakaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
