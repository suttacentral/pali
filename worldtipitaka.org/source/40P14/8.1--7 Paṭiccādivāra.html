8.1--7 Paṭiccādivāra
Sanidassanaduka
Paṭiccādivāra
11.
35Anidassanaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanidassano dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Anidassanaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassano dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Anidassanaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassano ca naanidassano ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
36Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  avigate tīṇi. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)
