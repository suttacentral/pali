4.6 Tassapāpiyasikā
Samathakkhandhaka
Tassapāpiyasikā
205.
855Tena kho pana samayena upavāḷo bhikkhu saṃghamajjhe āpattiyā anuyuñjiyamāno avajānitvā paṭijānāti, paṭijānitvā avajānāti, aññenaññaṃ paṭicarati, sampajānamusā bhāsati. Ye te bhikkhū appicchā…pe…  te ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma upavāḷo bhikkhu saṃghamajjhe āpattiyā anuyuñjiyamāno avajānitvā paṭijānissati, paṭijānitvā avajānissati, aññenaññaṃ paṭicarissati, sampajānamusā bhāsissatī”ti. Atha kho te bhikkhū bhagavato etamatthaṃ ārocesuṃ…pe…  “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave…pe…  “saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti…pe…  vigarahitvā…pe…  dhammiṃ kathaṃ katvā bhikkhū āmantesi—  “tena hi, bhikkhave, saṃgho upavāḷassa bhikkhuno tassapāpiyasikākammaṃ karotu. Evañca pana, bhikkhave, kātabbaṃ. Paṭhamaṃ upavāḷo bhikkhu codetabbo, codetvā sāretabbo, sāretvā āpattiṃ āropetabbo, āpattiṃ āropetvā byattena bhikkhunā paṭibalena saṃgho ñāpetabbo— 
Samathakkhandhaka
Tassapāpiyasikā
206.
856‘Suṇātu me, bhante, saṃgho. Ayaṃ upavāḷo bhikkhu saṃghamajjhe āpattiyā anuyuñjiyamāno avajānitvā paṭijānāti, paṭijānitvā avajānāti, aññenaññaṃ paṭicarati, sampajānamusā bhāsati. Yadi saṃghassa pattakallaṃ, saṃgho upavāḷassa bhikkhuno tassapāpiyasikākammaṃ kareyya. Esā ñatti.
Samathakkhandhaka
Tassapāpiyasikā
857Suṇātu me, bhante, saṃgho. Ayaṃ upavāḷo bhikkhu saṃghamajjhe āpattiyā anuyuñjiyamāno avajānitvā paṭijānāti, paṭijānitvā avajānāti, aññenaññaṃ paṭicarati, sampajānamusā bhāsati. Saṃgho upavāḷassa bhikkhuno tassapāpiyasikākammaṃ karoti. Yassāyasmato khamati upavāḷassa bhikkhuno tassapāpiyasikākammassa karaṇaṃ, so tuṇhassa; yassa nakkhamati, so bhāseyya.
Samathakkhandhaka
Tassapāpiyasikā
858Dutiyampi etamatthaṃ vadāmi…pe…  tatiyampi etamatthaṃ vadāmi…pe… .
Samathakkhandhaka
Tassapāpiyasikā
859Kataṃ saṃghena upavāḷassa bhikkhuno tassapāpiyasikākammaṃ. Khamati saṃghassa, tasmā tuṇhī, evametaṃ dhārayāmī’ti.
Samathakkhandhaka
Tassapāpiyasikā
207.
860Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, dhammikāni tassapāpiyasikākammassa karaṇāni. Asuci ca hoti, alajjī ca, sānuvādo ca, tassa saṃgho tassapāpiyasikākammaṃ karoti dhammena, samaggena—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañca dhammikāni tassapāpiyasikākammassa karaṇāni.
