2.1.7 Pañcālacaṇḍasutta
Devaputtasaṃyutta
Paṭhamavagga
Pañcālacaṇḍasutta
88.
395Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho pañcālacaṇḍo devaputto bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi— 
396
“Sambādhe vata okāsaṃ,
Avindi bhūrimedhaso;
Yo jhānamabujjhi buddho,
Paṭilīnanisabho munī”ti.
397
“Sambādhe vāpi vindanti, (pañcālacaṇḍāti bhagavā)
Dhammaṃ nibbānapattiyā;
Ye satiṃ paccalatthaṃsu,
Sammā te susamāhitā”ti.
