1.5.5 Anomasutta
Devatāsaṃyutta
Ādittavagga
Anomasutta
45.
250
“Anomanāmaṃ nipuṇatthadassiṃ,
Paññādadaṃ kāmālaye asattaṃ;
Taṃ passatha sabbaviduṃ sumedhaṃ,
Ariye pathe kamamānaṃ mahesin”ti.
