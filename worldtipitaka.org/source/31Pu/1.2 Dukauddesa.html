1.2 Dukauddesa
Mātikā
Dukauddesa
8.
64Dve puggalā— 
65Kodhano ca, upanāhī ca. (1)
66Makkhī ca, paḷāsī ca. (2)
67Issukī ca, maccharī ca. (3)
68Saṭho ca, māyāvī ca. (4)
69Ahiriko ca, anottappī ca. (5)
70Dubbaco ca, pāpamitto ca. (6)
71Indriyesu aguttadvāro ca, bhojane amattaññū ca. (7)
72Muṭṭhassati ca, asampajāno ca. (8)
73Sīlavipanno ca, diṭṭhivipanno ca. (9)
74Ajjhattasaṃyojano ca, bahiddhāsaṃyojano ca. (10)
75Akkodhano ca, anupanāhī ca. (11)
76Amakkhī ca, apaḷāsī ca. (12)
77Anissukī ca, amaccharī ca. (13)
78Asaṭho ca, amāyāvī ca. (14)
79Hirimā ca, ottappī ca. (15)
80Suvaco ca, kalyāṇamitto ca. (16)
81Indriyesu guttadvāro ca, bhojane mattaññū ca. (17)
82Upaṭṭhitassati ca, sampajāno ca. (18)
83Sīlasampanno ca, diṭṭhisampanno ca. (19)
84Dve puggalā dullabhā lokasmiṃ. (20)
85Dve puggalā duttappayā. (21)
86Dve puggalā sutappayā. (22)
87Dvinnaṃ puggalānaṃ āsavā vaḍḍhanti. (23)
88Dvinnaṃ puggalānaṃ āsavā na vaḍḍhanti. (24)
89Hīnādhimutto ca, paṇītādhimutto ca. (25)
90Titto ca, tappetā ca. (26)
91Dukaṃ.
