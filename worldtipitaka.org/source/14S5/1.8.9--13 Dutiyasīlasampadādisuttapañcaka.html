1.8.9--13 Dutiyasīlasampadādisuttapañcaka
Maggasaṃyutta
Dutiyaekadhammapeyyālavagga
Dutiyasīlasampadādisuttapañcaka
85--89.
193“Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi, yena anuppanno vā ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo uppajjati, uppanno vā ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvanāpāripūriṃ gacchati, yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, sīlasampadā…pe…  yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, chandasampadā…pe…  yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, attasampadā…pe…  yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, diṭṭhisampadā…pe…  yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, appamādasampadā…pe… .
194Chaṭṭhaṃ.
