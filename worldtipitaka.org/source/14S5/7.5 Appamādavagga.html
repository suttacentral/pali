7.5 Appamādavagga
Iddhipādasaṃyutta
Appamādavagga
1532(Appamādavaggo vitthāretabbo.)
1533Tassuddānaṃ
1534
Tathāgataṃ padaṃ kūṭaṃ,
mūlaṃ sāro ca vassikaṃ;
Rājā candimasūriyā,
vatthena dasamaṃ padanti.
