3.3.4 Suddhasutta
Satipaṭṭhānasaṃyutta
Sīlaṭṭhitivagga
Suddhasutta
390.
874Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Cattārome, bhikkhave, satipaṭṭhānā. Katame cattāro? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu kāye kāyānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhijjhādomanassaṃ; vedanāsu…pe…  citte…pe…  dhammesu dhammānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhijjhādomanassaṃ. Ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro satipaṭṭhānā”ti.
875Catutthaṃ.
