6.2 Appamādavagga
Balasaṃyutta
Appamādavagga
1315(Appamādavaggo vitthāretabbo.)
1316Tassuddānaṃ
1317
Tathāgataṃ padaṃ kūṭaṃ,
mūlaṃ sārena vassikaṃ;
Rājā candimasūriyā,
vatthena dasamaṃ padanti.
