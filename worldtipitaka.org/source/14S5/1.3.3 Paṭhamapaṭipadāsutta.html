1.3.3 Paṭhamapaṭipadāsutta
Maggasaṃyutta
Micchattavagga
Paṭhamapaṭipadāsutta
23.
92Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Micchāpaṭipadañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, sammāpaṭipadañca. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamā ca, bhikkhave, micchāpaṭipadā? Seyyathidaṃ—  micchādiṭṭhi…pe…  micchāsamādhi. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, micchāpaṭipadā. Katamā ca, bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipadā? Seyyathidaṃ—  sammādiṭṭhi…pe…  sammāsamādhi. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipadā”ti.
93Tatiyaṃ.
