4.3.4 Atthasutta
Catutthapaṇṇāsaka
Sādhuvagga
Atthasutta
181.
1330“Atthañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi anatthañca. Taṃ suṇātha…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, anattho? Pāṇātipāto…pe…  micchādiṭṭhi—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, anattho.
1331Katamo ca, bhikkhave, attho? Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī…pe…  sammādiṭṭhi—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, attho”ti.
1332Catutthaṃ.
