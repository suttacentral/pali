3.3.5 Pañcamasutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Parisuddhavagga
Pañcamasutta
127.
1090“Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhammā ekantanibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya upasamāya abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattanti, nāññatra sugatavinayā. Katame dasa? Sammādiṭṭhi…pe…  sammāvimutti—  ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhammā ekantanibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya upasamāya abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattanti, nāññatra sugatavinayā”ti.
1091Pañcamaṃ.
