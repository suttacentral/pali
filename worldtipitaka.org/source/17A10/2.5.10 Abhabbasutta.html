2.5.10 Abhabbasutta
Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka
Upālivagga
Abhabbasutta
100.
918“Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhamme appahāya abhabbo arahattaṃ sacchikātuṃ. Katame dasa? Rāgaṃ, dosaṃ, mohaṃ, kodhaṃ, upanāhaṃ, makkhaṃ, paḷāsaṃ, issaṃ, macchariyaṃ, mānaṃ—  ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhamme appahāya abhabbo arahattaṃ sacchikātuṃ.
919Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhamme pahāya bhabbo arahattaṃ sacchikātuṃ. Katame dasa? Rāgaṃ, dosaṃ, mohaṃ, kodhaṃ, upanāhaṃ, makkhaṃ, paḷāsaṃ, issaṃ, macchariyaṃ, mānaṃ—  ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhamme pahāya bhabbo arahattaṃ sacchikātun”ti.
920Dasamaṃ.
921Upālivaggo pañcamo.
922Tassuddānaṃ
923
Kāmabhogī bhayaṃ diṭṭhi,
vajjiyamāhituttiyā;
Kokanudo āhuneyyo,
thero upāli abhabboti.
924Dutiyo paṇṇāsako samatto.
