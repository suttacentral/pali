6.2.8 Aññamaññacatukka
Paṭiccasamuppādavibhaṅga
Abhidhammabhājanīya
Aññamaññacatukka
272.
1098Tasmiṃ samaye avijjāpaccayā saṅkhāro, saṅkhārapaccayāpi avijjā; saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṃ, viññāṇapaccayāpi saṅkhāro; viññāṇapaccayā nāmaṃ, nāmapaccayāpi viññāṇaṃ; nāmapaccayā chaṭṭhāyatanaṃ, chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayāpi nāmaṃ; chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayā phasso, phassapaccayāpi chaṭṭhāyatanaṃ; phassapaccayā vedanā, vedanāpaccayāpi phasso; vedanāpaccayā taṇhā, taṇhāpaccayāpi vedanā; taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṃ, upādānapaccayāpi taṇhā; upādānapaccayā bhavo, bhavapaccayā jāti, jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṃ. Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hoti.
273.
1099Tattha katamā avijjā? Yaṃ aññāṇaṃ adassanaṃ…pe…  avijjālaṅgī moho akusalamūlaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “avijjā”.
1100Tattha katamo avijjāpaccayā saṅkhāro? Yā cetanā sañcetanā sañcetayitattaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “avijjāpaccayā saṅkhāro”.
1101Tattha katamā saṅkhārapaccayāpi avijjā? Yaṃ aññāṇaṃ adassanaṃ…pe…  avijjālaṅgī moho akusalamūlaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “saṅkhārapaccayāpi avijjā”.
1102Tattha katamaṃ saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṃ? Yaṃ cittaṃ mano mānasaṃ…pe…  tajjāmanoviññāṇadhātu—  idaṃ vuccati “saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṃ”.
1103Tattha katamo viññāṇapaccayāpi saṅkhāro? Yā cetanā sañcetanā sañcetayitattaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “viññāṇapaccayāpi saṅkhāro”.
1104Tattha katamaṃ viññāṇapaccayā nāmaṃ? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  idaṃ vuccati “viññāṇapaccayā nāmaṃ”.
1105Tattha katamaṃ nāmapaccayāpi viññāṇaṃ? Yaṃ cittaṃ mano mānasaṃ…pe…  tajjāmanoviññāṇadhātu—  idaṃ vuccati “nāmapaccayāpi viññāṇaṃ”.
1106Tattha katamaṃ nāmapaccayā chaṭṭhāyatanaṃ? Yaṃ cittaṃ mano mānasaṃ…pe…  tajjāmanoviññāṇadhātu—  idaṃ vuccati “nāmapaccayā chaṭṭhāyatanaṃ”.
1107Tattha katamaṃ chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayāpi nāmaṃ? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  idaṃ vuccati “chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayāpi nāmaṃ”.
1108Tattha katamo chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayā phasso? Yo phasso phusanā samphusanā samphusitattaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayā phasso”.
1109Tattha katamaṃ phassapaccayāpi chaṭṭhāyatanaṃ? Yaṃ cittaṃ mano mānasaṃ…pe…  tajjāmanoviññāṇadhātu—  idaṃ vuccati “phassapaccayāpi chaṭṭhāyatanaṃ”.
1110Tattha katamā phassapaccayā vedanā? Yaṃ cetasikaṃ sātaṃ cetasikaṃ sukhaṃ cetosamphassajaṃ sātaṃ sukhaṃ vedayitaṃ cetosamphassajā sātā sukhā vedanā—  ayaṃ vuccati “phassapaccayā vedanā”.
1111Tattha katamo vedanāpaccayāpi phasso? Yo phasso phusanā samphusanā samphusitattaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “vedanāpaccayāpi phasso”.
1112Tattha katamā vedanāpaccayā taṇhā? Yo rāgo sārāgo…pe…  cittassa sārāgo—  ayaṃ vuccati “vedanāpaccayā taṇhā”.
1113Tattha katamā taṇhāpaccayāpi vedanā? Yaṃ cetasikaṃ sātaṃ cetasikaṃ sukhaṃ cetosamphassajaṃ sātaṃ sukhaṃ vedayitaṃ cetosamphassajā sātā sukhā vedanā—  ayaṃ vuccati “taṇhāpaccayāpi vedanā”.
1114Tattha katamaṃ taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṃ? Yā diṭṭhi diṭṭhigataṃ…pe…  titthāyatanaṃ vipariyāsaggāho—  idaṃ vuccati “taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṃ”.
1115Tattha katamā upādānapaccayāpi taṇhā? Yo rāgo…pe…  cittassa sārāgo—  ayaṃ vuccati “upādānapaccayāpi taṇhā”.
1116Tattha katamo upādānapaccayā bhavo? Ṭhapetvā upādānaṃ, vedanākkhandho saññākkhandho saṅkhārakkhandho viññāṇakkhandho—  ayaṃ vuccati “upādānapaccayā bhavo”.
1117Tattha katamā bhavapaccayā jāti? Yā tesaṃ tesaṃ dhammānaṃ jāti sañjāti nibbatti abhinibbatti pātubhāvo—  ayaṃ vuccati “bhavapaccayā jāti”.
1118Tattha katamaṃ jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṃ? Atthi jarā, atthi maraṇaṃ. Tattha katamā jarā? Yā tesaṃ tesaṃ dhammānaṃ jarā jīraṇatā āyuno saṃhāni—  ayaṃ vuccati “jarā”. Tattha katamaṃ maraṇaṃ? Yo tesaṃ tesaṃ dhammānaṃ khayo vayo bhedo paribhedo aniccatā antaradhānaṃ—  idaṃ vuccati “maraṇaṃ”. Iti ayañca jarā, idañca maraṇaṃ. Idaṃ vuccati “jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṃ”.
1119Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hotīti. Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa saṅgati hoti, samāgamo hoti, samodhānaṃ hoti, pātubhāvo hoti. Tena vuccati “evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hotī”ti. (1:13)
274.
1120Tasmiṃ samaye avijjāpaccayā saṅkhāro, saṅkhārapaccayāpi avijjā; saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṃ, viññāṇapaccayāpi saṅkhāro; viññāṇapaccayā nāmaṃ, nāmapaccayāpi viññāṇaṃ; nāmapaccayā phasso, phassapaccayāpi nāmaṃ; phassapaccayā vedanā, vedanāpaccayāpi phasso; vedanāpaccayā taṇhā, taṇhāpaccayāpi vedanā; taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṃ, upādānapaccayāpi taṇhā; upādānapaccayā bhavo, bhavapaccayā jāti, jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṃ. Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hoti.

1128Nāmapaccayā phassoti. Tattha katamaṃ nāmaṃ? Ṭhapetvā phassaṃ, vedanākkhandho saññākkhandho saṅkhārakkhandho viññāṇakkhandho—  idaṃ vuccati “nāmaṃ”.
1129Tattha katamo nāmapaccayā phasso? Yo phasso phusanā samphusanā samphusitattaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “nāmapaccayā phasso”.
1130Tattha katamaṃ phassapaccayāpi nāmaṃ? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho, viññāṇakkhandho—  idaṃ vuccati “phassapaccayāpi nāmaṃ” …pe…  tena vuccati “evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hotī”ti. (2:14)

1137Tattha katamaṃ viññāṇapaccayā nāmarūpaṃ? Atthi nāmaṃ, atthi rūpaṃ. Tattha katamaṃ nāmaṃ? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  idaṃ vuccati “nāmaṃ”. Tattha katamaṃ rūpaṃ? Cakkhāyatanassa upacayo, sotāyatanassa upacayo, ghānāyatanassa upacayo, jivhāyatanassa upacayo, kāyāyatanassa upacayo, yaṃ vā panaññampi atthi rūpaṃ cittajaṃ cittahetukaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ—  idaṃ vuccati “rūpaṃ”. Iti idañca nāmaṃ, idañca rūpaṃ. Idaṃ vuccati “viññāṇapaccayā nāmarūpaṃ”.

Yaṃ rūpaṃ nissāya manoviññāṇadhātu vattati—  idaṃ vuccati “rūpaṃ”. Iti idañca nāmaṃ, idañca rūpaṃ. Idaṃ vuccati “nāmarūpaṃ”.

1100Tattha katamo avijjāpaccayā saṅkhāro? Yā cetanā sañcetanā sañcetayitattaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “avijjāpaccayā saṅkhāro”.

1103Tattha katamo viññāṇapaccayāpi saṅkhāro? Yā cetanā sañcetanā sañcetayitattaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “viññāṇapaccayāpi saṅkhāro”.
1104Tattha katamaṃ viññāṇapaccayā nāmaṃ? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  idaṃ vuccati “viññāṇapaccayā nāmaṃ”.
1105Tattha katamaṃ nāmapaccayāpi viññāṇaṃ? Yaṃ cittaṃ mano mānasaṃ…pe…  tajjāmanoviññāṇadhātu—  idaṃ vuccati “nāmapaccayāpi viññāṇaṃ”.
1106Tattha katamaṃ nāmapaccayā chaṭṭhāyatanaṃ? Yaṃ cittaṃ mano mānasaṃ…pe…  tajjāmanoviññāṇadhātu—  idaṃ vuccati “nāmapaccayā chaṭṭhāyatanaṃ”.

1109Tattha katamaṃ phassapaccayāpi chaṭṭhāyatanaṃ? Yaṃ cittaṃ mano mānasaṃ…pe…  tajjāmanoviññāṇadhātu—  idaṃ vuccati “phassapaccayāpi chaṭṭhāyatanaṃ”.

1111Tattha katamo vedanāpaccayāpi phasso? Yo phasso phusanā samphusanā samphusitattaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “vedanāpaccayāpi phasso”.
1112Tattha katamā vedanāpaccayā taṇhā? Yo rāgo sārāgo…pe…  cittassa sārāgo—  ayaṃ vuccati “vedanāpaccayā taṇhā”.
1113Tattha katamā taṇhāpaccayāpi vedanā? Yaṃ cetasikaṃ sātaṃ cetasikaṃ sukhaṃ cetosamphassajaṃ sātaṃ sukhaṃ vedayitaṃ cetosamphassajā sātā sukhā vedanā—  ayaṃ vuccati “taṇhāpaccayāpi vedanā”.
1114Tattha katamaṃ taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṃ? Yā diṭṭhi diṭṭhigataṃ…pe…  titthāyatanaṃ vipariyāsaggāho—  idaṃ vuccati “taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṃ”.
1115Tattha katamā upādānapaccayāpi taṇhā? Yo rāgo…pe…  cittassa sārāgo—  ayaṃ vuccati “upādānapaccayāpi taṇhā”.
1116Tattha katamo upādānapaccayā bhavo? Ṭhapetvā upādānaṃ, vedanākkhandho saññākkhandho saṅkhārakkhandho viññāṇakkhandho—  ayaṃ vuccati “upādānapaccayā bhavo”.
1117Tattha katamā bhavapaccayā jāti? Yā tesaṃ tesaṃ dhammānaṃ jāti sañjāti nibbatti abhinibbatti pātubhāvo—  ayaṃ vuccati “bhavapaccayā jāti”.
1118Tattha katamaṃ jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṃ? Atthi jarā, atthi maraṇaṃ. Tattha katamā jarā? Yā tesaṃ tesaṃ dhammānaṃ jarā jīraṇatā āyuno saṃhāni—  ayaṃ vuccati “jarā”. Tattha katamaṃ maraṇaṃ? Yo tesaṃ tesaṃ dhammānaṃ khayo vayo bhedo paribhedo aniccatā antaradhānaṃ—  idaṃ vuccati “maraṇaṃ”. Iti ayañca jarā, idañca maraṇaṃ. Idaṃ vuccati “jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṃ”.
1119Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hotīti. Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa saṅgati hoti, samāgamo hoti, samodhānaṃ hoti, pātubhāvo hoti. Tena vuccati “evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hotī”ti. (1:13)
