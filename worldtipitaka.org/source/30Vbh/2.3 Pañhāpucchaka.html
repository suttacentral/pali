Āyatanavibhaṅga
Pañhāpucchaka
168.
573Dvādasāyatanāni—  cakkhāyatanaṃ, rūpāyatanaṃ, sotāyatanaṃ, saddāyatanaṃ, ghānāyatanaṃ, gandhāyatanaṃ, jivhāyatanaṃ, rasāyatanaṃ, kāyāyatanaṃ, phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ, manāyatanaṃ, dhammāyatanaṃ.

Pañhāpucchaka
169.
574Dvādasannaṃ āyatanānaṃ kati kusalā, kati akusalā, kati abyākatā…pe…  kati saraṇā, kati araṇā?
