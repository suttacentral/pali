10.1 Suttantabhājanīya
Bojjhaṅgavibhaṅga
Suttantabhājanīya
466.
1555Satta bojjhaṅgā—  satisambojjhaṅgo, dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgo, vīriyasambojjhaṅgo, pītisambojjhaṅgo, passaddhisambojjhaṅgo, samādhisambojjhaṅgo, upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo.
467.
1556Tattha katamo satisambojjhaṅgo? Idha bhikkhu satimā hoti paramena satinepakkena samannāgato, cirakatampi cirabhāsitampi saritā hoti anussaritā—  ayaṃ vuccati “satisambojjhaṅgo”. (1)
1557So tathā sato viharanto taṃ dhammaṃ paññāya pavicinati pavicarati parivīmaṃsamāpajjati—  ayaṃ vuccati “dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgo”. (2)
1558Tassa taṃ dhammaṃ paññāya pavicinato pavicarato parivīmaṃsamāpajjato āraddhaṃ hoti vīriyaṃ asallīnaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “vīriyasambojjhaṅgo”. (3)
1559Āraddhavīriyassa uppajjati pīti nirāmisā—  ayaṃ vuccati “pītisambojjhaṅgo”. (4)
1560Pītimanassa kāyopi passambhati, cittampi passambhati—  ayaṃ vuccati “passaddhisambojjhaṅgo”. (5)
1561Passaddhakāyassa sukhino cittaṃ samādhiyati—  ayaṃ vuccati “samādhisambojjhaṅgo”. (6)
1562So tathā samāhitaṃ cittaṃ sādhukaṃ ajjhupekkhitā hoti—  ayaṃ vuccati “upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo”. (7)
468.
1563Satta bojjhaṅgā—  satisambojjhaṅgo, dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgo, vīriyasambojjhaṅgo, pītisambojjhaṅgo, passaddhisambojjhaṅgo, samādhisambojjhaṅgo, upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo.
469.
1564Tattha katamo satisambojjhaṅgo? Atthi ajjhattaṃ dhammesu sati, atthi bahiddhā dhammesu sati. Yadapi ajjhattaṃ dhammesu sati tadapi satisambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattati. Yadapi bahiddhā dhammesu sati tadapi satisambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattati. (1)
1565Tattha katamo dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgo? Atthi ajjhattaṃ dhammesu pavicayo, atthi bahiddhā dhammesu pavicayo. Yadapi ajjhattaṃ dhammesu pavicayo tadapi dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattati. Yadapi bahiddhā dhammesu pavicayo tadapi dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattati. (2)
1566Tattha katamo vīriyasambojjhaṅgo? Atthi kāyikaṃ vīriyaṃ, atthi cetasikaṃ vīriyaṃ. Yadapi kāyikaṃ vīriyaṃ tadapi vīriyasambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattati. Yadapi cetasikaṃ vīriyaṃ tadapi vīriyasambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattati. (3)
1567Tattha katamo pītisambojjhaṅgo? Atthi savitakkasavicārā pīti, atthi avitakkaavicārā pīti. Yadapi savitakkasavicārā pīti tadapi pītisambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattati. Yadapi avitakkaavicārā pīti tadapi pītisambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattati. (4)
1568Tattha katamo passaddhisambojjhaṅgo? Atthi kāyapassaddhi, atthi cittapassaddhi. Yadapi kāyapassaddhi tadapi passaddhisambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattati. Yadapi cittapassaddhi tadapi passaddhisambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattati. (5)
1569Tattha katamo samādhisambojjhaṅgo? Atthi savitakko savicāro samādhi, atthi avitakko avicāro samādhi. Yadapi savitakko savicāro samādhi tadapi samādhisambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattati. Yadapi avitakko avicāro samādhi tadapi samādhisambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattati. (6)
1570Tattha katamo upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo? Atthi ajjhattaṃ dhammesu upekkhā, atthi bahiddhā dhammesu upekkhā. Yadapi ajjhattaṃ dhammesu upekkhā tadapi upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattati. Yadapi bahiddhā dhammesu upekkhā tadapi upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattati. (7)
470.
1571Satta bojjhaṅgā—  satisambojjhaṅgo, dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgo, vīriyasambojjhaṅgo, pītisambojjhaṅgo, passaddhisambojjhaṅgo, samādhisambojjhaṅgo, upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo.
471.
1572Tattha katamo satisambojjhaṅgo? Idha bhikkhu satisambojjhaṅgaṃ bhāveti vivekanissitaṃ virāganissitaṃ nirodhanissitaṃ vossaggapariṇāmiṃ, dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgaṃ bhāveti…pe…  vīriyasambojjhaṅgaṃ bhāveti…  pītisambojjhaṅgaṃ bhāveti…  passaddhisambojjhaṅgaṃ bhāveti…  samādhisambojjhaṅgaṃ bhāveti…  upekkhāsambojjhaṅgaṃ bhāveti vivekanissitaṃ virāganissitaṃ nirodhanissitaṃ vossaggapariṇāmiṃ.
1573Suttantabhājanīyaṃ.
