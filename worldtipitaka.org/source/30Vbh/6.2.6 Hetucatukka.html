6.2.6 Hetucatukka
Paṭiccasamuppādavibhaṅga
Abhidhammabhājanīya
Hetucatukka
256.
1027Tasmiṃ samaye avijjāpaccayā saṅkhāro avijjāhetuko, saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṃ saṅkhārahetukaṃ, viññāṇapaccayā nāmaṃ viññāṇahetukaṃ, nāmapaccayā chaṭṭhāyatanaṃ nāmahetukaṃ, chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayā phasso chaṭṭhāyatanahetuko, phassapaccayā vedanā phassahetukā, vedanāpaccayā taṇhā vedanāhetukā, taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṃ taṇhāhetukaṃ; upādānapaccayā bhavo, bhavapaccayā jāti, jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṃ. Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hoti.
257.
1028Tattha katamā avijjā? Yaṃ aññāṇaṃ adassanaṃ…pe…  avijjālaṅgī moho akusalamūlaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “avijjā”.
1029Tattha katamo avijjāpaccayā saṅkhāro avijjāhetuko? Yā cetanā sañcetanā sañcetayitattaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “avijjāpaccayā saṅkhāro avijjāhetuko”.
1030Tattha katamaṃ saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṃ saṅkhārahetukaṃ? Yaṃ cittaṃ mano mānasaṃ…pe…  tajjāmanoviññāṇadhātu—  idaṃ vuccati “saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṃ saṅkhārahetukaṃ”.
1031Tattha katamaṃ viññāṇapaccayā nāmaṃ viññāṇahetukaṃ? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  idaṃ vuccati “viññāṇapaccayā nāmaṃ viññāṇahetukaṃ”.
1032Tattha katamaṃ nāmapaccayā chaṭṭhāyatanaṃ nāmahetukaṃ? Yaṃ cittaṃ mano mānasaṃ…pe…  tajjāmanoviññāṇadhātu—  idaṃ vuccati “nāmapaccayā chaṭṭhāyatanaṃ nāmahetukaṃ”.
1033Tattha katamo chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayā phasso chaṭṭhāyatanahetuko? Yo phasso phusanā samphusanā samphusitattaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayā phasso chaṭṭhāyatanahetuko”.
1034Tattha katamā phassapaccayā vedanā phassahetukā? Yaṃ cetasikaṃ sātaṃ cetasikaṃ sukhaṃ cetosamphassajaṃ sātaṃ sukhaṃ vedayitaṃ cetosamphassajā sātā sukhā vedanā—  ayaṃ vuccati “phassapaccayā vedanā phassahetukā”.
1035Tattha katamā vedanāpaccayā taṇhā vedanāhetukā? Yo rāgo sārāgo…pe…  cittassa sārāgo—  ayaṃ vuccati “vedanāpaccayā taṇhā vedanāhetukā”.
1036Tattha katamaṃ taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṃ taṇhāhetukaṃ? Yā diṭṭhi diṭṭhigataṃ…pe…  titthāyatanaṃ vipariyāsaggāho—  idaṃ vuccati “taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṃ taṇhāhetukaṃ” …pe…  tena vuccati “evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hotī”ti. (1:5)
258.
1037Tasmiṃ samaye avijjāpaccayā saṅkhāro avijjāhetuko, saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṃ saṅkhārahetukaṃ, viññāṇapaccayā nāmaṃ viññāṇahetukaṃ, nāmapaccayā phasso nāmahetuko, phassapaccayā vedanā phassahetukā, vedanāpaccayā taṇhā vedanāhetukā, taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṃ taṇhāhetukaṃ; upādānapaccayā bhavo, bhavapaccayā jāti, jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṃ. Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hoti.
259.
1038Tattha katamā avijjā? Yaṃ aññāṇaṃ adassanaṃ…pe…  avijjālaṅgī moho akusalamūlaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “avijjā”.
1039Tattha katamo avijjāpaccayā saṅkhāro avijjāhetuko? Yā cetanā sañcetanā sañcetayitattaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “avijjāpaccayā saṅkhāro avijjāhetuko”.
1040Tattha katamaṃ saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṃ saṅkhārahetukaṃ? Yaṃ cittaṃ mano mānasaṃ…pe…  tajjāmanoviññāṇadhātu—  idaṃ vuccati saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṃ saṅkhārahetukaṃ.
1041Tattha katamaṃ viññāṇapaccayā nāmaṃ viññāṇahetukaṃ? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  idaṃ vuccati “viññāṇapaccayā nāmaṃ viññāṇahetukaṃ”.
1042Nāmapaccayā phasso nāmahetukoti. Tattha katamaṃ nāmaṃ? Ṭhapetvā phassaṃ, vedanākkhandho saññākkhandho saṅkhārakkhandho viññāṇakkhandho—  idaṃ vuccati “nāmaṃ”.
1043Tattha katamo nāmapaccayā phasso nāmahetuko? Yo phasso phusanā samphusanā samphusitattaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “nāmapaccayā phasso nāmahetuko” …pe…  tena vuccati “evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hotī”ti. (2:6)
260.
1044Tasmiṃ samaye avijjāpaccayā saṅkhāro avijjāhetuko, saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṃ saṅkhārahetukaṃ, viññāṇapaccayā nāmarūpaṃ viññāṇahetukaṃ, nāmarūpapaccayā chaṭṭhāyatanaṃ nāmarūpahetukaṃ, chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayā phasso chaṭṭhāyatanahetuko, phassapaccayā vedanā phassahetukā, vedanāpaccayā taṇhā vedanāhetukā, taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṃ taṇhāhetukaṃ; upādānapaccayā bhavo, bhavapaccayā jāti, jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṃ. Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hoti.
261.
1045Tattha katamā avijjā? Yaṃ aññāṇaṃ adassanaṃ…pe…  avijjālaṅgī moho akusalamūlaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “avijjā”.
1046Tattha katamo avijjāpaccayā saṅkhāro avijjāhetuko? Yā cetanā sañcetanā sañcetayitattaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “avijjāpaccayā saṅkhāro avijjāhetuko”.
1047Tattha katamaṃ saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṃ saṅkhārahetukaṃ? Yaṃ cittaṃ mano mānasaṃ…pe…  tajjāmanoviññāṇadhātu—  idaṃ vuccati “saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṃ saṅkhārahetukaṃ”.
1048Tattha katamaṃ viññāṇapaccayā nāmarūpaṃ viññāṇahetukaṃ? Atthi nāmaṃ, atthi rūpaṃ. Tattha katamaṃ nāmaṃ? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  idaṃ vuccati “nāmaṃ”. Tattha katamaṃ rūpaṃ? Cakkhāyatanassa upacayo, sotāyatanassa upacayo, ghānāyatanassa upacayo, jivhāyatanassa upacayo, kāyāyatanassa upacayo, yaṃ vā panaññampi atthi rūpaṃ cittajaṃ cittahetukaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ—  idaṃ vuccati “rūpaṃ”. Iti idañca nāmaṃ, idañca rūpaṃ. Idaṃ vuccati “viññāṇapaccayā nāmarūpaṃ viññāṇahetukaṃ”.
1049Nāmarūpapaccayā chaṭṭhāyatanaṃ nāmarūpahetukanti. Atthi nāmaṃ, atthi rūpaṃ. Tattha katamaṃ nāmaṃ? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  idaṃ vuccati “nāmaṃ”. Tattha katamaṃ rūpaṃ? Yaṃ rūpaṃ nissāya manoviññāṇadhātu vattati—  idaṃ vuccati “rūpaṃ”. Iti idañca nāmaṃ, idañca rūpaṃ. Idaṃ vuccati “nāmarūpaṃ”.
1050Tattha katamaṃ nāmarūpapaccayā chaṭṭhāyatanaṃ nāmarūpahetukaṃ? Yaṃ cittaṃ mano mānasaṃ…pe…  tajjāmanoviññāṇadhātu—  idaṃ vuccati “nāmarūpapaccayā chaṭṭhāyatanaṃ nāmarūpahetukaṃ”.
1051Tattha katamo chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayā phasso chaṭṭhāyatanahetuko? Yo phasso phusanā samphusanā samphusitattaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayā phasso chaṭṭhāyatanahetuko” …pe…  tena vuccati “evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hotī”ti. (3:7)
262.
1052Tasmiṃ samaye avijjāpaccayā saṅkhāro avijjāhetuko, saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṃ saṅkhārahetukaṃ viññāṇapaccayā nāmarūpaṃ viññāṇahetukaṃ, nāmarūpapaccayā saḷāyatanaṃ nāmarūpahetukaṃ, chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayā phasso chaṭṭhāyatanahetuko, phassapaccayā vedanā phassahetukā, vedanāpaccayā taṇhā vedanāhetukā, taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṃ taṇhāhetukaṃ; upādānapaccayā bhavo, bhavapaccayā jāti, jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṃ. Cakkhāyatanassa upacayo, sotāyatanassa upacayo, ghānāyatanassa upacayo, jivhāyatanassa upacayo, kāyāyatanassa upacayo, yaṃ vā panaññampi atthi rūpaṃ cittajaṃ cittahetukaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ—  idaṃ vuccati “rūpaṃ”. Iti idañca nāmaṃ, idañca rūpaṃ. Idaṃ vuccati “viññāṇapaccayā nāmarūpaṃ viññāṇahetukaṃ”.

Cattāro ca mahābhūtā, yañca rūpaṃ nissāya manoviññāṇadhātu vattati—  idaṃ vuccati “rūpaṃ”. Iti idañca nāmaṃ, idañca rūpaṃ. Idaṃ vuccati “nāmarūpaṃ”.
1058Tattha katamaṃ nāmarūpapaccayā saḷāyatanaṃ nāmarūpahetukaṃ? Cakkhāyatanaṃ, sotāyatanaṃ, ghānāyatanaṃ, jivhāyatanaṃ, kāyāyatanaṃ, manāyatanaṃ—  idaṃ vuccati “nāmarūpapaccayā saḷāyatanaṃ nāmarūpahetukaṃ”.
1059Tattha katamo chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayā phasso chaṭṭhāyatanahetuko? Yo phasso phusanā samphusanā samphusitattaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayā phasso chaṭṭhāyatanahetuko”.
1060Tattha katamā phassapaccayā vedanā phassahetukā? Yaṃ cetasikaṃ sātaṃ cetasikaṃ sukhaṃ cetosamphassajaṃ sātaṃ sukhaṃ vedayitaṃ cetosamphassajā sātā sukhā vedanā—  ayaṃ vuccati “phassapaccayā vedanā phassahetukā”.
1061Tattha katamā vedanāpaccayā taṇhā vedanāhetukā? Yo rāgo sārāgo…pe…  cittassa sārāgo—  ayaṃ vuccati “vedanāpaccayā taṇhā vedanāhetukā”.
1062Tattha katamaṃ taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṃ taṇhāhetukaṃ? Yā diṭṭhi diṭṭhigataṃ…pe…  titthāyatanaṃ vipariyāsaggāho—  idaṃ vuccati “taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṃ taṇhāhetukaṃ” …pe…  tena vuccati “evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hotī”ti. (4:8)

1053Tattha katamā avijjā? Yaṃ aññāṇaṃ adassanaṃ…pe…  avijjālaṅgī moho akusalamūlaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “avijjā”.
1054Tattha katamo avijjāpaccayā saṅkhāro avijjāhetuko? Yā cetanā sañcetanā sañcetayitattaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “avijjāpaccayā saṅkhāro avijjāhetuko”.
1055Tattha katamaṃ saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṃ saṅkhārahetukaṃ? Yaṃ cittaṃ mano mānasaṃ…pe…  tajjāmanoviññāṇadhātu—  idaṃ vuccati “saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṃ saṅkhārahetukaṃ”.
1056Tattha katamaṃ viññāṇapaccayā nāmarūpaṃ viññāṇahetukaṃ? Atthi nāmaṃ, atthi rūpaṃ. Tattha katamaṃ nāmaṃ? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  idaṃ vuccati “nāmaṃ”. Tattha katamaṃ rūpaṃ? Cakkhāyatanassa upacayo, sotāyatanassa upacayo, ghānāyatanassa upacayo, jivhāyatanassa upacayo, kāyāyatanassa upacayo, yaṃ vā panaññampi atthi rūpaṃ cittajaṃ cittahetukaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ—  idaṃ vuccati “rūpaṃ”. Iti idañca nāmaṃ, idañca rūpaṃ. Idaṃ vuccati “viññāṇapaccayā nāmarūpaṃ viññāṇahetukaṃ”.
1057Nāmarūpapaccayā saḷāyatanaṃ nāmarūpahetukanti. Atthi nāmaṃ, atthi rūpaṃ. Tattha katamaṃ nāmaṃ? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  idaṃ vuccati “nāmaṃ”. Tattha katamaṃ rūpaṃ? Cattāro ca mahābhūtā, yañca rūpaṃ nissāya manoviññāṇadhātu vattati—  idaṃ vuccati “rūpaṃ”. Iti idañca nāmaṃ, idañca rūpaṃ. Idaṃ vuccati “nāmarūpaṃ”.
1058Tattha katamaṃ nāmarūpapaccayā saḷāyatanaṃ nāmarūpahetukaṃ? Cakkhāyatanaṃ, sotāyatanaṃ, ghānāyatanaṃ, jivhāyatanaṃ, kāyāyatanaṃ, manāyatanaṃ—  idaṃ vuccati “nāmarūpapaccayā saḷāyatanaṃ nāmarūpahetukaṃ”.
1059Tattha katamo chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayā phasso chaṭṭhāyatanahetuko? Yo phasso phusanā samphusanā samphusitattaṃ—  ayaṃ vuccati “chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayā phasso chaṭṭhāyatanahetuko”.
1060Tattha katamā phassapaccayā vedanā phassahetukā? Yaṃ cetasikaṃ sātaṃ cetasikaṃ sukhaṃ cetosamphassajaṃ sātaṃ sukhaṃ vedayitaṃ cetosamphassajā sātā sukhā vedanā—  ayaṃ vuccati “phassapaccayā vedanā phassahetukā”.
1061Tattha katamā vedanāpaccayā taṇhā vedanāhetukā? Yo rāgo sārāgo…pe…  cittassa sārāgo—  ayaṃ vuccati “vedanāpaccayā taṇhā vedanāhetukā”.
1062Tattha katamaṃ taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṃ taṇhāhetukaṃ? Yā diṭṭhi diṭṭhigataṃ…pe…  titthāyatanaṃ vipariyāsaggāho—  idaṃ vuccati “taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṃ taṇhāhetukaṃ” …pe…  tena vuccati “evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hotī”ti. (4:8)
