1.3.1.6 Vitakkattika
Khandhavibhaṅga
Pañhāpucchaka
Tika
Vitakkattika
505Rūpakkhandho avitakkaavicāro. Tayo khandhā siyā savitakkasavicārā, siyā avitakkavicāramattā, siyā avitakkaavicārā. Saṅkhārakkhandho siyā savitakkasavicāro, siyā avitakkavicāramatto, siyā avitakkaavicāro, siyā na vattabbo—  “savitakkasavicāro”tipi, “avitakkavicāramatto”tipi, “avitakkaavicāro”tipi.
