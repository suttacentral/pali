29 Sekkhattika, Kusalattika
Sekkhattika, Kusalattika
38.
121Nasekkhaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā sekkho kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha…pe…  avigate cha.
122Nasekkhaṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā nevasekkhanāsekkho akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
123Nasekkhaṃ naabyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nevasekkhanāsekkho abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  avigate pañca.
