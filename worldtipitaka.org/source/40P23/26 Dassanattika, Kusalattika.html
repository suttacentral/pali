26 Dassanattika, Kusalattika
Dassanattika, Kusalattika
35.
112Nadassanena pahātabbaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbo kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
113Nadassanena pahātabbaṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā dassanena pahātabbo akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha…pe…  avigate cha.
114Nadassanena pahātabbaṃ naabyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbo abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  avigate cha.
