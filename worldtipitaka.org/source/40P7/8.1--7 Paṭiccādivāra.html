8.1--7 Paṭiccādivāra
Dassanattika
Paṭiccādivāra
21.
45Nadassanena pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
46Hetuyā ekūnatiṃsa, ārammaṇe catuvīsa…pe…  vipāke nava…pe…  avigate ekūnatiṃsa.
