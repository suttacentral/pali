9.1--7 Paṭiccādivāra
Dassanahetuttika
Paṭiccādivāra
22.
47Nadassanena pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
48Hetuyā ekatiṃsa, ārammaṇe aṭṭhavīsa, adhipatiyā ekūnatiṃsa…pe…  vipāke nava…pe…  avigate ekatiṃsa.
