8.1.12 Vaṅgīsasutta
Vaṅgīsasaṃyutta
Vaṅgīsavagga
Vaṅgīsasutta
220.
1369Ekaṃ samayaṃ āyasmā vaṅgīso sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena āyasmā vaṅgīso aciraarahattappatto hutvā vimuttisukhaṃ paṭisaṃvedī tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imā gāthāyo abhāsi— 
1370
“Kāveyyamattā vicarimha pubbe,
Gāmā gāmaṃ purā puraṃ;
Athaddasāma sambuddhaṃ,
Saddhā no upapajjatha.
1371
So me dhammamadesesi,
khandhāyatanadhātuyo;
Tassāhaṃ dhammaṃ sutvāna,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ.
1372
Bahunnaṃ vata atthāya,
bodhiṃ ajjhagamā muni;
Bhikkhūnaṃ bhikkhunīnañca,
ye niyāmagataddasā.
1373
Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
mama buddhassa santike;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.
1374
Pubbenivāsaṃ jānāmi,
Dibbacakkhuṃ visodhitaṃ;
Tevijjo iddhipattomhi,
Cetopariyāyakovido”ti.
1375Tassuddānaṃ
1376
Nikkhantaṃ arati ceva,
pesalā atimaññanā;
Ānandena subhāsitā,
sāriputtapavāraṇā;
Parosahassaṃ koṇḍañño,
moggallānena gaggarā;
Vaṅgīsena dvādasāti.
1377Vaṅgīsasaṃyuttaṃ samattaṃ.
