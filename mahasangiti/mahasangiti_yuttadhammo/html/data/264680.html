52.
140Sahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sahetuko dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā—  sahetukaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)
141Sahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ahetuko dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe paṭicca vicikicchāsahagato uddhaccasahagato moho. (2)
142Sahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sahetuko ca ahetuko ca dhammā uppajjanti ārammaṇapaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagataṃ uddhaccasahagataṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā moho ca…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (3)
53.
143Ahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ahetuko dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā—  ahetukaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe vatthuṃ paṭicca ahetukā khandhā. (1)
144Ahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sahetuko dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagataṃ uddhaccasahagataṃ mohaṃ paṭicca sampayuttakā khandhā; paṭisandhikkhaṇe vatthuṃ paṭicca sahetukā khandhā. (2)
145Sahetukañca ahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca sahetuko dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagataṃ uddhaccasahagataṃ ekaṃ khandhañca mohañca paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe sahetukaṃ ekaṃ khandhañca vatthuñca paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)
