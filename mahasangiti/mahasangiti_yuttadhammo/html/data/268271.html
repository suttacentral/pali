3.4.10 Dukkhudrayasutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Sādhuvagga
Dukkhudrayasutta
143.
1124“Dukkhudrayañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṃ desessāmi sukhudrayañca. Taṃ suṇātha…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, dukkhudrayo dhammo? Micchādiṭṭhi…pe…  micchāvimutti—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, dukkhudrayo dhammo. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sukhudrayo dhammo? Sammādiṭṭhi…pe…  sammāvimutti—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sukhudrayo dhammo”ti.
1125Dasamaṃ.
