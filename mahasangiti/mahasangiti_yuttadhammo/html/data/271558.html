1.1.1.4.1 Anulomapaccanīka
Mahāpaṇṇāsaka
Paṭhamavagga
Puggalakathā
Avayavasaccikaṭṭha
Anulomapaccanīka
13.
48๐ Puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenāti? Āmantā. Sabbesu puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenāti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe.
49Ājānāhi niggahaṃ. Hañci puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthena, tena vata re vattabbe—  “sabbesu puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā”ti. Yaṃ tattha vadesi—  “vattabbe kho—  ‘puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthena’, no ca vattabbe—  ‘sabbesu puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā’”ti micchā.
50No ce pana vattabbe—  “sabbesu puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā”ti, no ca vata re vattabbe—  “puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā”ti. Yaṃ tattha vadesi—  “vattabbe kho—  ‘puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthena’, no ca vattabbe—  ‘sabbesu puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā’”ti micchā…pe… .
51Pañcamo niggaho.
