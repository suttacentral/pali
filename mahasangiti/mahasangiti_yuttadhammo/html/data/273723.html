2.3.8 Nandivisālasutta
Devaputtasaṃyutta
Nānātitthiyavagga
Nandivisālasutta
109.
538Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho nandivisālo devaputto bhagavantaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi— 
539
“Catucakkaṃ navadvāraṃ,
puṇṇaṃ lobhena saṃyutaṃ;
Paṅkajātaṃ mahāvīra,
kathaṃ yātrā bhavissatī”ti.
540
“Chetvā naddhiṃ varattañca,
icchālobhañca pāpakaṃ;
Samūlaṃ taṇhamabbuyha,
evaṃ yātrā bhavissatī”ti.
