10.
146Kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kusalo dhammo uppajjati āsevanapaccayā—  kusalaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā, tayo khandhe paṭicca eko khandho, dve khandhe paṭicca dve khandhā. (1)
147Akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo dhammo uppajjati āsevanapaccayā—  akusalaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā, tayo khandhe paṭicca eko khandho, dve khandhe paṭicca dve khandhā. (1)
148Abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato dhammo uppajjati āsevanapaccayā—  kiriyābyākataṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā, tayo khandhe paṭicca eko khandho, dve khandhe paṭicca dve khandhā. (1)
