12.11.30 Pettidevapettivisayasutta
Saccasaṃyutta
Pañcagatipeyyālavagga
Pettidevapettivisayasutta
1201.
2545…  “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye pettivisayā cutā devesu paccājāyanti; atha kho eteva bahutarā sattā ye pettivisayā cutā pettivisaye paccājāyanti. Taṃ kissa hetu? Adiṭṭhattā, bhikkhave, catunnaṃ ariyasaccānaṃ. Katamesaṃ catunnaṃ? Dukkhassa ariyasaccassa, dukkhasamudayassa ariyasaccassa, dukkhanirodhassa ariyasaccassa, dukkhanirodhagāminiyā paṭipadāya ariyasaccassa.
2546Tasmātiha, bhikkhave, ‘idaṃ dukkhan’ti yogo karaṇīyo, ‘ayaṃ dukkhasamudayo’ti yogo karaṇīyo, ‘ayaṃ dukkhanirodho’ti yogo karaṇīyo, ‘ayaṃ dukkhanirodhagāminī paṭipadā’ti yogo karaṇīyo”ti.
2547Idamavoca bhagavā. Attamanā te bhikkhū bhagavato bhāsitaṃ abhinandunti.
2548Tiṃsatimaṃ.
2549Pañcagatipeyyālavaggo ekādasamo.
2550Tassuddānaṃ
2551
Manussato cutā chāpi,
devā cutā nirayato;
Tiracchānapettivisayā,
tiṃsamatto gativaggoti.
2552Saccasaṃyuttaṃ dvādasamaṃ.
2553Mahāvaggo pañcamo.
2554Tassuddānaṃ
2555
Maggabojjhaṅgaṃ satiyā,
indriyaṃ sammappadhānaṃ;
Baliddhipādānuruddhā,
jhānānāpānasaṃyutaṃ;
Sotāpatti saccañcāti,
mahāvaggoti vuccatīti.
2556Mahāvaggasaṃyuttapāḷi niṭṭhitā.
2557Saṃyuttanikāyo samatto.
