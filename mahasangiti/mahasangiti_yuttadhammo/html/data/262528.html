Naanantara-nasamanantaraduka
Aṭṭhaka
1716Nasamanantarapaccayā nahetupaccayā naārammaṇapaccayā naadhipatipaccayā naanantarapaccayā nasahajātapaccayā naaññamaññapaccayā nissaye tīṇi, upanissaye nava, purejāte tīṇi, pacchājāte tīṇi, kamme dve, āhāre ekaṃ, indriye ekaṃ, vippayutte pañca, atthiyā satta, avigate satta. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
1717Nasamanantaramūlakaṃ.
