366»  Niruddhaṃ nirujjhamānanti? No.
367«  Nirujjhamānaṃ niruddhanti? No.
368»  Na niruddhaṃ na nirujjhamānanti?
369Bhaṅgakkhaṇe na niruddhaṃ, no ca na nirujjhamānaṃ. Uppādakkhaṇe anāgatañca cittaṃ na ceva niruddhaṃ na ca nirujjhamānaṃ.
370«  Na nirujjhamānaṃ na niruddhanti?
371Atītaṃ cittaṃ na nirujjhamānaṃ, no ca na niruddhaṃ. Uppādakkhaṇe anāgatañca cittaṃ na ceva nirujjhamānaṃ na ca niruddhaṃ.
