1.3.4.12 Aniccānupassīsutta
Khandhasaṃyutta
Uparipaṇṇāsaka
Kukkuḷavagga
Aniccānupassīsutta
147.
783Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Saddhāpabbajitassa, bhikkhave, kulaputtassa ayamanudhammo hoti—  yaṃ rūpe aniccānupassī vihareyya. Vedanāya…  saññāya…  saṅkhāresu…  viññāṇe aniccānupassī vihareyya…pe…  ‘parimuccati dukkhasmā’ti vadāmī”ti.
784Dvādasamaṃ.
