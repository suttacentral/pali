Nahetu-naadhipati
407.
881Nahetuṃ hīnaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu hīno dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. (1)
882Hetuṃ hīnaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu hīno dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā…  tīṇi.
883Nahetuṃ hīnaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu hīno dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā…  tīṇi.
884Hetuṃ hīnañca nahetuṃ hīnañca dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu hīno dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā…  tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
408.
885Nahetuyā ekaṃ, naadhipatiyā nava, napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, navippayutte nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
886Hetupaccayā naadhipatiyā nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
887Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
888(Sahajātavāropi paccayavāropi nissayavāropi saṃsaṭṭhavāropi sampayuttavāropi paṭiccavārasadisā vitthāretabbā.)
