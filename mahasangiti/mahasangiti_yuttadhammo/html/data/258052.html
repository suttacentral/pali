16 Saraṇaduka, Hetusampayuttaduka
Saraṇaduka, Hetusampayuttaduka
63.
140Nasaraṇaṃ nahetusampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo hetusampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naaraṇaṃ nahetusampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca saraṇo hetusampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
141Hetuyā dve…pe…  purejāte āsevane ekaṃ…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate dve.
64.
142Nasaraṇaṃ nahetuvippayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo hetuvippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naaraṇaṃ nahetuvippayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo hetuvippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
143Hetuyā dve, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  sahajāte cattāri…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate cattāri.
