307Sahetuko dhammo ahetukassa dhammassa vipākapaccayena paccayo—  vipākā sahetukā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vipākapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (2)
308Sahetuko dhammo sahetukassa ca ahetukassa ca dhammassa vipākapaccayena paccayo—  vipāko sahetuko eko khandho tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ…pe…  dve khandhā…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (3)
309Ahetuko dhammo ahetukassa dhammassa vipākapaccayena paccayo—  vipāko ahetuko eko khandho tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ…pe…  dve khandhā…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  khandhā vatthussa vipākapaccayena paccayo. (1)
