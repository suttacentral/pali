2255Hīnaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca majjhimo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  hīne khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. (2)

2257Majjhimaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca majjhimo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  majjhimaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  khandhe paṭicca vatthu, vatthuṃ paṭicca khandhā; ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ…pe…  mahābhūte paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ kaṭattārūpaṃ upādārūpaṃ.
