40.1.8.14 Kāyabandhanānisaṃsa
Pilindavacchavagga
Pilindavacchattheraapadāna
Dānānisaṃsakathā
Kāyabandhanānisaṃsa
127.
4424
Kāyabandhe jine datvā,
saṃghe gaṇavaruttame;
Chānisaṃse anubhomi,
kammānucchavike mama.
128.
4425
Samādhīsu na kampāmi,
vasī homi samādhisu;
Abhejjapariso homi,
ādeyyavacano sadā.
129.
4426
Upaṭṭhitasati homi,
tāso mayhaṃ na vijjati;
Devaloke manusse vā,
anubandhā ime guṇā.
