2.9.2.1 Hetugocchaka
Niddesa
Navamanaya Sampayuttenasampayuttapadaniddesa
Duka
Hetugocchaka
342.
428Hetūhi dhammehi ye dhammā…  hetūhi ceva sahetukehi ca dhammehi ye dhammā…  hetūhi ceva hetusampayuttehi ca dhammehi ye dhammā sampayuttā, tehi dhammehi ye dhammā sampayuttā…  te dhammā tīhi khandhehi ekenāyatanena ekāya dhātuyā sampayuttā; ekena khandhena ekenāyatanena ekāya dhātuyā kehici sampayuttā.
343.
429Sahetukehi ceva na ca hetūhi dhammehi ye dhammā…  hetusampayuttehi ceva na ca hetūhi dhammehi ye dhammā…  na hetusahetukehi dhammehi ye dhammā sampayuttā, tehi dhammehi ye dhammā sampayuttā…  te dhammā ekena khandhena ekenāyatanena ekāya dhātuyā kehici sampayuttā.
