10.1.9 Paṭhamasukkāsutta
Yakkhasaṃyutta
Indakavagga
Paṭhamasukkāsutta
243.
1505Ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā rājagahe viharati veḷuvane kalandakanivāpe. Tena kho pana samayena sukkā bhikkhunī mahatiyā parisāya parivutā dhammaṃ deseti. Atha kho sukkāya bhikkhuniyā abhippasanno yakkho rājagahe rathikāya rathikaṃ siṅghāṭakena siṅghāṭakaṃ upasaṅkamitvā tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imā gāthāyo abhāsi— 
1506
“Kiṃ me katā rājagahe manussā,
Madhupītāva seyare;
Ye sukkaṃ na payirupāsanti,
Desentiṃ amataṃ padaṃ.
1507
Tañca pana appaṭivānīyaṃ,
Asecanakamojavaṃ;
Pivanti maññe sappaññā,
Valāhakamiva panthagū”ti.
