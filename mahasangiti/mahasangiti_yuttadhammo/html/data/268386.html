1.2.1 Mohapariññāsutta
Ekakanipāta
Dutiyavagga
Mohapariññāsutta
11.
56Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ— 
57“Mohaṃ, bhikkhave, anabhijānaṃ aparijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ avirājayaṃ appajahaṃ abhabbo dukkhakkhayāya. Mohañca kho, bhikkhave, abhijānaṃ parijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ virājayaṃ pajahaṃ bhabbo dukkhakkhayāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati— 
58
“Yena mohena mūḷhāse,
sattā gacchanti duggatiṃ;
Taṃ mohaṃ sammadaññāya,
pajahanti vipassino;
Pahāya na punāyanti,
imaṃ lokaṃ kudācanan”ti.
59Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
60Paṭhamaṃ.
