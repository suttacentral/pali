2.2.6 Gatisutta
Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka
Satipaṭṭhānavagga
Gatisutta
68.
451“Pañcimā, bhikkhave, gatiyo. Katamā pañca? Nirayo, tiracchānayoni, pettivisayo, manussā, devā—  imā kho, bhikkhave, pañca gatiyo.
452Imāsaṃ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṃ gatīnaṃ pahānāya…pe…  ime cattāro satipaṭṭhānā bhāvetabbā”ti.
453Chaṭṭhaṃ.
