11.5.10 Aṅgasutta
Sotāpattisaṃyutta
Sagāthakapuññābhisandavagga
Aṅgasutta
1046.
2086“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, sotāpattiyaṅgāni. Katamāni cattāri? Sappurisasaṃsevo, saddhammassavanaṃ, yonisomanasikāro, dhammānudhammappaṭipatti—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri sotāpattiyaṅgānī”ti.
2087Dasamaṃ.
2088Sagāthakapuññābhisandavaggo pañcamo.
2089Tassuddānaṃ
2090
Abhisandā tayo vuttā,
Duve mahaddhanena ca;
Suddhaṃ nandiyaṃ bhaddiyaṃ,
Mahānāmaṅgena te dasāti.
