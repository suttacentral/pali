28.2 Tilamuṭṭhidāyakattheraapadāna
Suvaṇṇabibbohanavagga
Tilamuṭṭhidāyakattheraapadāna
5.
3229
“Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
satthā lokagganāyako;
Manomayena kāyena,
iddhiyā upasaṅkami.
6.
3230
Satthāraṃ upasaṅkantaṃ,
vanditvā purisuttamaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
tilamuṭṭhimadāsahaṃ.
7.
3231
Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
tilamuṭṭhiyidaṃ phalaṃ.
8.
3232
Ito soḷasakappamhi,
tantiso nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
9.
3233
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
3234Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tilamuṭṭhidāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
3235Tilamuṭṭhidāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
