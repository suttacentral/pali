18.6 Maggadattikattheraapadāna
Kumudavagga
Maggadattikattheraapadāna
26.
2545
“Anomadassī bhagavā,
dvipadindo narāsabho;
Diṭṭhadhammasukhatthāya,
abbhokāsamhi caṅkami.
27.
2546
Uddhate pāde pupphāni,
sobhaṃ muddhani tiṭṭhare;
Pasannacitto sumano,
vanditvā pupphamokiriṃ.
28.
2547
Vīsakappasahassamhi,
ito pañca janā ahuṃ;
Pupphacchadaniyā nāma,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.
29.
2548
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
2549Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā maggadattiko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2550Maggadattikattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
