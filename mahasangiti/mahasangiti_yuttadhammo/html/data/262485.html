1508Indriya sahajāta nissaya āhāra atthi avigatanti nahetuyā satta…pe…  naaññamaññe tīṇi, naupanissaye satta…pe…  nasampayutte tīṇi, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā satta, novigate satta.
1509Indriya sahajāta aññamañña nissaya āhāra atthi avigatanti nahetuyā tīṇi…pe…  nasampayutte ekaṃ, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā tīṇi, novigate tīṇi.
