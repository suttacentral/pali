Paccayānulomapaccanīya
Hetuduka
35.
1538Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe pañca, naadhipatiyā dvādasa, naanantare nasamanantare naaññamaññe naupanissaye pañca, napurejāte satta, napacchājāte sattarasa, naāsevane ekādasa, nakamme satta, navipāke sattarasa, nasampayutte pañca, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā novigate pañca. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
1539Anulomapaccanīyaṃ.
