234Sahetukaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho sahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  sahetukaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)
235Ahetukaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho sahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagataṃ uddhaccasahagataṃ mohaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā vicikicchāsahagatā uddhaccasahagatā khandhā. (1)
236Sahetukañca ahetukañca dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho sahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagataṃ uddhaccasahagataṃ ekaṃ khandhañca mohañca saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)
