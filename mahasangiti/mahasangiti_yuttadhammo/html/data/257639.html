24 Hetusampayuttaduka, Kusalattika
Hetusampayuttaduka, Kusalattika
31.
86Hetusampayuttaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetusampayutto nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.
87Hetusampayuttaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetusampayutto naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
88Hetuvippayuttaṃ abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paccayā nahetuvippayutto naabyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
