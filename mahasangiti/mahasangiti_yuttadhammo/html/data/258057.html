Saraṇaduka, Mahantaradukādi
81.
175Nasaraṇaṃ nasārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo sārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.
176Nasaraṇaṃ naanārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo anārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naaraṇaṃ naanārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo anārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve, adhipatiyā dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate dve.
