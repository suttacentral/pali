18 Kusalattika, Ajjhattattikadvaya
Kusalattika, Ajjhattattikadvaya
23.
76Nakusalo naajjhatto dhammo ajjhattassa kusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Ārammaṇe aṭṭhārasa, adhipatiyā soḷasa, upanissaye aṭṭhārasa, purejāte atthiyā avigate nava.
77Nakusalo nabahiddhā dhammo bahiddhā kusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Ārammaṇe aṭṭhārasa, adhipatiyā cha, upanissaye aṭṭhārasa, purejāte atthiyā avigate nava.
24.
78Nakusalaṃ naajjhattārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato ajjhattārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
79Nakusalaṃ nabahiddhārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato bahiddhārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
