44.4 Ñāṇatthavikattheraapadāna
Ekavihārivagga
Ñāṇatthavikattheraapadāna
35.
5704
“Kaṇikāraṃva jalitaṃ,
dīparukkhaṃva jotitaṃ;
Kañcanaṃva virocantaṃ,
addasaṃ dvipaduttamaṃ.
36.
5705
Kamaṇḍaluṃ ṭhapetvāna,
vākacīrañca kuṇḍikaṃ;
Ekaṃsaṃ ajinaṃ katvā,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ thaviṃ ahaṃ.
37.
5706
‘Tamandhakāraṃ vidhamaṃ,
mohajālasamākulaṃ;
Ñāṇālokaṃ dassetvāna,
nittiṇṇosi mahāmuni.
38.
5707
Samuddharasimaṃ lokaṃ,
sabbāvantamanuttaraṃ;
Ñāṇe te upamā natthi,
yāvatājagatogati.
39.
5708
Tena ñāṇena sabbaññū,
iti buddho pavuccati;
Vandāmi taṃ mahāvīraṃ,
sabbaññutamanāvaraṃ’.
40.
5709
Satasahassito kappe,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ thaviṃ ahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ñāṇatthavāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
41.
5710
Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
…pe… 
viharāmi anāsavo.
42.
5711
Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.
43.
5712
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
5713Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ñāṇatthaviko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
5714Ñāṇatthavikattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
