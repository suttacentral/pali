2.1.2 Dutiyakassapasutta
Devaputtasaṃyutta
Paṭhamavagga
Dutiyakassapasutta
83.
374Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho kassapo devaputto bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi— 
375
“Bhikkhu siyā jhāyī vimuttacitto,
Ākaṅkhe ce hadayassānupattiṃ;
Lokassa ñatvā udayabbayañca,
Sucetaso anissito tadānisaṃso”ti.
