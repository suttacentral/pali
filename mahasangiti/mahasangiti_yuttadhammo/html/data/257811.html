11 Kusalattika, Parittattika
Kusalattika, Parittattika
23.
60Abyākataṃ parittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo naparitto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
61Kusalaṃ mahaggataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo namahaggato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  aññamaññe tīṇi…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
62Kusalaṃ appamāṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo naappamāṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
