16.2.6 Chakkaniddesa
Ñāṇavibhaṅga
Niddesa
Chakkaniddesa
805.
2547Tattha katamā chasu abhiññāsu paññā? Iddhividhe ñāṇaṃ, sotadhātuvisuddhiyā ñāṇaṃ, paracitte ñāṇaṃ, pubbenivāsānussatiyā ñāṇaṃ, sattānaṃ cutūpapāte ñāṇaṃ, āsavānaṃ khaye ñāṇaṃ—  imā chasu abhiññāsu paññā. Evaṃ chabbidhena ñāṇavatthu.
2548Chakkaṃ.
