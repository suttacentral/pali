5.8.3 Iddhipādasutta
Pañcamapaṇṇāsaka
Rāgapeyyāla
Iddhipādasutta
276.
1603“Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya cattāro dhammā bhāvetabbā. Katame cattāro? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu chandasamādhipadhānasaṅkhārasamannāgataṃ iddhipādaṃ bhāveti; vīriyasamādhi…pe…  cittasamādhi…pe…  vīmaṃsāsamādhipadhānasaṅkhārasamannāgataṃ iddhipādaṃ bhāveti. Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya ime cattāro dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti.
1604Tatiyaṃ.
