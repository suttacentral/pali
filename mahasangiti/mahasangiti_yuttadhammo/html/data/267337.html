21.3 Kiṅkaṇipupphiyattheraapadāna
Kaṇikārapupphiyavagga
Kiṅkaṇipupphiyattheraapadāna
10.
2763
“Kañcanagghiyasaṅkāso,
sabbaññū lokanāyako;
Odakaṃ dahamoggayha,
sināyi lokanāyako.
11.
2764
Paggayha kiṅkaṇiṃ pupphaṃ,
Vipassissābhiropayiṃ;
Udaggacitto sumano,
Dvipadindassa tādino.
12.
2765
Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
13.
2766
Sattavīsatikappamhi,
rājā bhīmaratho ahu;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
14.
2767
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
2768Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kiṅkaṇipupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2769Kiṅkaṇipupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
