133.
852Lokiyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca lokiyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  lokiyaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  khandhe paṭicca vatthu, vatthuṃ paṭicca khandhā, ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ…pe…  mahābhūte paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ, kaṭattārūpaṃ, upādārūpaṃ. (1)
134.
853Lokuttaraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca lokuttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  lokuttaraṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)
854Lokuttaraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca lokiyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  lokuttare khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. (2)
855Lokuttaraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca lokiyo ca lokuttaro ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā—  lokuttaraṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (3)
856Lokiyañca lokuttarañca dhammaṃ paṭicca lokiyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  lokuttare khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
