1.1.6 Mānasutta
Ekakanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Mānasutta
6.
27Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ— 
28“Ekadhammaṃ, bhikkhave, pajahatha; ahaṃ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāya. Katamaṃ ekadhammaṃ? Mānaṃ, bhikkhave, ekadhammaṃ pajahatha; ahaṃ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati— 
29
“Yena mānena mattāse,
sattā gacchanti duggatiṃ;
Taṃ mānaṃ sammadaññāya,
pajahanti vipassino;
Pahāya na punāyanti,
imaṃ lokaṃ kudācanan”ti.
30Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
31Chaṭṭhaṃ.
