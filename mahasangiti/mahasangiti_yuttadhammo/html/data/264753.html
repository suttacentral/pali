185.
504Nahetu sahetuko dhammo nahetusahetukassa dhammassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā nahetū sahetukā khandhā pacchimānaṃ pacchimānaṃ nahetusahetukānaṃ khandhānaṃ āsevanapaccayena paccayo…  anulomaṃ gotrabhussa…  anulomaṃ vodānassa…  gotrabhu maggassa…  vodānaṃ maggassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo. (1)
505Nahetu ahetuko dhammo nahetuahetukassa dhammassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā nahetū ahetukā khandhā pacchimānaṃ pacchimānaṃ nahetuahetukānaṃ khandhānaṃ āsevanapaccayena paccayo. (1)
