7.2.1 Cakkhusutta
Rāhulasaṃyutta
Dutiyavagga
Cakkhusutta
198.
1137Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. Atha kho āyasmā rāhulo yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ nisīdi. Ekamantaṃ nisinnaṃ kho āyasmantaṃ rāhulaṃ bhagavā etadavoca—  “taṃ kiṃ maññasi, rāhula, cakkhuṃ niccaṃ vā aniccaṃ vā”ti? “Aniccaṃ, bhante”. “Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vā taṃ sukhaṃ vā”ti? “Dukkhaṃ, bhante”. “Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vipariṇāmadhammaṃ, kallaṃ nu taṃ samanupassituṃ—  ‘etaṃ mama, esohamasmi, eso me attā’”ti? “No hetaṃ, bhante”. “Sotaṃ…pe…  ghānaṃ…  jivhā…  kāyo…  mano nicco vā anicco vā”ti? “Anicco, bhante”. “Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vā taṃ sukhaṃ vā”ti? “Dukkhaṃ, bhante”. “Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vipariṇāmadhammaṃ, kallaṃ nu taṃ samanupassituṃ—  ‘etaṃ mama, esohamasmi, eso me attā’”ti? “No hetaṃ, bhante”. “Evaṃ passaṃ, rāhula, sutavā ariyasāvako cakkhusmimpi nibbindati…pe…  sotasmimpi nibbindati…  ghānasmimpi nibbindati…  jivhāyapi nibbindati…  kāyasmimpi nibbindati…  manasmimpi nibbindati; nibbindaṃ virajjati; virāgā vimuccati; vimuttasmiṃ vimuttamiti ñāṇaṃ hoti. ‘Khīṇā jāti, vusitaṃ brahmacariyaṃ, kataṃ karaṇīyaṃ, nāparaṃ itthattāyā’ti pajānātī”ti. (Etena peyyālena dasa suttantā kātabbā.)
1138Paṭhamaṃ.
