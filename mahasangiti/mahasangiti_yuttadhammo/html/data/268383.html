1.1.8 Mānapariññāsutta
Ekakanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Mānapariññāsutta
8.
37Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ— 
38“Mānaṃ, bhikkhave, anabhijānaṃ aparijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ avirājayaṃ appajahaṃ abhabbo dukkhakkhayāya. Mānañca kho, bhikkhave, abhijānaṃ parijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ virājayaṃ pajahaṃ bhabbo dukkhakkhayāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati— 
39
“Mānupetā ayaṃ pajā,
mānaganthā bhave ratā;
Mānaṃ aparijānantā,
āgantāro punabbhavaṃ.
40
Ye ca mānaṃ pahantvāna,
vimuttā mānasaṅkhaye;
Te mānaganthābhibhuno,
sabbadukkhamupaccagun”ti.
41Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
42Aṭṭhamaṃ.
