2.
2305Kilesaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kileso akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
2306Nokilesaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nokileso akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
2307Kilesaṃ akusalañca nokilesaṃ akusalañca dhammaṃ paṭicca kileso akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
3.
2308Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe nava (sabbattha nava), avigate nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
4.
2309Kilesaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kileso akusalo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchaṃ paṭicca vicikicchāsahagato moho, uddhaccaṃ paṭicca uddhaccasahagato moho. (1)
2310Nokilesaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kileso akusalo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe paṭicca vicikicchāsahagato uddhaccasahagato moho. (1)
2311Kilesaṃ akusalañca nokilesaṃ akusalañca dhammaṃ paṭicca kileso akusalo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate khandhe ca vicikicchañca paṭicca vicikicchāsahagato moho, uddhaccasahagate khandhe ca uddhaccañca paṭicca uddhaccasahagato moho. (1) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
5.
2312Nahetuyā tīṇi, naadhipatiyā nava, napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, navippayutte nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ, sahajātavārādi vitthāretabbo.)
6.
2313Kileso akusalo dhammo kilesassa akusalassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

2314Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā nava (majjhe tīṇi sahajātādhipati), anantare nava, samanantare nava…pe…  upanissaye nava, āsevane nava, kamme tīṇi, āhāre tīṇi, indriye tīṇi, jhāne tīṇi, magge nava, sampayutte nava…pe…  avigate nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
2315Nokilesaṃ abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nokileso abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
