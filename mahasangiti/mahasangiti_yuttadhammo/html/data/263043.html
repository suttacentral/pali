1655Sekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  sekkhaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)
1656Sekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  sekkhe khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. (2)
1657Sekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sekkho ca nevasekkhanāsekkho ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā—  sekkhaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (3)
2.
1658Asekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca asekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  asekkhaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe… . (1)
1659Asekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  asekkhe khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. (2)
1660Asekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca asekkho ca nevasekkhanāsekkho ca dhammā…pe…  asekkhaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (3)
3.
1661Nevasekkhanāsekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  nevasekkhanāsekkhaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  khandhe paṭicca vatthu, vatthuṃ paṭicca khandhā, ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ paṭicca tayo mahābhūtā…pe…  dve mahābhūte paṭicca dve mahābhūtā, mahābhūte paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ kaṭattārūpaṃ upādārūpaṃ. (1)
