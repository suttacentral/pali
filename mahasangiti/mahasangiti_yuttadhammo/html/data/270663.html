720Katame dhammā kusalā? Yasmiṃ samaye kāmāvacaraṃ kusalaṃ cittaṃ uppannaṃ hoti somanassasahagataṃ ñāṇasampayuttaṃ hīnaṃ…pe…  majjhimaṃ…pe…  paṇītaṃ…pe…  chandādhipateyyaṃ…pe…  vīriyādhipateyyaṃ…pe…  cittādhipateyyaṃ…pe…  vīmaṃsādhipateyyaṃ…pe…  chandādhipateyyaṃ hīnaṃ…pe…  majjhimaṃ…pe…  paṇītaṃ…pe…  vīriyādhipateyyaṃ hīnaṃ…pe…  majjhimaṃ…pe…  paṇītaṃ…pe…  cittādhipateyyaṃ hīnaṃ…pe…  majjhimaṃ…pe…  paṇītaṃ…pe…  vīmaṃsādhipateyyaṃ hīnaṃ…pe…  majjhimaṃ…pe…  paṇītaṃ, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti…pe…  avikkhepo hoti…pe…  ime dhammā kusalā.
