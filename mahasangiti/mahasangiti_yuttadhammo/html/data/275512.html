3.5.3 Visamasutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Maṅgalavagga
Visamasutta
149.
1069“Tīhi, bhikkhave…pe…  visamena kāyakammena, visamena vacīkammena, visamena manokammena—  imehi kho…pe…  evaṃ niraye.
1070Tīhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi…pe…  samena kāyakammena, samena vacīkammena, samena manokammena—  imehi kho…pe…  evaṃ sagge”ti.
1071Tatiyaṃ.
