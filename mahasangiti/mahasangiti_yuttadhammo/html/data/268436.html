3.2.2 Cakkhusutta
Tikanipāta
Dutiyavagga
Cakkhusutta
61.
393Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ— 
394“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, cakkhūni. Katamāni tīṇi? Maṃsacakkhu, dibbacakkhu, paññācakkhu—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi cakkhūnī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati— 
395
“Maṃsacakkhu dibbacakkhu,
paññācakkhu anuttaraṃ;
Etāni tīṇi cakkhūni,
akkhāsi purisuttamo.
396
Maṃsacakkhussa uppādo,
maggo dibbassa cakkhuno;
Yato ñāṇaṃ udapādi,
paññācakkhu anuttaraṃ;
Yassa cakkhussa paṭilābhā,
sabbadukkhā pamuccatī”ti.
397Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
398Dutiyaṃ.
