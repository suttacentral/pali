1.45 Apalāḷanavatthu
Mahākhandhaka
Apalāḷanavatthu
480Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū therānaṃ bhikkhūnaṃ sāmaṇere apalāḷenti. Therā sāmaṃ dantakaṭṭhampi mukhodakampi gaṇhantā kilamanti. Bhagavato etamatthaṃ ārocesuṃ. “Na, bhikkhave, aññassa parisā apalāḷetabbā. Yo apalāḷeyya, āpatti dukkaṭassā”ti.
