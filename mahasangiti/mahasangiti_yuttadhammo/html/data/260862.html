55.
2590Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe sattarasa, naadhipatiyā naanantare nasamanantare naupanissaye napurejāte napacchājāte naāsevane nakamme navipāke naāhāre naindriye najhāne namagge navippayutte nonatthiyā novigate sattarasa. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
2591Anulomapaccanīyaṃ.
