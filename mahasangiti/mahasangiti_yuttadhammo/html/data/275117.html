15.3.7
Aṭṭhānapāḷi
Tatiyavagga
293.
380“Aṭṭhānametaṃ, bhikkhave, anavakāso yaṃ kāyasucaritasamaṅgī tannidānā tappaccayā kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā apāyaṃ duggatiṃ vinipātaṃ nirayaṃ upapajjeyya. Netaṃ ṭhānaṃ vijjati. Ṭhānañca kho etaṃ, bhikkhave, vijjati yaṃ kāyasucaritasamaṅgī tannidānā tappaccayā kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā sugatiṃ saggaṃ lokaṃ upapajjeyya. Ṭhānametaṃ vijjatī”ti.
