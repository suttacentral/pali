Nahetuduka
46.
197Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe cuddasa, anantare samanantare cuddasa, sahajāte tettiṃsa, aññamaññe bāvīsa, nissaye tettiṃsa, upanissaye cuddasa, purejāte cha, āsevane pañca, kamme tettiṃsa…pe…  jhāne tettiṃsa, magge tīṇi, sampayutte cuddasa, vippayutte tettiṃsa…pe…  avigate tettiṃsa. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
198(Yathā kusalattike paccanīyānulomagaṇanā, evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
199Paṭiccavāro.
