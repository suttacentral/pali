2378Vipāko dhammo vipākassa dhammassa jhānapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi. Vipākadhammadhammo vipākadhammadhammassa jhānapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
2379Nevavipākanavipākadhammadhammo nevavipākanavipākadhammadhammassa jhānapaccayena paccayo—  nevavipākanavipākadhammadhammā jhānaṅgā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ jhānapaccayena paccayo.
