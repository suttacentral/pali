1.5.8.6 Amūlakasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Pācittiyakaṇḍa
Sahadhammikavagga
Amūlakasikkhāpada
459.
1367Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū bhikkhuṃ amūlakena saṃghādisesena anuddhaṃsenti.
1368Ye te bhikkhū appicchā…pe…  te ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma chabbaggiyā bhikkhū bhikkhuṃ amūlakena saṃghādisesena anuddhaṃsessantī”ti…pe…  “saccaṃ kira tumhe, bhikkhave, bhikkhuṃ amūlakena saṃghādisesena anuddhaṃsethā”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā…pe…  kathañhi nāma tumhe, moghapurisā, bhikkhuṃ amūlakena saṃghādisesena anuddhaṃsessatha. Netaṃ, moghapurisā, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya…pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddiseyyātha— 
460.
1369“Yo pana bhikkhu bhikkhuṃ amūlakena saṃghādisesena anuddhaṃseyya, pācittiyan”ti. (76:125)
461.
1370Yo panāti yo yādiso…pe…  bhikkhūti…pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippeto bhikkhūti.
1371Bhikkhunti aññaṃ bhikkhuṃ.
1372Amūlakaṃ nāma adiṭṭhaṃ assutaṃ aparisaṅkitaṃ.
1373Saṃghādisesenāti terasannaṃ aññatarena.
1374Anuddhaṃseyyāti codeti vā codāpeti vā, āpatti pācittiyassa.
462.
1375Upasampanne upasampannasaññī amūlakena saṃghādisesena anuddhaṃseti, āpatti pācittiyassa. Upasampanne vematiko amūlakena saṃghādisesena anuddhaṃseti, āpatti pācittiyassa. Upasampanne anupasampannasaññī amūlakena saṃghādisesena anuddhaṃseti, āpatti pācittiyassa.
1376Ācāravipattiyā vā diṭṭhivipattiyā vā anuddhaṃseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Anupasampannaṃ anuddhaṃseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Anupasampanne upasampannasaññī, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Anupasampanne vematiko, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Anupasampanne anupasampannasaññī, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
463.
1377Anāpatti—  tathāsaññī codeti, vā codāpeti vā, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.
1378Amūlakasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
