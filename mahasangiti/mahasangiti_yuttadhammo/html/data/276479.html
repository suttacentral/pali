1.5.4.10 Dantaponasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Pācittiyakaṇḍa
Bhojanavagga
Dantaponasikkhāpada
263.
848Tena samayena buddho bhagavā vesāliyaṃ viharati mahāvane kūṭāgārasālāyaṃ. Tena kho pana samayena aññataro bhikkhu sabbapaṃsukūliko susāne viharati. So manussehi diyyamānaṃ na icchati paṭiggahetuṃ, susānepi rukkhamūlepi ummārepi ayyavosāṭitakāni sāmaṃ gahetvā paribhuñjati. Manussā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma ayaṃ bhikkhu amhākaṃ ayyavosāṭitakāni sāmaṃ gahetvā paribhuñjissati. Theroyaṃ bhikkhu vaṭharo manussamaṃsaṃ maññe khādatī”ti.
849Assosuṃ kho bhikkhū tesaṃ manussānaṃ ujjhāyantānaṃ khiyyantānaṃ vipācentānaṃ. Ye te bhikkhū appicchā…pe…  te ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma bhikkhu adinnaṃ mukhadvāraṃ āhāraṃ āharissatī”ti…pe…  “saccaṃ kira tvaṃ, bhikkhu, adinnaṃ mukhadvāraṃ āhāraṃ āharasī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā…pe…  kathañhi nāma tvaṃ, moghapurisa, adinnaṃ mukhadvāraṃ āhāraṃ āharissasi. Netaṃ, moghapurisa, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya…pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddiseyyātha— 
850“Yo pana bhikkhu adinnaṃ mukhadvāraṃ āhāraṃ āhareyya, pācittiyan”ti.
851Evañcidaṃ bhagavatā bhikkhūnaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ paññattaṃ hoti.
264.
852Tena kho pana samayena bhikkhū udakadantapone kukkuccāyanti. Bhagavato etamatthaṃ ārocesuṃ…pe…  “anujānāmi, bhikkhave, udakadantaponaṃ sāmaṃ gahetvā paribhuñjituṃ. Evañca pana, bhikkhave, imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddiseyyātha— 
265.
853“Yo pana bhikkhu adinnaṃ mukhadvāraṃ āhāraṃ āhareyya, aññatra udakadantaponā, pācittiyan”ti. (40:89)
266.
854Yo panāti yo yādiso…pe…  bhikkhūti…pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippeto bhikkhūti.
855Adinnaṃ nāma appaṭiggahitakaṃ vuccati.
856Dinnaṃ nāma kāyena vā kāyapaṭibaddhena vā nissaggiyena vā dente hatthapāse ṭhito kāyena vā kāyapaṭibaddhena vā paṭiggaṇhāti, etaṃ dinnaṃ nāma.
857Āhāro nāma udakadantaponaṃ ṭhapetvā yaṃ kiñci ajjhoharaṇīyaṃ, eso āhāro nāma.
858Aññatra udakadantaponāti ṭhapetvā udakadantaponaṃ.
859“Khādissāmi bhuñjissāmī”ti gaṇhāti, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Ajjhohāre ajjhohāre āpatti pācittiyassa.
267.
860Appaṭiggahitake appaṭiggahitakasaññī adinnaṃ mukhadvāraṃ āhāraṃ āhāreti, aññatra udakadantaponā, āpatti pācittiyassa. Appaṭiggahitake vematiko adinnaṃ mukhadvāraṃ āhāraṃ āhāreti, aññatra udakadantaponā, āpatti pācittiyassa. Appaṭiggahitake paṭiggahitakasaññī adinnaṃ mukhadvāraṃ āhāraṃ āhāreti, aññatra udakadantaponā, āpatti pācittiyassa.
861Paṭiggahitake appaṭiggahitakasaññī, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Paṭiggahitake vematiko, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Paṭiggahitake paṭiggahitakasaññī, anāpatti.
268.
862Anāpatti—  udakadantapone, cattāri mahāvikaṭāni sati paccaye asati kappiyakārake sāmaṃ gahetvā paribhuñjati, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.
863Dantaponasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ dasamaṃ.
Bhojanavaggo catuttho.
864Tassuddānaṃ
865
Piṇḍo gaṇaṃ paraṃ pūvaṃ,
dve ca vuttā pavāraṇā;
Vikāle sannidhī khīraṃ,
dantaponena te dasāti.
