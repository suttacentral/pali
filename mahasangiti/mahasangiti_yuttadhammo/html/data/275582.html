2.1.10 Gihisāmīcisutta
Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka
Puññābhisandavagga
Gihisāmīcisutta
60.
431Atha kho anāthapiṇḍiko gahapati yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ nisīdi. Ekamantaṃ nisinnaṃ kho anāthapiṇḍikaṃ gahapatiṃ bhagavā etadavoca— 
432“Catūhi kho, gahapati, dhammehi samannāgato ariyasāvako gihisāmīcipaṭipadaṃ paṭipanno hoti yasopaṭilābhiniṃ saggasaṃvattanikaṃ. Katamehi catūhi? Idha, gahapati, ariyasāvako bhikkhusaṃghaṃ paccupaṭṭhito hoti cīvarena, bhikkhusaṃghaṃ paccupaṭṭhito hoti piṇḍapātena, bhikkhusaṃghaṃ paccupaṭṭhito hoti senāsanena, bhikkhusaṃghaṃ paccupaṭṭhito hoti gilānappaccayabhesajjaparikkhārena. Imehi kho, gahapati, catūhi dhammehi samannāgato ariyasāvako gihisāmīcipaṭipadaṃ paṭipanno hoti yasopaṭilābhiniṃ saggasaṃvattanikanti.
433
Gihisāmīcipaṭipadaṃ,
paṭipajjanti paṇḍitā;
Sammaggate sīlavante,
cīvarena upaṭṭhitā.
434
Piṇḍapātasayanena,
Gilānappaccayena ca;
Tesaṃ divā ca ratto ca,
Sadā puññaṃ pavaḍḍhati;
Saggañca kamatiṭṭhānaṃ,
Kammaṃ katvāna bhaddakan”ti.
435Dasamaṃ.
436Puññābhisandavaggo paṭhamo.
437Tassuddānaṃ
438
Dve puññābhisandā dve ca,
saṃvāsā samajīvino;
Suppavāsā sudatto ca,
bhojanaṃ gihisāmicīti.
