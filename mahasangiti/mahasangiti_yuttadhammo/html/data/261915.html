1509Kusalaṃ kilesavippayuttaṃ saṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kusalo kilesavippayutto saṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Kusalaṃ kilesavippayuttaṃ saṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato kilesavippayutto saṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Kusalaṃ kilesavippayuttaṃ saṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kusalo kilesavippayutto saṃkilesiko ca abyākato kilesavippayutto saṃkilesiko ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)
