495.
1099Nahetuṃ anāgatārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu anāgatārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. Nahetuṃ anāgatārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu anāgatārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. (2)
1100Hetuṃ anāgatārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu anāgatārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
496.
1101Nahetuyā dve, naadhipatiyā nava, napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, namagge ekaṃ, navippayutte nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
1102Hetupaccayā naadhipatiyā nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
1103Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe dve. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
