Hetu
159.
440Nahetuṃ sahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu sahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  nahetuṃ sahetukaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)
441Nahetuṃ sahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu ahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  nahetū sahetuke khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (2)
442Nahetuṃ sahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu sahetuko ca nahetu ahetuko ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā—  nahetuṃ sahetukaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (3)
160.
443Nahetuṃ ahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu ahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…pe…  ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ paṭicca…pe…  mahābhūte paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ kaṭattārūpaṃ upādārūpaṃ. (1)
444Nahetuṃ ahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu sahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  paṭisandhikkhaṇe vatthuṃ paṭicca nahetū sahetukā khandhā. (2)
445Nahetuṃ ahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu sahetuko ca nahetu ahetuko ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā—  paṭisandhikkhaṇe vatthuṃ paṭicca nahetū sahetukā khandhā, mahābhūte paṭicca kaṭattārūpaṃ. (3)
161.
446Nahetuṃ sahetukañca nahetuṃ ahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu sahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  paṭisandhikkhaṇe nahetuṃ sahetukaṃ ekaṃ khandhañca vatthuñca paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe ca…pe… . (1)
447Nahetuṃ sahetukañca nahetuṃ ahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu ahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  nahetū sahetuke khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (2)
448Nahetuṃ sahetukañca nahetuṃ ahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu sahetuko ca nahetu ahetuko ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā—  paṭisandhikkhaṇe nahetuṃ sahetukaṃ ekaṃ khandhañca vatthuñca paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  nahetū sahetuke khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca kaṭattārūpaṃ. (3)
