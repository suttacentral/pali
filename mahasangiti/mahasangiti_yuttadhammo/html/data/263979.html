64.
107Hetu abyākato dhammo hetussa abyākatassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. Hetu abyākato dhammo nahetussa abyākatassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo. Hetu abyākato dhammo hetussa abyākatassa ca nahetussa abyākatassa ca dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (3)
65.
108Nahetu abyākato dhammo nahetussa abyākatassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo.
66.
109Nahetuyā nava, naārammaṇe nava, naadhipatiyā nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ, paccanīyaṃ.)
110Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ, anulomapaccanīyaṃ.)
111Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ, paccanīyānulomaṃ.)
112(Yathā kusalattike pañhāvārassa anulomampi paccanīyampi anulomapaccanīyampi paccanīyānulomampi gaṇitaṃ, evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
113Hetudukakusalattikaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
