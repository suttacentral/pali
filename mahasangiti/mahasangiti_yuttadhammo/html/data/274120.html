9.1.12 Dutiyasiṅgālasutta
Opammasaṃyutta
Opammavagga
Dutiyasiṅgālasutta
234.
1245Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. “Assuttha no tumhe, bhikkhave, rattiyā paccūsasamayaṃ jarasiṅgālassa vassamānassā”ti? “Evaṃ, bhante”. “Siyā kho, bhikkhave, tasmiṃ jarasiṅgāle yā kāci kataññutā kataveditā, na tveva idhekacce sakyaputtiyapaṭiññe siyā yā kāci kataññutā kataveditā. Tasmātiha, bhikkhave, evaṃ sikkhitabbaṃ—  ‘kataññuno bhavissāma katavedino; na ca no amhesu appakampi kataṃ nassissatī’ti. Evañhi vo, bhikkhave, sikkhitabban”ti.
1246Dvādasamaṃ.
1247Opammavaggo paṭhamo.
1248Tassuddānaṃ
1249
Kūṭaṃ nakhasikhaṃ kulaṃ,
okkhā satti dhanuggaho;
Āṇi kaliṅgaro nāgo,
biḷāro dve siṅgālakāti.
1250Opammasaṃyuttaṃ samattaṃ.
