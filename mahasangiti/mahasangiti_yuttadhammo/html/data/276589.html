1.7.6.8 Daṇḍapāṇisikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Surusuruvagga
Daṇḍapāṇisikkhāpada
635.
1984Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū daṇḍapāṇissa dhammaṃ desenti…pe… .
1985“Na daṇḍapāṇissa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (58:203)
1986Daṇḍo nāma majjhimassa purisassa catuhattho daṇḍo. Tato ukkaṭṭho adaṇḍo, omako adaṇḍo.
1987Na daṇḍapāṇissa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca daṇḍapāṇissa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1988Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.
1989Aṭṭhamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
