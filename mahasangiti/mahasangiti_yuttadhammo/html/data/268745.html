3.1.4 Dhaniyattheragāthā
Tikanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Dhaniyattheragāthā
228.
446
“Sukhañce jīvituṃ icche,
sāmaññasmiṃ apekkhavā;
Saṃghikaṃ nātimaññeyya,
cīvaraṃ pānabhojanaṃ.
229.
447
Sukhañce jīvituṃ icche,
sāmaññasmiṃ apekkhavā;
Ahi mūsikasobbhaṃva,
sevetha sayanāsanaṃ.
230.
448
Sukhañce jīvituṃ icche,
sāmaññasmiṃ apekkhavā;
Itarītarena tusseyya,
ekadhammañca bhāvaye”ti.
