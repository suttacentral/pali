99.
171Hetu adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo hetussa adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
100.
172Hetu adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo hetussa adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  nava.
101.
173Hetu adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo hetussa adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati…  tīṇi.
174Nahetu adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo nahetussa adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati…  tīṇi.
175Hetu adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto ca nahetu adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto ca dhammā hetussa adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati…  tīṇi. (Ārammaṇādhipatiyeva)…pe… .
