1.1.2.4.3 Pattavagga
Bhikkhuvibhaṅga
Paṭhamabhāga
Katāpattivāra
Nissaggiyakaṇḍa
Pattavagga
164.
374Atirekapattaṃ dasāhaṃ atikkāmento ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Nissaggiyaṃ pācittiyaṃ. (1)
375Ūnapañcabandhanena pattena aññaṃ navaṃ pattaṃ cetāpento dve āpattiyo āpajjati. Cetāpeti, payoge dukkaṭaṃ; cetāpite nissaggiyaṃ pācittiyaṃ. (2)
376Bhesajjāni paṭiggahetvā sattāhaṃ atikkāmento ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Nissaggiyaṃ pācittiyaṃ. (3)
377Atirekamāse sese gimhāne vassikasāṭikacīvaraṃ pariyesanto dve āpattiyo āpajjati. Pariyesati, payoge dukkaṭaṃ; pariyiṭṭhe nissaggiyaṃ pācittiyaṃ. (4)
378Bhikkhussa sāmaṃ cīvaraṃ datvā kupito anattamano acchindanto dve āpattiyo āpajjati. Acchindati, payoge dukkaṭaṃ; acchinne nissaggiyaṃ pācittiyaṃ. (5)
379Sāmaṃ suttaṃ viññāpetvā tantavāyehi cīvaraṃ vāyāpento dve āpattiyo āpajjati. Vāyāpeti, payoge dukkaṭaṃ; vāyāpite nissaggiyaṃ pācittiyaṃ. (6)
380Pubbe appavārito aññātakassa gahapatikassa tantavāye upasaṅkamitvā cīvare vikappaṃ āpajjanto dve āpattiyo āpajjati. Vikappaṃ āpajjati, payoge dukkaṭaṃ; vikappaṃ āpanne nissaggiyaṃ pācittiyaṃ. (7)
381Accekacīvaraṃ paṭiggahetvā cīvarakālasamayaṃ atikkāmento ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Nissaggiyaṃ pācittiyaṃ. (8)
382Tiṇṇaṃ cīvarānaṃ aññataraṃ cīvaraṃ antaraghare nikkhipitvā atirekachārattaṃ vippavasanto ekaṃ āpattiṃ āpajjati. Nissaggiyaṃ pācittiyaṃ. (9)
383Jānaṃ saṃghikaṃ lābhaṃ pariṇataṃ attano pariṇāmento dve āpattiyo āpajjati. Pariṇāmeti, payoge dukkaṭaṃ; pariṇāmite nissaggiyaṃ pācittiyaṃ. (10)
384Pattavaggo tatiyo.
Tiṃsa nissaggiyā pācittiyā niṭṭhitā.
