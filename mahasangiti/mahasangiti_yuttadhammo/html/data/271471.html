2.4.2.5.5 Ganthaganthasampayuttaduka
Niddesa
Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa
Dukaatthuddhāra
Ganthagocchaka
Ganthaganthasampayuttaduka
1497.
2208Katame dhammā ganthā ceva ganthasampayuttā ca? Yattha diṭṭhi ca lobho ca ekato uppajjanti—  ime dhammā ganthā ceva ganthasampayuttā ca.
1498.
2209Katame dhammā ganthasampayuttā ceva no ca ganthā? Aṭṭha lobhasahagatacittuppādā dve domanassasahagatacittuppādā, etthuppanne ganthe ṭhapetvā—  ime dhammā ganthasampayuttā ceva no ca ganthā. Ganthavippayuttā dhammā na vattabbā—  ganthā ceva ganthasampayuttā cātipi, ganthasampayuttā ceva no ca ganthātipi.
