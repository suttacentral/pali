40.1.8.11 Teladhārānisaṃsa
Pilindavacchavagga
Pilindavacchattheraapadāna
Dānānisaṃsakathā
Teladhārānisaṃsa
121.
4418
Teladhāre sugate datvā,
saṃghe gaṇavaruttame;
Pañcānisaṃse anubhomi,
kammānucchavike mama.
122.
4419
Sucārurūpo subhaddo,
susamuggatamānaso;
Avikkhittamano homi,
sabbārakkhehi rakkhito.
