Anantara
25.
1668Gantho dhammo ganthassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā ganthā pacchimānaṃ pacchimānaṃ ganthānaṃ anantarapaccayena paccayo. (1)
1669Gantho dhammo noganthassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā ganthā pacchimānaṃ pacchimānaṃ noganthānaṃ khandhānaṃ anantarapaccayena paccayo; ganthā vuṭṭhānassa anantarapaccayena paccayo. (2)
1670Gantho dhammo ganthassa ca noganthassa ca dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā ganthā pacchimānaṃ pacchimānaṃ ganthānaṃ sampayuttakānañca khandhānaṃ anantarapaccayena paccayo. (3)
26.
1671Nogantho dhammo noganthassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi. (Dve āvajjanā kātabbā, paṭhamo natthi.)
1672Gantho ca nogantho ca dhammā ganthassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi. (Ekampi vuṭṭhānaṃ kātabbaṃ, majjhe.)
