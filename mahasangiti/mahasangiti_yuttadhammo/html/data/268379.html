1.1.4 Kodhasutta
Ekakanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Kodhasutta
4.
17Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ— 
18“Ekadhammaṃ, bhikkhave, pajahatha; ahaṃ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāya. Katamaṃ ekadhammaṃ? Kodhaṃ, bhikkhave, ekadhammaṃ pajahatha; ahaṃ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati— 
19
“Yena kodhena kuddhāse,
sattā gacchanti duggatiṃ;
Taṃ kodhaṃ sammadaññāya,
pajahanti vipassino;
Pahāya na punāyanti,
imaṃ lokaṃ kudācanan”ti.
20Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
21Catutthaṃ.
