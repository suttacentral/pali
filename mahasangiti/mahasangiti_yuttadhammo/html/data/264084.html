395.
851Hetu mahaggatārammaṇo dhammo hetussa mahaggatārammaṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
396.
852Nahetuyā nava, naārammaṇe nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
853Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
854Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
855(Yathā kusalattike pañhāvārassa anulomampi paccanīyampi anulomapaccanīyampi paccanīyānulomampi gaṇitaṃ, evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
