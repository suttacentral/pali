12 Kusalattika, Parittārammaṇattika
Kusalattika, Parittārammaṇattika
16.
57Nakusalaṃ naparittārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato parittārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
58Nakusalaṃ namahaggatārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo mahaggatārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate nava.
59Nakusalaṃ naappamāṇārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo appamāṇārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
