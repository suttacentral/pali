3.5.7 Kāmayogasutta
Tikanipāta
Pañcamavagga
Kāmayogasutta
96.
685Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ— 
686“Kāmayogayutto, bhikkhave, bhavayogayutto āgāmī hoti āgantā itthattaṃ. Kāmayogavisaṃyutto, bhikkhave, bhavayogayutto anāgāmī hoti anāgantā itthattaṃ. Kāmayogavisaṃyutto, bhikkhave, bhavayogavisaṃyutto arahā hoti, khīṇāsavo”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati— 
687
“Kāmayogena saṃyuttā,
bhavayogena cūbhayaṃ;
Sattā gacchanti saṃsāraṃ,
jātimaraṇagāmino.
688
Ye ca kāme pahantvāna,
appattā āsavakkhayaṃ;
Bhavayogena saṃyuttā,
anāgāmīti vuccare.
689
Ye ca kho chinnasaṃsayā,
khīṇamānapunabbhavā;
Te ve pāraṅgatā loke,
ye pattā āsavakkhayan”ti.
690Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
691Sattamaṃ.
692Tatiyabhāṇavāraṃ.
