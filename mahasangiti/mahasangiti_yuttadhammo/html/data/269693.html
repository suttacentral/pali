3.7.13 Samuddaekarasapañha
Milindapañha
Arūpadhammavavatthānavagga
Samuddaekarasapañha
106.
498Rājā āha—  “bhante nāgasena, kena kāraṇena samuddo ekaraso loṇaraso”ti? “Cirasaṇṭhitattā kho, mahārāja, udakassa samuddo ekaraso loṇaraso”ti.
499“Kallosi, bhante nāgasenā”ti.
500Samuddaekarasapañho terasamo.
