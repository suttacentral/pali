33.
1687Gantho dhammo noganthassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
1688Nogantho dhammo noganthassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
1689Nogantho dhammo ganthassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Purejātaṃ—  vatthu ganthānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
1690Nogantho dhammo ganthassa ca noganthassa ca dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Purejātaṃ—  vatthu ganthānaṃ sampayuttakānañca khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (3)
1691Gantho ca nogantho ca dhammā noganthassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
