1.7.10 Lokasutta
Devatāsaṃyutta
Addhavagga
Lokasutta
70.
329
“Kismiṃ loko samuppanno,
kismiṃ kubbati santhavaṃ;
Kissa loko upādāya,
kismiṃ loko vihaññatī”ti.
330
“Chasu loko samuppanno,
chasu kubbati santhavaṃ;
Channameva upādāya,
chasu loko vihaññatī”ti.
