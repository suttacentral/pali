12.3.3 Sammāsambuddhasutta
Saccasaṃyutta
Koṭigāmavagga
Sammāsambuddhasutta
1093.
2278Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, ariyasaccāni. Katamāni cattāri? Dukkhaṃ ariyasaccaṃ…pe…  dukkhanirodhagāminī paṭipadā ariyasaccaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri ariyasaccāni. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, catunnaṃ ariyasaccānaṃ yathābhūtaṃ abhisambuddhattā tathāgato ‘arahaṃ sammāsambuddho’ti vuccati.
2279Tasmātiha, bhikkhave, ‘idaṃ dukkhan’ti yogo karaṇīyo…pe…  ‘ayaṃ dukkhanirodhagāminī paṭipadā’ti yogo karaṇīyo”ti.
2280Tatiyaṃ.
