2.4.3.7 Paṭibāhanasikkhāpada
Bhikkhunivibhaṅga
Pācittiyakaṇḍa
Naggavagga
Paṭibāhanasikkhāpada
911.
2842Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena bhikkhunisaṃghassa akālacīvaraṃ uppannaṃ hoti. Atha kho bhikkhunisaṃgho taṃ cīvaraṃ bhājetukāmo sannipati. Tena kho pana samayena thullanandāya bhikkhuniyā antevāsiniyo bhikkhuniyo pakkantā honti. Thullanandā bhikkhunī tā bhikkhuniyo etadavoca—  “ayye, bhikkhuniyo pakkantā, na tāva cīvaraṃ bhājīyissatī”ti. Cīvaravibhaṅgaṃ paṭibāhi. Bhikkhuniyo na tāva cīvaraṃ bhājīyissatīti pakkamiṃsu. Thullanandā bhikkhunī antevāsinīsu bhikkhunīsu āgatāsu taṃ cīvaraṃ bhājāpesi.
2843Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā…pe…  tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma ayyā thullanandā dhammikaṃ cīvaravibhaṅgaṃ paṭibāhissatī”ti…pe…  “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī dhammikaṃ cīvaravibhaṅgaṃ paṭibāhatī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā…pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī dhammikaṃ cīvaravibhaṅgaṃ paṭibāhissati. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya…pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu— 
912.
2844“Yā pana bhikkhunī dhammikaṃ cīvaravibhaṅgaṃ paṭibāheyya, pācittiyan”ti. (27:82)
913.
2845Yā panāti yā yādisā…pe…  bhikkhunīti…pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.
2846Dhammiko nāma cīvaravibhaṅgo samaggo bhikkhunisaṃgho sannipatitvā bhājeti.
2847Paṭibāheyyāti kathaṃ imaṃ cīvaraṃ na bhājeyyāti paṭibāhati, āpatti pācittiyassa.
914.
2848Dhammike dhammikasaññā paṭibāhati, āpatti pācittiyassa. Dhammike vematikā paṭibāhati, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Dhammike adhammikasaññā paṭibāhati, anāpatti. Adhammike dhammikasaññā, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Adhammike vematikā, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Adhammike adhammikasaññā, anāpatti.
915.
2849Anāpatti—  ānisaṃsaṃ dassetvā paṭibāhati, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.
2850Sattamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
