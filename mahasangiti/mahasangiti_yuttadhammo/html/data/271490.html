2.4.2.10.7 Cittasahabhūduka
Niddesa
Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa
Dukaatthuddhāra
Mahantaraduka
Cittasahabhūduka
1537.
2248Katame dhammā cittasahabhuno? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho, kāyaviññatti, vacīviññatti—  ime dhammā cittasahabhuno.
1538.
2249Katame dhammā no cittasahabhuno? Cittañca, avasesañca rūpaṃ, nibbānañca—  ime dhammā no cittasahabhuno.
