16 Kusalattika, Saraṇaduka
Kusalattika, Saraṇaduka
36.
81Nakusalaṃ nasaraṇaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā akusalo saraṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
82Nakusalaṃ naaraṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato araṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naabyākataṃ naaraṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato araṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nakusalaṃ naaraṇañca naabyākataṃ naaraṇañca dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato araṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
