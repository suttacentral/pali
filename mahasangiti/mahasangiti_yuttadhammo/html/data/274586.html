1.13.6 Khilasutta
Maggasaṃyutta
Esanāvagga
Khilasutta
166.
320“Tayome, bhikkhave, khilā. Katame tayo? Rāgo khilo, doso khilo, moho khilo—  ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo khilā. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, tiṇṇannaṃ khilānaṃ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya…pe…  ayaṃ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo”ti.
321Chaṭṭhaṃ.
