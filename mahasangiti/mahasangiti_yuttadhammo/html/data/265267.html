2749Cittasampayutto ca cittavippayutto ca dhammā cittavippayuttassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. Pacchājātā—  cittasampayuttakā khandhā ca kabaḷīkāro āhāro ca imassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. Pacchājātā—  cittasampayuttakā khandhā ca rūpajīvitindriyañca kaṭattārūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. (2)
