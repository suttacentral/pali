1.14.10 Uddhambhāgiyasutta
Maggasaṃyutta
Oghavagga
Uddhambhāgiyasutta
181.
353“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, uddhambhāgiyāni saṃyojanāni. Katamāni pañca? Rūparāgo, arūparāgo, māno, uddhaccaṃ, avijjā—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcuddhambhāgiyāni saṃyojanāni. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṃ uddhambhāgiyānaṃ saṃyojanānaṃ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo. Katamo ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu sammādiṭṭhiṃ bhāveti vivekanissitaṃ…pe…  sammāsamādhiṃ bhāveti vivekanissitaṃ virāganissitaṃ nirodhanissitaṃ vossaggapariṇāmiṃ. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṃ uddhambhāgiyānaṃ saṃyojanānaṃ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya ayaṃ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabboti.
354Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, uddhambhāgiyāni saṃyojanāni. Katamāni pañca? Rūparāgo, arūparāgo, māno, uddhaccaṃ, avijjā—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcuddhambhāgiyāni saṃyojanāni. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṃ uddhambhāgiyānaṃ saṃyojanānaṃ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo. Katamo ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu sammādiṭṭhiṃ bhāveti…pe…  sammāsamādhiṃ bhāveti rāgavinayapariyosānaṃ dosavinayapariyosānaṃ mohavinayapariyosānaṃ…  amatogadhaṃ amataparāyanaṃ amatapariyosānaṃ…  nibbānaninnaṃ nibbānapoṇaṃ nibbānapabbhāraṃ. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṃ uddhambhāgiyānaṃ saṃyojanānaṃ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya ayaṃ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo”ti.
355Dasamaṃ.
356Oghavaggo aṭṭhamo.
357Tassuddānaṃ
358
Ogho yogo upādānaṃ,
ganthaṃ anusayena ca;
Kāmaguṇā nīvaraṇaṃ,
khandhā oruddhambhāgiyāti.
359Vagguddānaṃ
360
Avijjāvaggo paṭhamo,
dutiyaṃ vihāraṃ vuccati;
Micchattaṃ tatiyo vaggo,
catutthaṃ paṭipanneneva.
361
Titthiyaṃ pañcamo vaggo,
chaṭṭho sūriyena ca;
Bahukate sattamo vaggo,
uppādo aṭṭhamena ca.
362
Divasavaggo navamo,
Dasamo appamādena ca;
Ekādasabalavaggo,
Dvādasa esanā pāḷiyaṃ;
Oghavaggo bhavati terasāti.
