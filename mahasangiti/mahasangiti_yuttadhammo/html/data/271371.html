2.3.2.3.5 Āsavaāsavasampayuttaduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Dukanikkhepa
Āsavagocchaka
Āsavaāsavasampayuttaduka
1114.
1805Katame dhammā āsavā ceva āsavasampayuttā ca? Kāmāsavo avijjāsavena āsavo ceva āsavasampayutto ca, avijjāsavo kāmāsavena āsavo ceva āsavasampayutto ca, bhavāsavo avijjāsavena āsavo ceva āsavasampayutto ca, avijjāsavo bhavāsavena āsavo ceva āsavasampayutto ca, diṭṭhāsavo avijjāsavena āsavo ceva āsavasampayutto ca, avijjāsavo diṭṭhāsavena āsavo ceva āsavasampayutto ca—  ime dhammā āsavā ceva āsavasampayuttā ca.
1115.
1806Katame dhammā āsavasampayuttā ceva no ca āsavā? Tehi dhammehi ye dhammā sampayuttā, te dhamme ṭhapetvā vedanākkhandho…pe…  viññāṇakkhandho—  ime dhammā āsavasampayuttā ceva no ca āsavā.
