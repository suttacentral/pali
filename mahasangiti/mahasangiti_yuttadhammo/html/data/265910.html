433.
941Hetu micchattaniyato dhammo hetussa micchattaniyatassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
942Nahetu micchattaniyato dhammo nahetussa micchattaniyatassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātādhipati…  tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
434.
943Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi, sahajāte nava, aññamaññe nava, nissaye nava, upanissaye nava, kamme tīṇi, āhāre tīṇi, indriye tīṇi, jhāne tīṇi, magge tīṇi, sampayutte nava, atthiyā nava, avigate nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
