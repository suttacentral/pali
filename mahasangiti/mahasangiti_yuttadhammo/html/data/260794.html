Hetuduka
31.
1748Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe pañca, naadhipatiyā satta, naanantare nasamanantare naaññamaññe naupanissaye pañca, napurejāte satta, napacchājāte sattarasa, naāsevane sattarasa, nakamme cattāri, navipāke nava, nasampayutte pañca, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā pañca, novigate pañca. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
1749Anulomapaccanīyaṃ.
