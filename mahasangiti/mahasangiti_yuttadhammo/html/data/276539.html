1.7.1.8 Dutiyaokkhittacakkhusikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Parimaṇḍalavagga
Dutiyaokkhittacakkhusikkhāpada
583.
1720Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū tahaṃ tahaṃ olokentā antaraghare nisīdanti…pe… .
1721“Okkhittacakkhu antaraghare nisīdissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (8:153)
1722Okkhittacakkhunā antaraghare nisīditabbaṃ yugamattaṃ pekkhantena. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca tahaṃ tahaṃ olokento antaraghare nisīdati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1723Anāpatti—  asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.
1724Aṭṭhamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
