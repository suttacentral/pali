4.7.8 Dutiyarukkhasutta
Indriyasaṃyutta
Bodhipakkhiyavagga
Dutiyarukkhasutta
538.
1244“Seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, ye keci devānaṃ tāvatiṃsānaṃ rukkhā, pārichattako tesaṃ aggamakkhāyati; evameva kho, bhikkhave, ye keci bodhipakkhiyā dhammā, paññindriyaṃ tesaṃ aggamakkhāyati, yadidaṃ—  bodhāya. Katame ca, bhikkhave, bodhipakkhiyā dhammā? Saddhindriyaṃ, bhikkhave, bodhipakkhiyo dhammo, taṃ bodhāya saṃvattati. Vīriyindriyaṃ…pe…  satindriyaṃ…  samādhindriyaṃ…  paññindriyaṃ bodhipakkhiyo dhammo, taṃ bodhāya saṃvattati. Seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, ye keci devānaṃ tāvatiṃsānaṃ rukkhā, pārichattako tesaṃ aggamakkhāyati; evameva kho, bhikkhave, ye keci bodhipakkhiyā dhammā, paññindriyaṃ tesaṃ aggamakkhāyati, yadidaṃ—  bodhāyā”ti.
1245Aṭṭhamaṃ.
