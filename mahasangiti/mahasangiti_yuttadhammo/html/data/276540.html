1.7.1.9 Ukkhittakasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Parimaṇḍalavagga
Ukkhittakasikkhāpada
584.
1725Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū ukkhittakāya antaraghare gacchanti…pe… .
1726“Na ukkhittakāya antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (9:154)
1727Na ukkhittakāya antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca ekato vā ubhato vā ukkhipitvā antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1728Anāpatti—  asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.
1729Navamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
