2.4.9.5 Gandhavaṇṇakasikkhāpada
Bhikkhunivibhaṅga
Pācittiyakaṇḍa
Chattupāhanavagga
Gandhavaṇṇakasikkhāpada
1198.
3469Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyanti. Manussā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyissanti, seyyathāpi gihiniyo kāmabhoginiyo”ti.
3470Assosuṃ kho bhikkhuniyo tesaṃ manussānaṃ ujjhāyantānaṃ khiyyantānaṃ vipācentānaṃ. Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā…pe…  tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyissantī”ti…pe…  “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyantī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā…pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyissanti. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya…pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu— 
1199.
3471“Yā pana bhikkhunī gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyeyya, pācittiyan”ti. (88:143)
1200.
3472Yā panāti yā yādisā…pe…  bhikkhunīti…pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.
3473Gandho nāma yo koci gandho. Vaṇṇakaṃ nāma yaṃ kiñci vaṇṇakaṃ. Nahāyeyyāti nahāyati. Payoge dukkaṭaṃ, nahānapariyosāne āpatti pācittiyassa.
1201.
3474Anāpatti—  ābādhappaccayā, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.
3475Pañcamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
