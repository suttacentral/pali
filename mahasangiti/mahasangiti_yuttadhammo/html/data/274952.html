11.5.6 Suddhakasutta
Sotāpattisaṃyutta
Sagāthakapuññābhisandavagga
Suddhakasutta
1042.
2074“Catūhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato ariyasāvako sotāpanno hoti avinipātadhammo niyato sambodhiparāyaṇo.
2075Katamehi catūhi? Idha, bhikkhave, ariyasāvako buddhe aveccappasādena samannāgato hoti—  itipi so bhagavā…pe…  satthā devamanussānaṃ buddho bhagavāti. Dhamme…pe…  saṃghe…pe…  ariyakantehi sīlehi samannāgato hoti akhaṇḍehi…pe…  samādhisaṃvattanikehi. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, catūhi dhammehi samannāgato ariyasāvako sotāpanno hoti avinipātadhammo niyato sambodhiparāyaṇo”ti.
2076Chaṭṭhaṃ.
