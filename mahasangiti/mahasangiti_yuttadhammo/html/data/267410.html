28.6 Potthakadāyakattheraapadāna
Suvaṇṇabibbohanavagga
Potthakadāyakattheraapadāna
24.
3256
“Satthāraṃ dhammamārabbha,
saṃghañcāpi mahesinaṃ;
Potthadānaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
dakkhiṇeyye anuttare.
25.
3257
Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
potthadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.
26.
3258
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
3259Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā potthakadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
3260Potthakadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
