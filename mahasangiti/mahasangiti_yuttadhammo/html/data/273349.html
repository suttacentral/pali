1.6.1 Adhammakammadvādasaka
Kammakkhandhaka
Āpattiyāappaṭikammeukkhepanīyakamma
Adhammakammadvādasaka
57.
267Tīhi, bhikkhave, aṅgehi samannāgataṃ, āpattiyā appaṭikamme, ukkhepanīyakammaṃ adhammakammañca hoti, avinayakammañca, duvūpasantañca. Asammukhā kataṃ hoti, appaṭipucchā kataṃ hoti, appaṭiññāya kataṃ hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṃ, āpattiyā appaṭikamme, ukkhepanīyakammaṃ adhammakammañca hoti, avinayakammañca, duvūpasantañca.
268Aparehipi, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṃ, āpattiyā appaṭikamme, ukkhepanīyakammaṃ adhammakammañca hoti, avinayakammañca, duvūpasantañca. Anāpattiyā kataṃ hoti, adesanāgāminiyā āpattiyā kataṃ hoti, desitāya āpattiyā kataṃ hoti…pe…  acodetvā kataṃ hoti, asāretvā kataṃ hoti, āpattiṃ anāropetvā kataṃ hoti…pe…  asammukhā kataṃ hoti, adhammena kataṃ hoti, vaggena kataṃ hoti…pe…  appaṭipucchā kataṃ hoti, adhammena kataṃ hoti, vaggena kataṃ hoti…pe…  appaṭiññāya kataṃ hoti, adhammena kataṃ hoti, vaggena kataṃ hoti…pe…  anāpattiyā kataṃ hoti, adhammena kataṃ hoti, vaggena kataṃ hoti…pe…  adesanāgāminiyā āpattiyā kataṃ hoti, adhammena kataṃ hoti, vaggena kataṃ hoti…pe…  desitāya āpattiyā kataṃ hoti, adhammena kataṃ hoti, vaggena kataṃ hoti…pe…  acodetvā kataṃ hoti, adhammena kataṃ hoti, vaggena kataṃ hoti…pe…  asāretvā kataṃ hoti, adhammena kataṃ hoti, vaggena kataṃ hoti…pe…  āpattiṃ anāropetvā kataṃ hoti, adhammena kataṃ hoti, vaggena kataṃ hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṃ, āpattiyā appaṭikamme, ukkhepanīyakammaṃ adhammakammañca hoti, avinayakammañca, duvūpasantañca.
269Āpattiyā appaṭikamme ukkhepanīyakamme
adhammakammadvādasakaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
