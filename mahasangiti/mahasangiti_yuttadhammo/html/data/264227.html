1735Saññojaniyo abyākato dhammo saññojaniyassa abyākatassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
