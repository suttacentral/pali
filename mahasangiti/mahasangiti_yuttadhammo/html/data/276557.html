1.7.3.6 Pallatthikasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Khambhakatavagga
Pallatthikasikkhāpada
601.
1810Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū pallatthikāya antaraghare nisīdanti…pe… .
1811“Na pallatthikāya antaraghare nisīdissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (26:171)
1812Na pallatthikāya antaraghare nisīditabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca hatthapallatthikāya vā dussapallatthikāya vā antaraghare nisīdati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1813Anāpatti— asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, vāsūpagatassa, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.
1814Chaṭṭhasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
