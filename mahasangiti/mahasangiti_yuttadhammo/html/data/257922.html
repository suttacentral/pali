35 Hetuduka, Ajjhattattika
Hetuduka, Ajjhattattika
82.
199Nahetu naajjhatto dhammo hetussa ajjhattassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā upanissaye nava, purejāte atthiyā avigate tīṇi.
200Nahetu nabahiddhā dhammo hetussa bahiddhā dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā upanissaye nava, purejāte atthiyā avigate tīṇi.
201(Ajjhattattiko na labbhati paṭiccavārādīsu.)
