Paccanīyuddhāra
45.
2411Sārammaṇo dhammo sārammaṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo. (1)
2412Sārammaṇo dhammo anārammaṇassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo. (2)
2413Sārammaṇo dhammo sārammaṇassa ca anārammaṇassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo. (3)
46.
2414Anārammaṇo dhammo anārammaṇassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)
2415Anārammaṇo dhammo sārammaṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo. (2)
2416Sārammaṇo ca anārammaṇo ca dhammā sārammaṇassa dhammassa sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. (1)
2417Sārammaṇo ca anārammaṇo ca dhammā anārammaṇassa dhammassa sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. (2)
