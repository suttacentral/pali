Paccanīyuddhāra
69.
1845Sekkho dhammo sekkhassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)
1846Sekkho dhammo asekkhassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (2)
1847Sekkho dhammo nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo. (3)
1848Sekkho dhammo sekkhassa ca nevasekkhanāsekkhassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (4)
70.
1849Asekkho dhammo asekkhassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)
1850Asekkho dhammo nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo. (2)
1851Asekkho dhammo asekkhassa ca nevasekkhanāsekkhassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (3)
71.
1852Nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)
1853Nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo sekkhassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo. (2)
1854Nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo asekkhassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo. (3)
72.
1855Sekkho ca nevasekkhanāsekkho ca dhammā sekkhassa dhammassa sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. (1)
1856Sekkho ca nevasekkhanāsekkho ca dhammā nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. (2)
1857Asekkho ca nevasekkhanāsekkho ca dhammā asekkhassa dhammassa sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. (1)
1858Asekkho ca nevasekkhanāsekkho ca dhammā nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. (2)
