1.3.4 Aṭṭhipuñjasutta
Ekakanipāta
Tatiyavagga
Aṭṭhipuñjasutta
24.
134Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ— 
135“Ekapuggalassa, bhikkhave, kappaṃ sandhāvato saṃsarato siyā evaṃ mahā aṭṭhikaṅkalo aṭṭhipuñjo aṭṭhirāsi yathāyaṃ vepullo pabbato, sace saṃhārako assa, sambhatañca na vinasseyyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati— 
136
“Ekassekena kappena,
puggalassaṭṭhisañcayo;
Siyā pabbatasamo rāsi,
iti vuttaṃ mahesinā.
137
So kho panāyaṃ akkhāto,
vepullo pabbato mahā;
Uttaro gijjhakūṭassa,
magadhānaṃ giribbaje.
138
Yato ca ariyasaccāni,
sammappaññāya passati;
Dukkhaṃ dukkhasamuppādaṃ,
dukkhassa ca atikkamaṃ;
Ariyañcaṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ,
dukkhūpasamagāminaṃ.
139
Sa sattakkhattuṃ paramaṃ,
sandhāvitvāna puggalo;
Dukkhassantakaro hoti,
sabbasaṃyojanakkhayā”ti.
140Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
141Catutthaṃ.
