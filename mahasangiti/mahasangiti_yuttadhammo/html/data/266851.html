5.10 Cūḷapanthakasutta
Soṇavagga
Cūḷapanthakasutta
50.
449Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena āyasmā cūḷapanthako bhagavato avidūre nisinno hoti pallaṅkaṃ ābhujitvā ujuṃ kāyaṃ paṇidhāya parimukhaṃ satiṃ upaṭṭhapetvā.
450Addasā kho bhagavā āyasmantaṃ cūḷapanthakaṃ avidūre nisinnaṃ pallaṅkaṃ ābhujitvā ujuṃ kāyaṃ paṇidhāya parimukhaṃ satiṃ upaṭṭhapetvā.
451Atha kho bhagavā etamatthaṃ viditvā tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imaṃ udānaṃ udānesi— 
452
“Ṭhitena kāyena ṭhitena cetasā,
Tiṭṭhaṃ nisinno uda vā sayāno;
Etaṃ satiṃ bhikkhu adhiṭṭhahāno,
Labhetha pubbāpariyaṃ visesaṃ;
Laddhāna pubbāpariyaṃ visesaṃ,
Adassanaṃ maccurājassa gacche”ti. (61)
453Dasamaṃ.
454Soṇavaggo pañcamo.
455Tassuddānaṃ
456
Piyo appāyukā kuṭṭhī,
kumārakā uposatho;
Soṇo ca revato bhedo,
sadhāya panthakena cāti.
