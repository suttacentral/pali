353Nasaraṇaṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā…  ekaṃ.
354Nasaraṇaṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā…  tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Nahetuyā ekaṃ, naārammaṇe tīṇi, naadhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  nakamme ekaṃ, naāhāre naindriye najhāne namagge ekaṃ…pe…  novigate tīṇi.
355Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
356Naārammaṇapaccayā hetuyā tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
