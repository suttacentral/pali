6 Kusalattika, Pītittika
Kusalattika, Pītittika
18.
44Kusalaṃ pītisahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo napītisahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  pañca. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā terasa, ārammaṇe nava…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate terasa.
