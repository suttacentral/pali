6.4.2 Kusalamūlasutta
Lābhasakkārasaṃyutta
Catutthavagga
Kusalamūlasutta
181.
1086Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. “Dāruṇo, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko. Lābhasakkārasilokena abhibhūtassa pariyādiṇṇacittassa, bhikkhave, devadattassa kusalamūlaṃ samucchedamagamā. Evaṃ dāruṇo kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko…pe…  sikkhitabban”ti.
1087Dutiyaṃ.
