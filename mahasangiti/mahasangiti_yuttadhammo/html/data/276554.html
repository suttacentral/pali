1.7.3.3 Oguṇṭhitasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Khambhakatavagga
Oguṇṭhitasikkhāpada
598.
1795Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū sasīsaṃ pārupitvā antaraghare gacchanti…pe… .
1796“Na oguṇṭhito antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (23:168)
1797Na oguṇṭhitena antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca sasīsaṃ pārupitvā antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1798Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.
1799Tatiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
