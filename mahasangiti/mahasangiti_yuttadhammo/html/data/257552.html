Saraṇaduka, Āsavagocchaka
47.
158Nasaraṇaṃ noāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate pañca.
159Nasaraṇaṃ nanoāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ. Sabbattha ekaṃ. Vipākaṃ natthi.)
160Nasaraṇaṃ nasāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ. Sabbattha ekaṃ.)
161Nasaraṇaṃ naanāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate pañca.
162Nasaraṇaṃ naāsavasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
163Nasaraṇaṃ naāsavavippayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ. Sabbattha ekaṃ. Vipākaṃ natthi.)
164Nasaraṇaṃ naāsavañceva naanāsavañca dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate pañca.
165Nasaraṇaṃ naanāsavañceva nano ca āsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ. Sabbattha ekaṃ. Vipākaṃ natthi.)
166Nasaraṇaṃ naāsavañceva naāsavavippayuttañca dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  nasaraṇaṃ naāsavavippayuttañceva nano ca āsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ. Sabbattha ekaṃ. Vipākaṃ natthi.)
167Nasaraṇaṃ naāsavavippayuttaṃ nasāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ. Sabbattha ekaṃ.)
168Nasaraṇaṃ naāsavavippayuttaṃ naanāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
