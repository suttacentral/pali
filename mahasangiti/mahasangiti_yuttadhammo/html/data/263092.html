6.
1884Parittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca paritto dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  parittaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ, dve khandhe…pe…  ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ paṭicca tayo mahābhūtā…pe…  mahābhūte paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ upādārūpaṃ. (1)
1885Mahaggataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca mahaggato dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  mahaggataṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)
1886Mahaggataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca paritto dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  mahaggate khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. (2)
1887Mahaggataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca paritto ca mahaggato ca dhammā uppajjanti adhipatipaccayā—  mahaggataṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (3)
1888Appamāṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca appamāṇo dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  appamāṇaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)
1889Appamāṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca paritto dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  appamāṇe khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. (2)
1890Appamāṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca paritto ca appamāṇo ca dhammā uppajjanti adhipatipaccayā—  appamāṇaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (3)
7.
1891Parittañca appamāṇañca dhammaṃ paṭicca paritto dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  appamāṇe khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. (1)
1892Parittañca mahaggatañca dhammaṃ paṭicca paritto dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  mahaggate khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. (1)
