4.6.10 Dutiyauppādasutta
Indriyasaṃyutta
Sūkarakhatavagga
Dutiyauppādasutta
530.
1224“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni bhāvitāni bahulīkatāni anuppannāni uppajjanti, nāññatra sugatavinayā. Katamāni pañca? Saddhindriyaṃ, vīriyindriyaṃ, satindriyaṃ, samādhindriyaṃ, paññindriyaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyāni bhāvitāni bahulīkatāni anuppannāni uppajjanti, nāññatra sugatavinayā”ti.
1225Dasamaṃ.
1226Sūkarakhatavaggo chaṭṭho.
1227Tassuddānaṃ
1228
Sālaṃ mallikaṃ sekho ca,
padaṃ sāraṃ patiṭṭhitaṃ;
Brahmasūkarakhatāyo,
uppādā apare duveti.
