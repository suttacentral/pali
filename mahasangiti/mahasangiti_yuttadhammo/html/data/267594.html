46.10 Gandhapūjakattheraapadāna
Jagatidāyakavagga
Gandhapūjakattheraapadāna
63.
5959
“Citāsu kurumānāsu,
nānāgandhe samāhaṭe;
Pasannacitto sumano,
gandhamuṭṭhimapūjayiṃ.
64.
5960
Satasahassito kappe,
citakaṃ yamapūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
citapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
65.
5961
Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
…pe… 
viharāmi anāsavo.
66.
5962
Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.
67.
5963
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (4771)
5964Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā gandhapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
5965Gandhapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.
5966Jagatidāyakavaggo chacattālīsamo.
5967Tassuddānaṃ
5968
Jagatī morahatthī ca,
āsanī ukkadhārako;
Akkami vanakoraṇḍi,
chattado jātipūjako.
5969
Paṭṭipupphī ca yo thero,
dasamo gandhapūjako;
Sattasaṭṭhi ca gāthāyo,
gaṇitāyo vibhāvibhi.
