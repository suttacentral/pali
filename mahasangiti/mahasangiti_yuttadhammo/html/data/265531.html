47.
3936Saṃkiliṭṭhaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe paccayā vicikicchāsahagato uddhaccasahagato moho. (1)
3937Asaṃkiliṭṭhaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā asaṃkiliṭṭho dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  ahetukaṃ asaṃkiliṭṭhaṃ…pe…  (yāva asaññasattā) cakkhāyatanaṃ paccayā cakkhuviññāṇaṃ…pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ paccayā kāyaviññāṇaṃ, vatthuṃ paccayā ahetukā asaṃkiliṭṭhā khandhā. (1)
3938Asaṃkiliṭṭhaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vatthuṃ paccayā vicikicchāsahagato uddhaccasahagato moho. (2)
3939Saṃkiliṭṭhañca asaṃkiliṭṭhañca dhammaṃ paccayā saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe ca vatthuñca paccayā vicikicchāsahagato uddhaccasahagato moho. (1)
