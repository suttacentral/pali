2.6 Mettikātherīgāthā
Dukanipāta
Mettikātherīgāthā
29.
54
“Kiñcāpi khomhi dukkhitā,
dubbalā gatayobbanā;
Daṇḍamolubbha gacchāmi,
pabbataṃ abhirūhiya.
30.
55
Nikkhipitvāna saṅghāṭiṃ,
pattakañca nikujjiya;
Nisinnā camhi selamhi,
atha cittaṃ vimucci me;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanan”ti.
