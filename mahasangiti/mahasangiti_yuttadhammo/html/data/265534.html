52.
3955Saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo saṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo—  rāgaṃ assādeti abhinandati, taṃ ārabbha rāgo uppajjati, diṭṭhi uppajjati; vicikicchā…pe…  uddhaccaṃ…pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati; diṭṭhiṃ assādeti…pe…  (kusalattikasadisaṃ); vicikicchaṃ ārabbha…pe…  uddhaccaṃ ārabbha…pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati; diṭṭhi…pe…  vicikicchā…pe…  uddhaccaṃ uppajjati. (1)
3956Saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo asaṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo—  ariyā pahīne kilese paccavekkhanti, vikkhambhite kilese…pe…  pubbe samudāciṇṇe…pe…  saṃkiliṭṭhe khandhe aniccato…pe…  vipassati, cetopariyañāṇena saṃkiliṭṭhacittasamaṅgissa cittaṃ jānāti; saṃkiliṭṭhā khandhā cetopariyañāṇassa, pubbenivāsānussatiñāṇassa, yathākammūpagañāṇassa, anāgataṃsañāṇassa, āvajjanāya ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (2)
53.
