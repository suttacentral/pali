53.7 Girinelapūjakattheraapadāna
Tiṇadāyakavagga
Girinelapūjakattheraapadāna
42.
6851
“Migaluddo pure āsiṃ,
vipine vicaraṃ ahaṃ;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
sabbadhammāna pāraguṃ.
43.
6852
Tasmiṃ mahākāruṇike,
sabbasattahite rate;
Pasannacitto sumano,
nelapupphamapūjayiṃ.
44.
6853
Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
45.
6854
Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
…pe… 
viharāmi anāsavo.
46.
6855
Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.
47.
6856
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
6857Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā girinelapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
6858Girinelapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
