53.
1110Āsavasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavasampayutto dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā…  tīṇi.
1111Āsavavippayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavavippayutto dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  āsavavippayuttaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  domanassasahagataṃ mohaṃ paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ, ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ…pe…  mahābhūte paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ upādārūpaṃ. (1)
1112Āsavavippayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavasampayutto dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  domanassasahagataṃ mohaṃ paṭicca sampayuttakā khandhā. (2)
1113Āsavavippayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavasampayutto ca āsavavippayutto ca dhammā uppajjanti adhipatipaccayā—  domanassasahagataṃ mohaṃ paṭicca sampayuttakā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ. (3)
54.
1114Āsavasampayuttañca āsavavippayuttañca dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavasampayutto dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  domanassasahagataṃ ekaṃ khandhañca mohañca paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)
1115Āsavasampayuttañca āsavavippayuttañca dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavavippayutto dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  āsavasampayutte khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ, domanassasahagate khandhe ca mohañca paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. (2)
1116Āsavasampayuttañca āsavavippayuttañca dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavasampayutto ca āsavavippayutto ca dhammā uppajjanti adhipatipaccayā—  domanassasahagataṃ ekaṃ khandhañca mohañca paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (3)
1117(Evaṃ sabbe paccayā vitthāretabbā. Saṃkhittaṃ.)
