8.9 Ṭhitañjaliyattheraapadāna
Nāgasamālavagga
Ṭhitañjaliyattheraapadāna
42.
1646
“Migaluddo pure āsiṃ,
Araññe kānane ahaṃ;
Tattha addasaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Bāttiṃsavaralakkhaṇaṃ.
43.
1647
Tatthāhaṃ añjaliṃ katvā,
pakkāmiṃ pācināmukho;
Avidūre nisinnassa,
niyake paṇṇasanthare.
44.
1648
Tato me asanīpāto,
matthake nipatī tadā;
Sohaṃ maraṇakālamhi,
akāsiṃ punarañjaliṃ.
45.
1649
Dvenavute ito kappe,
añjaliṃ akariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
añjalissa idaṃ phalaṃ.
46.
1650
Catupaṇṇāsakappamhi,
migaketusanāmako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
47.
1651
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
1652Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ṭhitañjaliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
1653Ṭhitañjaliyattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
