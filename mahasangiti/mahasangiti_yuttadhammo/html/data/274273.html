11.1.1 Suddhikasutta
Valāhakasaṃyutta
Valāhakavagga
Suddhikasutta
550.
1216Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Valāhakakāyike vo, bhikkhave, deve desessāmi. Taṃ suṇātha. Katame ca, bhikkhave, valāhakakāyikā devā? Santi, bhikkhave, sītavalāhakā devā; santi uṇhavalāhakā devā; santi abbhavalāhakā devā; santi vātavalāhakā devā; santi vassavalāhakā devā—  ime vuccanti, bhikkhave, ‘valāhakakāyikā devā’”ti.
1217Paṭhamaṃ.
