101.
277Saraṇaṃ ajjhattārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naajjhattārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Araṇaṃ ajjhattārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naajjhattārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā dve, aññamaññe ekaṃ…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate dve.
