7.11 Karaṇīyadoḷasaka
Kathinakkhandhaka
Karaṇīyadoḷasaka
320.
1495Bhikkhu atthatakathino kenacideva karaṇīyena pakkamati. Tassa bahisīmagatassa cīvarāsā uppajjati. So taṃ cīvarāsaṃ payirupāsati. Anāsāya labhati, āsāya na labhati. Tassa evaṃ hoti—  “idhevimaṃ cīvaraṃ kāressaṃ, na paccessan”ti. So taṃ cīvaraṃ kāreti. Tassa bhikkhuno niṭṭhānantiko kathinuddhāro. (1)
