1338Hetusampayutto akusalo dhammo hetusampayuttassa akusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

1339Nahetuyā nava, naārammaṇe nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
1340Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe dve. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
1341Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
