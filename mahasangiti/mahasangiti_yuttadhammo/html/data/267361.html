23.7 Sovaṇṇavaṭaṃsakiyattheraapadāna
Ālambaṇadāyakavagga
Sovaṇṇavaṭaṃsakiyattheraapadāna
34.
2944
“Uyyānabhūmiṃ niyyanto,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Vaṭaṃsakaṃ gahetvāna,
sovaṇṇaṃ sādhunimmitaṃ.
35.
2945
Sīghaṃ tato samāruyha,
hatthikkhandhagato ahaṃ;
Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,
sikhino lokabandhuno.
36.
2946
Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pupphapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
37.
2947
Sattavīse ito kappe,
eko āsiṃ janādhipo;
Mahāpatāpanāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
38.
2948
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
2949Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sovaṇṇavaṭaṃsakiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2950Sovaṇṇavaṭaṃsakiyattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
