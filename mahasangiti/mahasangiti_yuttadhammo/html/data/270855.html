1.3.2.2 Cūḷantaraduka
Khandhavibhaṅga
Pañhāpucchaka
Duka
Cūḷantaraduka
523Sappaccayā, saṅkhatā.
524Cattāro khandhā anidassanā. Rūpakkhandho siyā sanidassano, siyā anidassano. Cattāro khandhā appaṭighā. Rūpakkhandho siyā sappaṭigho, siyā appaṭigho. Rūpakkhandho rūpaṃ. Cattāro khandhā arūpā. Rūpakkhandho lokiyo. Cattāro khandhā siyā lokiyā, siyā lokuttarā; kenaci viññeyyā, kenaci na viññeyyā.
