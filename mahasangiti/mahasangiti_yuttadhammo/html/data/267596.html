Sālakusumiyavagga
Citakapūjakattheraapadāna
6.
5977
“Jhāyamānassa bhagavato,
sikhino lokabandhuno;
Aṭṭha campakapupphāni,
citakaṃ abhiropayiṃ.
7.
5978
Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
citapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
8.
5979
Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
…pe… 
viharāmi anāsavo.
9.
5980
Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.
10.
5981
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
5982Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā citakapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
5983Citakapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
