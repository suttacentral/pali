11 Kusalattika, Parittattika
Kusalattika, Parittattika
15.
54Nakusalaṃ naparittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato paritto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  aññamaññe tīṇi…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate pañca.
55Nakusalaṃ namahaggataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato mahaggato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
56Nakusalaṃ naappamāṇaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo appamāṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha…pe…  āsevane tīṇi…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
