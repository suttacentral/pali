1929Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū jivhānicchārakaṃ bhuñjanti…pe… .
1930“Na jivhānicchārakaṃ bhuñjissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (49:194)
1931Na jivhānicchārakaṃ bhuñjitabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca jivhānicchārakaṃ bhuñjati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1932Anāpatti— asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.
