8.2 Padasaññakattheraapadāna
Nāgasamālavagga
Padasaññakattheraapadāna
5.
1595
“Akkantañca padaṃ disvā,
tissassādiccabandhuno;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
pade cittaṃ pasādayiṃ.
6.
1596
Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
padasaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ.
7.
1597
Ito sattamake kappe,
sumedho nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
8.
1598
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
1599Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā padasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
1600Padasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
