8.2.14 Āsavakkhayasutta
Anuruddhasaṃyutta
Dutiyavagga
Āsavakkhayasutta
922.
1619“Imesañca panāhaṃ, āvuso, catunnaṃ satipaṭṭhānānaṃ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā āsavānaṃ khayā anāsavaṃ cetovimuttiṃ paññāvimuttiṃ diṭṭheva dhamme sayaṃ abhiññā sacchikatvā upasampajja viharāmī”ti.
1620Cuddasamaṃ.
1621Dutiyo vaggo.
1622Tassuddānaṃ
1623
Mahābhiññaṃ iddhi dibbaṃ,
Cetopariyaṃ ṭhānaṃ kammaṃ;
Sabbatthadhātudhimutti,
Indriyaṃ jhānaṃ tisso vijjāti.
1624Anuruddhasaṃyuttaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
