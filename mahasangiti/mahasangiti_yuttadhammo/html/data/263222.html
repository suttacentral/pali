45.
2561Maggārammaṇo dhammo maggārammaṇassa dhammassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā maggārammaṇā khandhā pacchimānaṃ pacchimānaṃ maggārammaṇānaṃ khandhānaṃ āsevanapaccayena paccayo.
2562Maggārammaṇo dhammo maggādhipatissa dhammassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo. (Anantarasadisaṃ. Nava pañhā kātabbā, āvajjanā na kātabbā.)
