17 Saraṇaduka, Hetusahetukadukādi
Saraṇaduka, Hetusahetukadukādi
65.
144Nasaraṇaṃ nahetuñceva naahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo hetu ceva sahetuko ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naaraṇaṃ nahetuñceva naahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca saraṇo hetu ceva sahetuko ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate dve.
145Nasaraṇaṃ naahetukañceva nanahetuñca dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo sahetuko ceva na ca hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naaraṇaṃ naahetukañceva nanahetuñca dhammaṃ paṭicca saraṇo sahetuko ceva na ca hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate dve.
146(Hetuhetusampayuttadukaṃ saṃkhittaṃ.)
