1.1.1.2.1 Anulomapaccanīka
Mahāpaṇṇāsaka
Paṭhamavagga
Puggalakathā
Okāsasaccikaṭṭha
Anulomapaccanīka
11.
40๐ Puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenāti? Āmantā. Sabbattha puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenāti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe.
41Ājānāhi niggahaṃ. Hañci puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthena, tena vata re vattabbe—  “sabbattha puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā”ti. Yaṃ tattha vadesi—  “vattabbe kho—  ‘puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthena’, no ca vattabbe—  ‘sabbattha puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā’”ti micchā.
42No ce pana vattabbe—  “sabbattha puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā”ti, no ca vata re vattabbe—  “puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā”ti. Yaṃ tattha vadesi—  “vattabbe kho—  ‘puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthena’, no ca vattabbe—  ‘sabbattha puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā’”ti micchā…pe… .
43Tatiyo niggaho.
