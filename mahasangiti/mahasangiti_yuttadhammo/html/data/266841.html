4.10 Sāriputtaupasamasutta
Meghiyavagga
Sāriputtaupasamasutta
40.
343Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena āyasmā sāriputto bhagavato avidūre nisinno hoti pallaṅkaṃ ābhujitvā ujuṃ kāyaṃ paṇidhāya attano upasamaṃ paccavekkhamāno.
344Addasā kho bhagavā āyasmantaṃ sāriputtaṃ avidūre nisinnaṃ pallaṅkaṃ ābhujitvā ujuṃ kāyaṃ paṇidhāya attano upasamaṃ paccavekkhamānaṃ.
345Atha kho bhagavā etamatthaṃ viditvā tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imaṃ udānaṃ udānesi— 
346
“Upasantasantacittassa,
netticchinnassa bhikkhuno;
Vikkhīṇo jātisaṃsāro,
mutto so mārabandhanā”ti. (50)
347Dasamaṃ.
348Meghiyavaggo catuttho.
349Tassuddānaṃ
350
Meghiyo uddhatā gopālo,
yakkho nāgena pañcamaṃ;
Piṇḍolo sāriputto ca,
sundarī bhavati aṭṭhamaṃ;
Upaseno vaṅgantaputto,
sāriputto ca te dasāti.
