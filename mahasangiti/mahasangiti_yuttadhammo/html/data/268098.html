2.1.1 Khemasutta
Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka
Khemavagga
Khemasutta
52.
406“‘Khemaṃ, kheman’ti, āvuso, vuccati. Kittāvatā nu kho, āvuso, khemaṃ vuttaṃ bhagavatā”ti?
407“Idhāvuso, bhikkhu vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati. Ettāvatāpi kho, āvuso, khemaṃ vuttaṃ bhagavatā pariyāyena…pe… .
408Puna caparaṃ, āvuso, bhikkhu sabbaso nevasaññānāsaññāyatanaṃ samatikkamma saññāvedayitanirodhaṃ upasampajja viharati, paññāya cassa disvā āsavā parikkhīṇā honti. Ettāvatāpi kho, āvuso, khemaṃ vuttaṃ bhagavatā nippariyāyenā”ti.
409Paṭhamaṃ.
