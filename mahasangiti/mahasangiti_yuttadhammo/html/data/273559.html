9.4.5 Dhammānupassanāsaccapabba
Mahāsatipaṭṭhānasutta
Dhammānupassanā
Dhammānupassanāsaccapabba
386.
1034Puna caparaṃ, bhikkhave, bhikkhu dhammesu dhammānupassī viharati catūsu ariyasaccesu. Kathañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhu dhammesu dhammānupassī viharati catūsu ariyasaccesu? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu ‘idaṃ dukkhan’ti yathābhūtaṃ pajānāti, ‘ayaṃ dukkhasamudayo’ti yathābhūtaṃ pajānāti, ‘ayaṃ dukkhanirodho’ti yathābhūtaṃ pajānāti, ‘ayaṃ dukkhanirodhagāminī paṭipadā’ti yathābhūtaṃ pajānāti.
Mahāsatipaṭṭhānasutta
Dhammānupassanā
Dhammānupassanāsaccapabba
1035Paṭhamabhāṇavāro niṭṭhito.
