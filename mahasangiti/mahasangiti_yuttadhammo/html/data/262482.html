462.
1481Indriya sahajāta nissaya magga atthi avigatanti nahetuyā satta, naārammaṇe satta…pe…  naaññamaññe tīṇi, naupanissaye satta…pe…  nasampayutte tīṇi, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā satta, novigate satta.
1482Indriya sahajāta aññamañña nissaya magga atthi avigatanti nahetuyā tīṇi…pe…  nasampayutte ekaṃ, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā tīṇi, novigate tīṇi.
1483Indriya sahajāta aññamañña nissaya magga sampayutta atthi avigatanti nahetuyā tīṇi…pe…  novigate tīṇi.
1484Indriya sahajāta nissaya magga vippayutta atthi avigatanti nahetuyā tīṇi…pe…  novigate tīṇi. [Avipāka—  4]
