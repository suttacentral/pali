22 Upādinnattika, Kusalattika
Upādinnattika, Kusalattika
31.
100Naanupādinnupādāniyaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā anupādinnupādāniyo kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha…pe…  avigate cha.
101Naanupādinnupādāniyaṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā anupādinnupādāniyo akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
102Naupādinnupādāniyaṃ naabyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anupādinnupādāniyo abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  avigate pañca.
