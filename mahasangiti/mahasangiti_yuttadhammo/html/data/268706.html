Dukanipāta
Dutiyavagga
Somamittattheragāthā
147.
315
“Parittaṃ dārumāruyha,
yathā sīde mahaṇṇave;
Evaṃ kusītamāgamma,
sādhujīvīpi sīdati;
Tasmā taṃ parivajjeyya,
kusītaṃ hīnavīriyaṃ.
148.
316
Pavivittehi ariyehi,
pahitattehi jhāyibhi;
Niccaṃ āraddhavīriyehi,
paṇḍitehi sahāvase”ti.
