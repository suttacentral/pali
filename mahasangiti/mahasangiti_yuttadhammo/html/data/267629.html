50.5 Upaḍḍhadussadāyakattheraapadāna
Kiṅkaṇipupphavagga
Upaḍḍhadussadāyakattheraapadāna
31.
6468
“Padumuttarabhagavato,
sujāto nāma sāvako;
Paṃsukūlaṃ gavesanto,
saṅkāre carate tadā.
32.
6469
Nagare haṃsavatiyā,
paresaṃ bhatako ahaṃ;
Upaḍḍhadussaṃ datvāna,
sirasā abhivādayiṃ.
33.
6470
Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.
34.
6471
Tettiṃsakkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjamakārayiṃ;
Sattasattatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ.
35.
6472
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ;
Upaḍḍhadussadānena,
modāmi akutobhayo.
36.
6473
Icchamāno cahaṃ ajja,
sakānanaṃ sapabbataṃ;
Khomadussehi chādeyyaṃ,
aḍḍhadussassidaṃ phalaṃ.
37.
6474
Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
aḍḍhadussassidaṃ phalaṃ.
38.
6475
Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
…pe… 
viharāmi anāsavo.
39.
6476
Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.
40.
6477
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
6478Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā upaḍḍhadussadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
6479Upaḍḍhadussadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
