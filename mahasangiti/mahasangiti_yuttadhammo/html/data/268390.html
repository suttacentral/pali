1.2.5 Taṇhāsaṃyojanasutta
Ekakanipāta
Dutiyavagga
Taṇhāsaṃyojanasutta
15.
77Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ— 
78“Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekasaṃyojanampi samanupassāmi yena saṃyojanena saṃyuttā sattā dīgharattaṃ sandhāvanti saṃsaranti yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, taṇhāsaṃyojanaṃ. Taṇhāsaṃyojanena hi, bhikkhave, saṃyuttā sattā dīgharattaṃ sandhāvanti saṃsarantī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati— 
79
“Taṇhādutiyo puriso,
dīghamaddhāna saṃsaraṃ;
Itthabhāvaññathābhāvaṃ,
saṃsāraṃ nātivattati.
80
Etamādīnavaṃ ñatvā,
Taṇhaṃ dukkhassa sambhavaṃ;
Vītataṇho anādāno,
Sato bhikkhu paribbaje”ti.
81Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
82Pañcamaṃ.
