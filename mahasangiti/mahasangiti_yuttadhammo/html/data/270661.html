710Asubhajhānaṃ soḷasakkhattukaṃ

263.
711Katame dhammā kusalā? Yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati uddhumātakasaññāsahagataṃ, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti…pe…  avikkhepo hoti…pe…  ime dhammā kusalā.
264.
712Katame dhammā kusalā? Yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati vinīlakasaññāsahagataṃ…pe…  vipubbakasaññāsahagataṃ…pe…  vicchiddakasaññāsahagataṃ…pe…  vikkhāyitakasaññāsahagataṃ…pe…  vikkhittakasaññāsahagataṃ…pe…  hatavikkhittakasaññāsahagataṃ…pe…  lohitakasaññāsahagataṃ…pe…  puḷavakasaññāsahagataṃ…pe…  aṭṭhikasaññāsahagataṃ, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti…pe…  avikkhepo hoti…pe…  ime dhammā kusalā.
713Asubhajhānaṃ soḷasakkhattukaṃ.
