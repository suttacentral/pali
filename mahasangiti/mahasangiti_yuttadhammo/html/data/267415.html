29.1 Paṇṇadāyakattheraapadāna
Paṇṇadāyakavagga
Paṇṇadāyakattheraapadāna
1.
3287
“Paṇṇasāle nisinnomhi,
paṇṇabhojanabhojano;
Upaviṭṭhañca maṃ santaṃ,
upāgacchi mahāisi.
2.
3288
Siddhattho lokapajjoto,
sabbalokatikicchako;
Tassa paṇṇaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
nisinnaṃ paṇṇasanthare.
3.
3289
Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ paṇṇamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
paṇṇadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.
4.
3290
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
3291Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā paṇṇadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
3292Paṇṇadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
