6.2.2 Rūpiyapātisutta
Lābhasakkārasaṃyutta
Dutiyavagga
Rūpiyapātisutta
168.
1053Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. “Dāruṇo, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko…pe…  idhāhaṃ, bhikkhave, ekaccaṃ puggalaṃ evaṃ cetasā ceto paricca pajānāmi—  ‘na cāyamāyasmā rūpiyapātiyāpi suvaṇṇacuṇṇaparipūrāya hetu sampajānamusā bhāseyyā’ti. Tamenaṃ passāmi aparena samayena lābhasakkārasilokena abhibhūtaṃ pariyādiṇṇacittaṃ sampajānamusā bhāsantaṃ. Evaṃ dāruṇo kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko…pe…  evañhi vo, bhikkhave, sikkhitabban”ti.
1054Dutiyaṃ.
