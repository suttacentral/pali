1803Ganthasampayutto dhammo ganthavippayuttassa dhammassa pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  ekaṃ.
1804Ganthavippayutto dhammo ganthavippayuttassa dhammassa pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  ekaṃ.
1805Ganthasampayutto ca ganthavippayutto ca dhammā ganthavippayuttassa dhammassa pacchājātapaccayena paccayo—  pacchājātā diṭṭhigatavippayuttalobhasahagatā khandhā ca lobho ca domanassasahagatā khandhā ca paṭighañca purejātassa imassa kāyassa pacchājātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
