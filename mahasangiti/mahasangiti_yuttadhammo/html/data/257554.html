18 Saraṇaduka, Mahantaraduka
Saraṇaduka, Mahantaraduka
50.
173Nasaraṇaṃ nasārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.
174Nasaraṇaṃ nacittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate pañca.
175Nasaraṇaṃ nacetasikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
176Nasaraṇaṃ nacittasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  nasaraṇaṃ nacittasaṃsaṭṭhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.
177Nasaraṇaṃ nacittasamuṭṭhānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.
178Nasaraṇaṃ nacittasahabhuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  nasaraṇaṃ nacittānuparivattiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
51.
179Nasaraṇaṃ nacittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  nasaraṇaṃ nacittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  nasaraṇaṃ nacittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe ekaṃ…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

182Nasaraṇaṃ naupādā dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cattāri, ārammaṇe dve…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate cattāri.
