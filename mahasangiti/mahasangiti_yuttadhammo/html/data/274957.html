11.6.1 Sagāthakasutta
Sotāpattisaṃyutta
Sappaññavagga
Sagāthakasutta
1047.
2091“Catūhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato ariyasāvako sotāpanno hoti avinipātadhammo niyato sambodhiparāyaṇo.
2092Katamehi catūhi? Idha, bhikkhave, ariyasāvako buddhe aveccappasādena samannāgato hoti—  itipi so bhagavā…pe…  satthā devamanussānaṃ buddho bhagavāti. Dhamme…pe…  saṃghe…pe…  ariyakantehi sīlehi samannāgato hoti akhaṇḍehi…pe…  samādhisaṃvattanikehi. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, catūhi dhammehi samannāgato ariyasāvako sotāpanno hoti avinipātadhammo niyato sambodhiparāyaṇo”ti. Idamavoca bhagavā. Idaṃ vatvāna sugato athāparaṃ etadavoca satthā— 
2093
“Yassa saddhā tathāgate,
acalā suppatiṭṭhitā;
Sīlañca yassa kalyāṇaṃ,
ariyakantaṃ pasaṃsitaṃ.
2094
Saṃghe pasādo yassatthi,
ujubhūtañca dassanaṃ;
Adaliddoti taṃ āhu,
amoghaṃ tassa jīvitaṃ.
2095
Tasmā saddhañca sīlañca,
pasādaṃ dhammadassanaṃ;
Anuyuñjetha medhāvī,
saraṃ buddhānasāsanan”ti.
2096Paṭhamaṃ.
