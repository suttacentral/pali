1.1.5 Mahāgovindacariya
Akittivagga
Dānapāramī
Mahāgovindacariya
37.
42
“Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
sattarājapurohito;
Pūjito naradevehi,
mahāgovindabrāhmaṇo.
38.
43
Tadāhaṃ sattarajjesu,
Yaṃ me āsi upāyanaṃ;
Tena demi mahādānaṃ,
Akkhobbhaṃ sāgarūpamaṃ.
39.
44
Na me dessaṃ dhanaṃ dhaññaṃ,
Napi natthi nicayo mayi;
Sabbaññutaṃ piyaṃ mayhaṃ,
Tasmā demi varaṃ dhanan”ti.
45Mahāgovindacariyaṃ pañcamaṃ.
