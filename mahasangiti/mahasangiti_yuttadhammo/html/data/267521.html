39.7 Kadaliphaladāyakattheraapadāna
Avaṭaphalavagga
Kadaliphaladāyakattheraapadāna
37.
4225
“Kaṇikāraṃva jalitaṃ,
puṇṇamāyeva candimaṃ;
Jalantaṃ dīparukkhaṃva,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ.
38.
4226
Kadaliphalaṃ paggayha,
adāsiṃ satthuno ahaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
vanditvāna apakkamiṃ.
39.
4227
Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ phalamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.
40.
4228
Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
…pe… 
viharāmi anāsavo.
41.
4229
Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.
42.
4230
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
4231Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kadaliphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
4232Kadaliphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
