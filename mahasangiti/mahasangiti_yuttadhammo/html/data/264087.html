403.
871Hetu appamāṇārammaṇo dhammo hetussa appamāṇārammaṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
404.

873Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
874Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
