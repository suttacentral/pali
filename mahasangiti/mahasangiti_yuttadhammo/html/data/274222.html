5.1.4 Samphassasutta
Uppādasaṃyutta
Uppādavagga
Samphassasutta
315.
1074Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Yo kho, bhikkhave, cakkhusamphassassa uppādo ṭhiti…pe…  jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo…pe…  yo manosamphassassa uppādo ṭhiti…pe…  jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo. Yo ca kho, bhikkhave, cakkhusamphassassa nirodho…pe…  jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo…pe…  yo manosamphassassa nirodho…pe…  jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo”ti.
1075Catutthaṃ.
