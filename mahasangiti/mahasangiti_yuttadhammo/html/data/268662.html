1.9.10 Sāmidattattheragāthā
Ekakanipāta
Navamavagga
Sāmidattattheragāthā
90.
201
“Pañcakkhandhā pariññātā,
tiṭṭhanti chinnamūlakā;
Vikkhīṇo jātisaṃsāro,
natthi dāni punabbhavo”ti.
202…  Sāmidatto thero… .
Vaggo navamo.
203Tassuddānaṃ
204
Thero samitigutto ca,
kassapo sīhasavhayo;
Nīto sunāgo nāgito,
paviṭṭho ajjuno isi;
Devasabho ca yo thero,
sāmidatto mahabbaloti.
