13.1.35--40 Kallitamūlakaārammaṇasuttādichakka
Jhānasaṃyutta
Jhānavagga
Kallitamūlakaārammaṇasuttādichakka
696--701.
1326Sāvatthinidānaṃ…  “samādhismiṃ kallitakusalo hoti, na samādhismiṃ ārammaṇakusalo…  samādhismiṃ ārammaṇakusalo hoti, na samādhismiṃ kallitakusalo…  neva samādhismiṃ kallitakusalo hoti, na ca samādhismiṃ ārammaṇakusalo…  samādhismiṃ kallitakusalo ca hoti, samādhismiṃ ārammaṇakusalo ca. Tatra, bhikkhave, yvāyaṃ jhāyī…pe…  uttamo ca pavaro cā”ti.
1327Pañcatiṃsatimaṃ.
1328(Purimamūlakāni viya yāva cattālīsamā kallitamūlakasappāyakārīsuttā cha suttāni pūretabbāni. Kallitamūlakaṃ.)
