1538Katamaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ upādā upādinnaṃ? Cakkhāyatanaṃ…pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ, itthindriyaṃ purisindriyaṃ jīvitindriyaṃ, yaṃ vā panaññampi atthi rūpaṃ kammassa katattā rūpāyatanaṃ gandhāyatanaṃ rasāyatanaṃ ākāsadhātu rūpassa upacayo rūpassa santati kabaḷīkāro āhāro—  idaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ upādā upādinnaṃ.
