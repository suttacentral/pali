1.7.2.9 Sīsappacālakasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Ujjagghikavagga
Sīsappacālakasikkhāpada
594.
1775Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū sīsappacālakaṃ antaraghare gacchanti sīsaṃ olambentā…pe… .
1776“Na sīsappacālakaṃ antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (19:164)
1777Na sīsappacālakaṃ antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Sīsaṃ paggahetvā gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca sīsappacālakaṃ antaraghare gacchati sīsaṃ olambento, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1778Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.
1779Navamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
