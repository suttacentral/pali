Kilesaduka
68.
210Nokilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nokilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nokilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nokileso ca nanokileso ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)
211Nanokilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
212Nokilesañca nanokilesañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
213Hetuyā nava…pe…  vipāke ekaṃ…pe…  avigate nava. (Sabbattha nava.)
