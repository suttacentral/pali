14 Kusalattika, Micchattaniyatattika
Kusalattika, Micchattaniyatattika
18.
63Nakusalaṃ namicchattaniyataṃ dhammaṃ paccayā akusalo micchattaniyato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
64Nakusalaṃ nasammattaniyataṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo sammattaniyato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
65Nakusalaṃ naaniyataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato aniyato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  avigate pañca.
