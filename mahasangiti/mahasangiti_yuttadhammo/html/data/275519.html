3.5.10 Pubbaṇhasutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Maṅgalavagga
Pubbaṇhasutta
156.
1089“Ye, bhikkhave, sattā pubbaṇhasamayaṃ kāyena sucaritaṃ caranti, vācāya sucaritaṃ caranti, manasā sucaritaṃ caranti, supubbaṇho, bhikkhave, tesaṃ sattānaṃ.
1090Ye, bhikkhave, sattā majjhanhikasamayaṃ kāyena sucaritaṃ caranti, vācāya sucaritaṃ caranti, manasā sucaritaṃ caranti, sumajjhanhiko, bhikkhave, tesaṃ sattānaṃ.
1091Ye, bhikkhave, sattā sāyanhasamayaṃ kāyena sucaritaṃ caranti, vācāya sucaritaṃ caranti, manasā sucaritaṃ caranti, susāyanho, bhikkhave, tesaṃ sattānanti.
1092
Sunakkhattaṃ sumaṅgalaṃ,
suppabhātaṃ suhuṭṭhitaṃ;
Sukhaṇo sumuhutto ca,
suyiṭṭhaṃ brahmacārisu.
1093
Padakkhiṇaṃ kāyakammaṃ,
vācākammaṃ padakkhiṇaṃ;
Padakkhiṇaṃ manokammaṃ,
paṇīdhi te padakkhiṇe;
Padakkhiṇāni katvāna,
labhantatthe padakkhiṇe.
1094
Te atthaladdhā sukhitā,
viruḷhā buddhasāsane;
Arogā sukhitā hotha,
saha sabbehi ñātibhī”ti.
1095Dasamaṃ.
1096Maṅgalavaggo pañcamo.
1097Tassuddānaṃ
1098
Akusalañca sāvajjaṃ,
visamāsucinā saha;
Caturo khatā vandanā,
pubbaṇhena ca te dasāti.
1099Tatiyo paṇṇāsako samatto.
