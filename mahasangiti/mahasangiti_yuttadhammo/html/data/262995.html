1432Ācayagāmiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ācayagāmī dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā—  ācayagāmiṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)
1433Apacayagāmiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca apacayagāmī dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā—  apacayagāmiṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)
1434Nevācayagāmināpacayagāmiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nevācayagāmināpacayagāmī dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā—  nevācayagāmināpacayagāmiṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe paṭicca dve khandhā. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  vatthuṃ paṭicca khandhā. (1)
