12.
2965Anidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati aññamaññapaccayā—  anidassanasappaṭighaṃ ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ paṭicca dve mahābhūtā, dve mahābhūte paṭicca ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ. (1)
2966Anidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati aññamaññapaccayā—  anidassanasappaṭighe mahābhūte paṭicca āpodhātu. (2)
2967Anidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanaappaṭigho ca dhammā uppajjanti aññamaññapaccayā—  anidassanasappaṭighaṃ ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ paṭicca dve mahābhūtā āpodhātu ca, dve mahābhūte paṭicca ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ āpodhātu ca. Bāhiraṃ…pe… . (3)
13.
2968Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati aññamaññapaccayā—  anidassanaappaṭighaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe anidassanaappaṭighaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā vatthu ca…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  khandhe paṭicca vatthu, vatthuṃ paṭicca khandhā. (1)
2969Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati aññamaññapaccayā—  āpodhātuṃ paṭicca anidassanasappaṭighā mahābhūtā. Bāhiraṃ…pe… . (2)
14.
2970Anidassanasappaṭighañca anidassanaappaṭighañca dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati aññamaññapaccayā—  anidassanasappaṭighaṃ ekaṃ mahābhūtañca āpodhātuñca paṭicca dve mahābhūtā, dve mahābhūte ca āpodhātuñca paṭicca ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ. Bāhiraṃ…pe… . (1)
