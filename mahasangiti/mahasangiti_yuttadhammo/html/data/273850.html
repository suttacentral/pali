10.1.2 Sakkanāmasutta
Yakkhasaṃyutta
Indakavagga
Sakkanāmasutta
236.
1455Ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā rājagahe viharati gijjhakūṭe pabbate. Atha kho sakkanāmako yakkho yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi— 
1456
“Sabbaganthappahīnassa,
vippamuttassa te sato;
Samaṇassa na taṃ sādhu,
yadaññamanusāsasī”ti.
1457
“Yena kenaci vaṇṇena,
saṃvāso sakka jāyati;
Na taṃ arahati sappañño,
manasā anukampituṃ.
1458
Manasā ce pasannena,
yadaññamanusāsati;
Na tena hoti saṃyutto,
yānukampā anuddayā”ti.
