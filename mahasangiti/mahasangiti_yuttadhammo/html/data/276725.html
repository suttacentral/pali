2.4.9.6 Vāsitakasikkhāpada
Bhikkhunivibhaṅga
Pācittiyakaṇḍa
Chattupāhanavagga
Vāsitakasikkhāpada
1202.
3476Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo vāsitakena piññākena nahāyanti. Manussā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo vāsitakena piññākena nahāyissanti, seyyathāpi gihiniyo kāmabhoginiyo”ti.
3477Assosuṃ kho bhikkhuniyo tesaṃ manussānaṃ ujjhāyantānaṃ khiyyantānaṃ vipācentānaṃ. Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā…pe…  tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo vāsitakena piññākena nahāyissantī”ti…pe…  “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo vāsitakena piññākena nahāyantī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā…pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo vāsitakena piññākena nahāyissanti. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya…pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu— 
1203.
3478“Yā pana bhikkhunī vāsitakena piññākena nahāyeyya, pācittiyan”ti. (89:144)
1204.
3479Yā panāti yā yādisā…pe…  bhikkhunīti…pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.
3480Vāsitakaṃ nāma yaṃ kiñci gandhavāsitakaṃ. Piññākaṃ nāma tilapiṭṭhaṃ vuccati. Nahāyeyyāti nahāyati. Payoge dukkaṭaṃ, nahānapariyosāne āpatti pācittiyassa.
1205.
3481Anāpatti—  ābādhappaccayā, pakatipiññākena nahāyati, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.
3482Chaṭṭhasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
